Stage 7-1-2 · What Is Noise?關卡 7-1-2 · 雜訊是什麼？SASP §7.1.2 ↗

A noise signal is a realisation of a stochastic process: an infinite family of random variables, one per sample index. Statistics such as mean $\mu$, variance $\sigma^2$, autocorrelation $r[k]$ describe the process; any single observed sequence only estimates them.

雜訊是某隨機過程的一個實現：每個取樣索引對應一個隨機變數。平均 $\mu$、變異數 $\sigma^2$、自相關 $r[k]$ 描述此過程；觀測到的一段序列只是估計它們。
