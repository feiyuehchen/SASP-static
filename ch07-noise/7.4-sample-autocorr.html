$$\hat r_x[k]\;=\;\frac{1}{N-k}\sum_{n=0}^{N-k-1}x[n]\,x[n+k]$$

Unbiased estimator — divides by $N-k$ (the actual overlap). Downside: variance grows at long lags, so the plot gets noisier toward the edges.

無偏估計器 — 除以實際重疊 $N-k$。缺點：長滯後的變異數變大，圖往兩端越抖。
