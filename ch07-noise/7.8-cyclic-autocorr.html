Stage 7-8 · Cyclic Autocorrelation關卡 7-8 · 循環自相關SASP §7.8 ↗

Computed by IFFT of $|X|^2$ (without zero-padding). Equals the cyclic autocorrelation $\tilde r^{\text{circ}}_x[k]=\tfrac{1}{N}\sum_n x[n]x[(n+k)\bmod N]$. Equivalent to assuming the signal repeats periodically — another source of bias.

用 $|X|^2$ 的 IFFT 計算（不補零）。得到循環自相關 $\tilde r^{\text{circ}}_x[k]=\tfrac{1}{N}\sum_n x[n]x[(n+k)\bmod N]$。等同於假設訊號週期性重複 — 另一個偏差來源。

Zero-pad to at least $2N$ before FFT to recover the linear autocorrelation.

FFT 前補零至少到 $2N$ 才能還原線性自相關。
