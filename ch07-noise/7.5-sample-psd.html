Stage 7-5 · Sample Power Spectral Density關卡 7-5 · 樣本功率頻譜密度SASP §7.5 ↗

$$\hat P_x(\omega)\;=\;\frac{1}{N}\bigl|\hat X(\omega)\bigr|^{2}$$

Known as the periodogram. Unbiased in expectation as $N\to\infty$ but does not become less noisy: variance stays equal to the true PSD regardless of $N$. Averaging multiple periodograms (Welch) is how we reduce noise.

即週期圖。當 $N\to\infty$ 時期望值無偏，但不變平滑：變異數與真值相等，不因 $N$ 變大而減。要降變異得平均多個（Welch）。
