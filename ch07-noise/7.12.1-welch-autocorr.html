Stage 7-12-1 · Welch Autocorrelation Estimate關卡 7-12-1 · Welch 自相關估計SASP §7.12.1 ↗

Inverse-transforming a Welch PSD gives an averaged autocorrelation — same structure, different domain. Useful when lag-domain features (e.g. pitch peaks) matter more than frequency-domain shape.

對 Welch PSD 作反 FFT 得到平均化自相關 — 不同域、同結構。當 lag 域特徵（如基頻峰）比頻域形狀更重要時好用。
