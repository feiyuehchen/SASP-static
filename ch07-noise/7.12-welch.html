Stage 7-12 · Welch's Method關卡 7-12 · Welch 法SASP §7.12 ↗

Split the signal into $K$ overlapping segments of length $L$, apply a window, periodogram each, average. Variance drops by a factor of the effective number of independent averages.

把訊號切成 $K$ 段重疊長度 $L$ 的片段，加窗、分別週期圖、平均。變異數隨等效獨立平均數下降。

$$\hat P_x^{\text{Welch}}(\omega)=\frac{1}{K}\sum_{m=0}^{K-1}\hat P_x^{m}(\omega)$$
