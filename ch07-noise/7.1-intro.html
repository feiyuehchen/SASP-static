Stage 7-1 · Introduction to Noise關卡 7-1 · 雜訊導論SASP §7.1 ↗

"Noise" in signal processing means a signal modelled as random — a realisation drawn from some probability distribution, not just any unexplained content.

訊號處理中的「雜訊」指被建模為隨機的訊號 — 從某機率分布中抽出的一個實現，不是任何解不開的內容。

This chapter focuses on stationary noise, where the statistics (mean, variance, autocorrelation) do not vary with time. Most audio noise — tape hiss, fan rumble, quantisation noise — is at least approximately stationary over short windows.

本章聚焦於穩態雜訊：其統計（平均值、變異數、自相關）不隨時間變。大部分音訊雜訊（磁帶嘶聲、風扇低頻、量化雜訊）在短視窗內至少近似穩態。
