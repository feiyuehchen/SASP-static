Stage 7-12-2 · Resolution vs Stability關卡 7-12-2 · 解析度 vs 穩定性SASP §7.12.2 ↗

Longer segments → finer resolution, but fewer of them → higher variance. Shorter segments → more averages → smoother PSD, but wider main lobe. Pick based on whether your features are narrow tones or broad noise shapes.

片段越長 → 解析度越細，但段數變少 → 變異變大。片段越短 → 平均數多 → PSD 越平滑，但主瓣變寬。依需求是「窄音」或「寬雜訊形狀」取捨。
