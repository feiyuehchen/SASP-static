Stage 7-11 · The Periodogram關卡 7-11 · 週期圖SASP §7.11 ↗

$$\hat P_x^{\text{peri}}(\omega_k)\;=\;\frac{1}{N}\,|X[k]|^{2}$$

Properties: asymptotically unbiased, but inconsistent — its variance does not go to zero as $N\to\infty$. Any single periodogram is a noisy estimate of the true PSD; averaging many is required.

性質：漸近無偏，但不一致 — 變異數不隨 $N\to\infty$ 下降。單一週期圖是真 PSD 的雜訊估計，要平均多次。
