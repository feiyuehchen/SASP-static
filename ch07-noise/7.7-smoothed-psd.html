Stage 7-7 · Smoothed PSD關卡 7-7 · 平滑 PSDSASP §7.7 ↗

1) Frequency-domain: convolve the periodogram with a smoothing kernel (Blackman-Tukey approach). 2) Time-domain (segment averaging): compute many short periodograms and average — that is Welch. Either way, variance drops but main-lobe width (resolution) grows.

兩條路：1) 頻域：週期圖與平滑核卷積（Blackman-Tukey）。2) 時域（分段平均）：多段短週期圖相加 — 就是 Welch。兩者都降低變異但加寬主瓣（降解析度）。
