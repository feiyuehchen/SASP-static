Stage 7-1-1 · Why Analyze Noise?關卡 7-1-1 · 為何分析雜訊？SASP §7.1.1 ↗

  • Measure SNR of a recording or a microphone.
  • 量測錄音或麥克風的 SNR。
  • Design a noise-reduction filter — you need to know the noise spectrum to suppress it.
  • 設計降噪濾波器 — 必須先知道雜訊頻譜才能壓抑它。
  • Characterise room acoustics via reverberant tails (noise-like).
  • 以殘響尾（類雜訊）刻畫房間聲學。
  • Generate controlled test signals (white, pink, brown) for evaluating systems.
  • 生成受控測試訊號（白、粉、布朗）以評估系統。
