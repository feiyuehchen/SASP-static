Stage 7-14-2 · Pink (1/f) Noise Synthesis關卡 7-14-2 · 粉紅 (1/f) 雜訊合成SASP §7.14.2 ↗

$P_y(\omega)\propto 1/|\omega|$ — equal power per octave. Approximated well by Voss-McCartney algorithm (summing multiple decimated white noise streams) or by FIR/IIR filters matching the $1/f$ envelope to a few dB over the audio band.

$P_y(\omega)\propto 1/|\omega|$ — 每個八度帶內功率相等。Voss-McCartney 演算法（多個降頻白雜訊相加）或 $1/f$ 形狀的 FIR/IIR 濾波器都能在音訊頻段內逼近到數 dB。
