Stage 7-3-1 · Testing for White Noise關卡 7-3-1 · 白性檢驗SASP §7.3.1 ↗

1) Plot the sample autocorrelation — all non-zero lags should be within a noise floor of $\pm 2/\sqrt{N}$ (95% CI). 2) Plot the periodogram — it should be flat up to statistical variation.

1) 畫樣本自相關 — 非零滯後值應落在 $\pm 2/\sqrt{N}$ 內（95% 信賴）。2) 畫週期圖 — 應平直（允許統計變異）。

Formal tests: Ljung-Box, Portmanteau, Kolmogorov-Smirnov on the periodogram vs uniform. For audio work the eye-balling of these two plots is usually enough.

正式檢定：Ljung-Box、Portmanteau、對週期圖做 KS 檢驗。音訊實務上肉眼看這兩張圖通常足夠。
