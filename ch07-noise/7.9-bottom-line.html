1) Window the signal (Hann is the default). 2) Split into overlapping segments. 3) Compute each periodogram. 4) Average. 5) Present as dB vs frequency. That is Welch's method — your default noise analyser.

1) 加 Hann 視窗。2) 切重疊片段。3) 每段取週期圖。4) 平均。5) 畫成 dB vs 頻率。這就是 Welch 法 — 預設的雜訊分析器。
