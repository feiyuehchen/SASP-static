Stage 7-14-1 · Example: FIR-Filtered White Noise關卡 7-14-1 · 範例：FIR 濾波白雜訊SASP §7.14.1 ↗

A single-pole lowpass (first-order IIR) turns white noise into red/brown noise. Even a short FIR moving average does the job for a rougher shape.

一階 IIR 單極低通就能把白雜訊變成紅/布朗雜訊；短 FIR 移動平均也可做出類似效果。
