Stage 7-10 · Why an Impulse Is Not White Noise關卡 7-10 · 為何脈衝不是白雜訊SASP §7.10 ↗

A unit impulse has a flat deterministic spectrum. White noise has a flat expected spectrum. The impulse has energy in one instant and zero everywhere else — not stationary, not random, and its phase is well-defined. The two are often confused because both sound/look flat after a single DFT.

脈衝的頻譜是平直的確定性頻譜；白雜訊的是期望平直頻譜。脈衝能量集中在一瞬、其他地方為零 — 不穩態、不隨機，相位也有定義。兩者常被混淆因為一次 DFT 都平。
