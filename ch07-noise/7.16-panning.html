Stage 7-16 · The Panning Problem關卡 7-16 · 聲像問題SASP §7.16 ↗

Stereo panning multiplies an incoherent (noise-like) source by $(\cos\theta, \sin\theta)$ in the two channels. Because the two signals are uncorrelated copies, total power = $\cos^2\theta + \sin^2\theta = 1$ — constant across the pan angle. For coherent (sinusoidal) sources the two channels add in phase at the listener and the pan law changes.

立體聲聲像把非相干（類雜訊）源在兩聲道乘上 $(\cos\theta,\sin\theta)$。因兩聲道不相干相加，總功率 $\cos^2\theta+\sin^2\theta=1$ — 聲像過程固定。對相干（正弦）源，兩聲道在聽者處相干疊加，聲像法則不同。
