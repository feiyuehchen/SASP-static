Stage 7-3 · White Noise關卡 7-3 · 白雜訊SASP §7.3 ↗

White noise has $r_x[k]=\sigma^2\delta[k]$ and therefore $P_x(\omega)=\sigma^2$ — constant. Samples are uncorrelated with each other (not necessarily independent, but uncorrelated is what matters for second-order statistics).

白雜訊 $r_x[k]=\sigma^2\delta[k]$、$P_x(\omega)=\sigma^2$ — 平直。各樣本不相關（未必獨立，但對二階統計來說不相關就夠了）。
