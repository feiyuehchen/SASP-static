Stage 7-6 · Biased Sample Autocorrelation關卡 7-6 · 偏差樣本自相關SASP §7.6 ↗

$$\tilde r_x[k]\;=\;\frac{1}{N}\sum_{n=0}^{N-k-1}x[n]\,x[n+k]$$

Divides by $N$ instead of $N-k$. Biased (shrinks long lags toward 0) but has lower variance and corresponds exactly to $\text{IDFT}\{\tfrac{1}{N}|X|^2\}$ — which links directly to the periodogram.

除以 $N$ 而非 $N-k$。有偏（把長滯後縮向 0）但變異較小；且剛好等於 $\text{IDFT}\{\tfrac{1}{N}|X|^2\}$ — 與週期圖直接對應。
