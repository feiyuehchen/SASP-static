Stage 7-14-3 · Pink Noise Analysis關卡 7-14-3 · 粉紅雜訊分析SASP §7.14.3 ↗

On a dB-vs-log-frequency plot, pink noise appears as a straight line of slope $-3$ dB/octave (or $-10$ dB/decade). Confirm by fitting a line to your Welch estimate in log-log space.

dB vs 對數頻率圖上，粉紅雜訊是斜率 $-3$ dB/octave（$-10$ dB/decade）的直線。在 log-log 空間對 Welch 估計擬合直線即可驗證。
