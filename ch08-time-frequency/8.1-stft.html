Stage 8-1 · Short-Time Fourier Transform關卡 8-1 · 短時距傅立葉轉換SASP §8.1 ↗

Slide a window along the signal; take a Fourier transform at each position. The stack of spectra is a 2-D function of time and frequency — the STFT.

把視窗沿訊號滑動，每個位置取一次傅立葉轉換。疊起來就是時間與頻率的 2D 函數 — 即 STFT。
