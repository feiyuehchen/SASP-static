Each cochlear place responds to nearby frequencies with an asymmetric roaming. Summing those smeared contributions gives the excitation pattern used by loudness models.

耳蝸每個位置對鄰近頻率皆有不對稱響應。把這些塗抹疊加起來，就是響度模型用到的激發圖樣。
