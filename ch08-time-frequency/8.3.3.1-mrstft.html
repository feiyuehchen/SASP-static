Stage 8-3-3-1 · Multi-Resolution STFT關卡 8-3-3-1 · 多解析 STFTSASP §8.3.3.1 ↗

To match the ear's constant-$Q$ resolution, compute several STFTs with different window lengths and combine. Low-frequency bands take the long-window STFT; high-frequency bands take the short-window one.

為了貼合耳朵的常 Q 解析，算幾組不同視窗長度的 STFT 再合併：低頻用長視窗、高頻用短視窗。
