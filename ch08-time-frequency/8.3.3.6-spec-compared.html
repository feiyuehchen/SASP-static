Linear STFT vs Bark-warped vs loudness-scaled: same signal, different stories. The loudness version compresses loud content, amplifies perceptually-important quiet content, and generally lines up with what a listener would call "the shape of the sound."

線性 STFT vs Bark 扭曲 vs 響度尺度：同一段訊號、三種故事。響度版壓縮大聲段、放大感知重要的安靜部分，最接近聽者口中「聲音的形狀」。
