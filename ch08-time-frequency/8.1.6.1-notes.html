Stage 8-1-6-1 · Notes關卡 8-1-6-1 · 注意事項SASP §8.1.6.1 ↗

  • Matlab's spectrogram computes the one-sided PSD by default — divide by $f_s$ if you want unit-consistent energy.
  • Matlab 的 spectrogram 預設給單邊 PSD — 想要能量單位一致要除以 $f_s$。
  • Plotting tip: 20*log10 the magnitude and clip at a reasonable dynamic range (e.g. 80 dB) for readable colours.
  • 繪圖小訣竅：取 20*log10 後以合理動態範圍（如 80 dB）截斷，顏色才讀得清楚。
