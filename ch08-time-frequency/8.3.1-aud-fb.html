Stage 8-3-1 · Auditory Filter Banks關卡 8-3-1 · 聽覺濾波器組SASP §8.3.1 ↗

24 Bark bands (or ~40 ERBs) tile the audible range with roughly constant-$Q$ filters that match the ear's critical bands. Summing STFT bins into these bands gives the first approximation of a perceptual spectrogram.

24 個 Bark 頻帶（或約 40 個 ERB）以近似常 Q 的濾波器覆蓋可聽範圍，對應耳朵的臨界頻帶。把 STFT bin 合併到這些頻帶，就是感知聲譜圖的第一版近似。
