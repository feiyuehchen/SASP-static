Rounded-exponential ("roex") filters fit masking data well. Their slopes are steeper toward lower frequencies at high levels — an important detail that real loudness models include.

Rounded-exponential (roex) 濾波器與遮蔽實驗資料吻合度高。高音量時下降側比上升側更陡 — 真實響度模型會把這細節納入。
