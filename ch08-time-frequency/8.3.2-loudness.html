Take the band energies, apply the equal-loudness contour (dB SPL → phons), then the $P^{0.3}$ power law to get sones. Display sones vs time and critical band. Now the colour scale matches what you hear, not what a meter reads.

把頻帶能量先套等響度曲線（dB SPL → phon），再用 $P^{0.3}$ 律轉成 sone。畫 sone 對時間與臨界頻帶。色階就對應聽感，而非儀表讀數。
