Stage 8-3 · Audio Spectrograms關卡 8-3 · 音訊聲譜圖SASP §8.3 ↗

Classic STFT spectrograms show equal-Hz bands — but our hearing is logarithmic and has frequency-dependent bandwidth. Audio-ready spectrograms warp the axis (Bark, ERB) and replace linear magnitude with perceived loudness.

傳統 STFT 聲譜圖用等 Hz 頻帶 — 但人耳是對數頻率、頻帶寬度隨頻率變。音訊用的聲譜圖改用 Bark、ERB 等尺度變換，把振幅換成感知響度。
