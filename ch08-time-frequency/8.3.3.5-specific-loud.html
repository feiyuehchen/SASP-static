Stage 8-3-3-5 · Specific Loudness關卡 8-3-3-5 · 特定響度SASP §8.3.3.5 ↗

$$N'(z)\;\propto\;\bigl[E(z)\bigr]^{0.23}$$

$N'$ is the specific loudness at critical band $z$; $E$ is the excitation. Integrating $N'$ across $z$ gives overall loudness in sones.

$N'$ 為臨界頻帶 $z$ 的特定響度；$E$ 為激發。對 $z$ 積分得到 sone 單位的總響度。
