Stage 8-2 · Classic Spectrograms關卡 8-2 · 經典聲譜圖SASP §8.2 ↗

Two flavours of speech spectrogram: narrow-band (long window, fine frequency, smeared in time — shows harmonics) and wide-band (short window, fine time, smeared in frequency — shows formants as bars).

語音聲譜圖有兩味：窄頻（長視窗，頻細、時糊 — 顯示諧波）與寬頻（短視窗，時細、頻糊 — 顯示共振峰條紋）。
