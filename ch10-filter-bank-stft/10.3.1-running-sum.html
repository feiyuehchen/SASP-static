Stage 10-3-1 · Running-Sum Lowpass關卡 10-3-1 · 累計和低通SASP §10.3.1 ↗

$$y[n]=\sum_{k=0}^{N-1}x[n-k]\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad H(\omega)=e^{-j\omega(N-1)/2}\,\frac{\sin(N\omega/2)}{\sin(\omega/2)}$$

The prototype: a length-$N$ running sum. Its frequency response is the Dirichlet kernel; zeros at multiples of $2\pi/N$ — exactly where the other DFT-FB channels want to put their peaks.

原型：長度 $N$ 的累計和。頻率響應為 Dirichlet 核；零點位於 $2\pi/N$ 的倍數 — 正是其他 DFT-FB 通道想放峰的位置。
