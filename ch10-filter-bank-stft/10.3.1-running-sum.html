Stage 10-3-1 · The Running-Sum Lowpass 關卡 10-3-1 · 累計和低通 SASP §10.3.1 ↗

The length-$N$ running sum is the cheapest possible lowpass — $N-1$ adds, no multiplies. It's also the worst behaved (Dirichlet sinc with $-13$ dB side lobes). Yet it's the secret prototype filter hiding inside every unwindowed DFT filter bank. Get to know it.

長度 $N$ 累計和是最便宜的低通 — 只要 $N-1$ 次加、完全不乘。它也是表現最糟的（Dirichlet sinc，旁波瓣僅 $-13$ dB）。但它正是藏在所有未加窗 DFT 濾波器組背後的祕密原型。讓我們仔細認識它。

Impulse response

脈衝響應

$$h_{\text{RS}}[n]\;=\;\begin{cases}1, & 0\le n < N \\ 0, & \text{otherwise}\end{cases}$$

A length-$N$ rectangle of height 1. Apply to any $x[n]$ and you get the running sum of the last $N$ samples:

高度 1、長度 $N$ 的矩形。套在任意 $x[n]$ 上，就是最近 $N$ 個樣本的累計和：

$$y[n]\;=\;(x * h_{\text{RS}})[n]\;=\;\sum_{m=0}^{N-1} x[n-m]$$

Equivalent to an $N$-tap moving average up to a scale factor $N$. In the DFT-FB interpretation we keep the scale-$N$ version so the DC bin matches $\sum_n x[n]$ directly.

等同於 $N$-tap 移動平均（差一個倍數 $N$）。在 DFT-FB 詮釋下我們保留倍數 $N$ 版本，讓直流 bin 恰好等於 $\sum_n x[n]$。

Frequency response — the Dirichlet sinc

頻率響應 — Dirichlet sinc

DTFT
$$H_{\text{RS}}(\omega)\;=\;\sum_{n=0}^{N-1} e^{-j\omega n}\;=\;e^{-j\omega(N-1)/2}\cdot\frac{\sin(N\omega/2)}{\sin(\omega/2)}$$

Magnitude is the Dirichlet kernel $|\sin(N\omega/2)/\sin(\omega/2)|$, a periodic sinc-shaped envelope. The linear-phase factor $e^{-j\omega(N-1)/2}$ is the constant group delay $(N-1)/2$ — no phase distortion, just a delay.

振幅是 Dirichlet kernel $|\sin(N\omega/2)/\sin(\omega/2)|$，一個週期 sinc 形狀的包絡。線性相位因子 $e^{-j\omega(N-1)/2}$ 代表等值群延遲 $(N-1)/2$ — 沒有相位失真，只是延遲。

Key numerical features

關鍵數值特徵

DC gain: $H_{\text{RS}}(0) = N$. That's why we keep the scale-$N$ factor.
First spectral null: $\omega = 2\pi/N$ — exactly one DFT bin.
Subsequent nulls: $\omega = 2\pi k/N$ for every integer $k\ne 0$.
Side-lobe peaks: midway between nulls, level $-13$ dB (first), $-17.8$ dB (second), decaying like $1/|\omega|$.
Main-lobe width: $4\pi/N$ (null-to-null); half-power bandwidth $\approx 0.886 \cdot 2\pi/N$.
Group delay: $(N-1)/2$ samples, constant. 直流增益：$H_{\text{RS}}(0) = N$。所以我們保留倍數 $N$。
第一個零點：$\omega = 2\pi/N$ — 恰好一個 DFT bin。
後續零點：$\omega = 2\pi k/N$，任意非零整數 $k$。
旁波瓣峰：兩零點中間，第一個 $-13$ dB、第二個 $-17.8$ dB，以 $1/|\omega|$ 速度衰減。
主波瓣寬：$4\pi/N$（零到零）；半功率頻寬約 $0.886 \cdot 2\pi/N$。
群延遲：$(N-1)/2$ 個樣本，固定。

z-transform and pole–zero view

Z 轉換與極零點觀點

$$H_{\text{RS}}(z)\;=\;\sum_{n=0}^{N-1} z^{-n}\;=\;\frac{1 - z^{-N}}{1 - z^{-1}}$$

$N$ zeros at the $N$-th roots of unity except the one at $z=1$ (which is cancelled by the denominator pole). So $N-1$ zeros are spread uniformly on the unit circle — this is the Dirichlet pattern's origin. This factored form also hints at the recursive cumulative-sum implementation: one multiply-free pole at $z=1$ + one unit delay at $z^{-N}$.

$N$ 個零點在 $N$ 次單位根上，除了 $z=1$ 那個（被分母的極點抵消）。所以 $N-1$ 個零點均勻分布於單位圓 — 這就是 Dirichlet 圖樣的起源。此因式分解也暗示一個遞迴的累計和實作：$z=1$ 有一個免乘極點、$z^{-N}$ 有一個單位延遲。

Cheap, but leaky. The running sum is the cheapest FIR LP you can build. Its $-13$ dB side-lobes are the default penalty for "no window, just a raw DFT". Every other window in Ch 4 and Ch 10 is a way to trade that cheapness for better side-lobe suppression.

便宜，但漏。累計和是你能做出的最便宜 FIR 低通。$-13$ dB 的旁波瓣是「不加窗、純做 DFT」的預設代價。Ch 4 和 Ch 10 的所有視窗都是在用「多一點計算」換「更低的旁波瓣」。
