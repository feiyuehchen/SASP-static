Stage 10-8-2 · Downsampling with Anti-Aliasing關卡 10-8-2 · 抗混疊的降取樣SASP §10.8.2 ↗

For alias-free decimation by $R$, each channel's bandpass must be narrower than $2\pi/R$. Longer / more-tapered windows give narrower channels but fatter main lobes.

要 $R$ 倍降取樣無混疊，每通道帶通需窄於 $2\pi/R$。更長、更收斂的視窗 → 通道更窄，但主瓣更寬。
