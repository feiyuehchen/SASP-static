Stage 10-3-4 · Uniform Running-Sum Filter Banks關卡 10-3-4 · 均勻累計和濾波器組SASP §10.3.4 ↗

$N$ running-sum channels, each modulated by $e^{j2\pi k n/N}$, form a uniform filter bank. DC gains sum to $N$ and the outputs, de-modulated and summed, recover the input.

$N$ 個累計和通道，分別以 $e^{j2\pi k n/N}$ 調變，構成均勻濾波器組。DC 增益總和 $N$；輸出反調變再相加即還原輸入。
