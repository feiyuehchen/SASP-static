Stage 10-3-4 · Uniform Running-Sum Filter Banks 關卡 10-3-4 · 均勻累計和濾波器組 SASP §10.3.4 ↗

Collect the single-channel building block of §10.3.3 into an $N$-channel uniform running-sum filter bank: $N$ bandpass filters, all identical-shape rectangular-sinc prototypes, placed on a uniform grid $\omega_k = 2\pi k/N$. We derive its total frequency response, show it equals an unwindowed DFT, and tee up the system diagram in §10.3.4.1.

把 §10.3.3 的單通道積木組成一個 $N$ 通道的均勻累計和濾波器組：$N$ 個帶通、同一個矩形-sinc 原型、等距排在 $\omega_k = 2\pi k/N$ 網格上。我們推導其總頻率響應、證明等價於未加窗 DFT，並為 §10.3.4.1 的系統圖預備。

The bank, written out

完整寫出這個組

CHANNEL k (k = 0..N-1)
$$h_k[n]\;=\;\underbrace{h_{\text{RS}}[n]}_{\text{rect of length }N}\cdot\;e^{j 2\pi k n / N}$$
FREQ RESPONSE
$$H_k(\omega)\;=\;H_{\text{RS}}(\omega - \omega_k)\;=\;e^{-j(\omega-\omega_k)(N-1)/2}\,\frac{\sin\!\bigl(N(\omega-\omega_k)/2\bigr)}{\sin\!\bigl((\omega-\omega_k)/2\bigr)}$$

$N$ Dirichlet sincs on the unit circle, regularly spaced by $2\pi/N$. Each peak sits at the next channel's first null. The total response sums to a constant on the unit circle:

$N$ 個 Dirichlet sinc 均勻分佈於單位圓，間距 $2\pi/N$。每個峰值恰落在下一個通道的第一零點。全部通道相加後在單位圓上恰為常數：

SUM OF CHANNELS = CONSTANT
$$\sum_{k=0}^{N-1} H_k(\omega)\;=\;N\quad\text{(for all }\omega\in[0, 2\pi)\text{)}$$

That is why the raw DFT FB has flat total response: the sinc peaks and sinc nulls interlock exactly. Individual sincs leak badly, but their sum doesn't — this is the punchline of "unwindowed DFT is a FB".

這就是未加窗 DFT FB 總響應平坦的原因：sinc 峰與 sinc 零完美咬合。單一 sinc 洩漏嚴重，總和卻不會 — 這正是「未加窗 DFT 是 FB」最爽快的結論。

Equivalent to an unwindowed DFT

等價於未加窗 DFT

Run the bank at hop $R=1$ and you get an STFT of $x$ with a rectangular window, one output sample per input sample. The matrix of outputs is literally $X_n[k]=\sum_m x[n+m]e^{-j\omega_k m}$ — a sliding DFT. This is the tightest possible FB–DFT equivalence.

讓此組以跳距 $R=1$ 運作，就得到矩形視窗的 STFT，每個輸入樣本對應一個輸出樣本。輸出矩陣正是 $X_n[k]=\sum_m x[n+m]e^{-j\omega_k m}$ — 一個滑動 DFT。這是 FB↔DFT 最緊的等價。

What's wrong with it (and what Ch 10 fixes)

它哪裡不好（以及 Ch 10 如何修）

1. Sidelobes leak at −13 dB. Fix: replace rect with Hann/Hamming/Blackman/Kaiser (§10.4+).
2. R = 1 is maximally oversampled. Fix: choose larger hop $R$, track the Nyquist constraint (§10.8).
3. Length = $N$ ties window shape to FFT size. Fix: Portnoff windows with length > N (§10.7). 1. 旁波瓣僅 −13 dB。解法：把矩形換成 Hann / Hamming / Blackman / Kaiser（§10.4 起）。
2. R = 1 過度取樣。解法：選較大跳距 $R$，並留意 Nyquist 限制（§10.8）。
3. 視窗長 = $N$ 把形狀綁死 FFT 大小。解法：用長度 > N 的 Portnoff 視窗（§10.7）。

Keep the structure, swap the prototype. The uniform part (equal spacing $2\pi/N$) is what makes an efficient FFT implementation possible. The running-sum part is the weakest link; every future improvement is a choice of a better prototype $w[n]$ keeping the same equal-spacing structure.

保留結構、替換原型。均勻的部分（等距 $2\pi/N$）讓 FFT 高效實作成為可能。累計和的部分是最弱環節；之後所有改進都是換一個更好的原型 $w[n]$、保留同樣的等距結構。

實驗 · 看 sinc 相加歸零平坦
