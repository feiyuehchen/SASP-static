Stage 10-8-2-1 · Properly Anti-Aliasing Window Transforms 關卡 10-8-2-1 · 正確抗混疊的視窗頻譜 SASP §10.8.2.1 ↗

State precisely what it means for a window's DTFT $W(\omega)$ to anti-alias a hop-$R$ FB, and give the standard "figure of merit" numbers you'll see in window-design tables.

精確敘述「視窗 DTFT $W(\omega)$ 能否抗跳距 $R$ 混疊」的含義，並給出視窗設計表中常見的「指標」數字。

Three figures of merit

三個指標

(a) Main-lobe width $B_m$. The frequency range around DC where $|W(\omega)|$ exceeds its $-6$ dB point. Units: radians/sample. Defines frequency resolution.
(b) Peak side-lobe level $A_s$. The tallest side-lobe, in dB below $|W(0)|$. Defines worst-case alias contribution.
(c) Asymptotic side-lobe roll-off. How fast side-lobes decay — rect: $1/|\omega|$; Hann: $1/|\omega|^3$; Blackman: $1/|\omega|^5$. Defines average alias energy over a wide band. (a) 主波瓣寬 $B_m$。直流周圍 $|W(\omega)|$ 超過 $-6$ dB 點的頻率區間。單位：rad/sample。決定頻率解析度。
(b) 峰值旁波瓣位階 $A_s$。最高旁波瓣（dB 相對 $|W(0)|$）。決定最壞情況的混疊貢獻。
(c) 旁波瓣漸近衰減率。旁波瓣衰減速度 — rect: $1/|\omega|$；Hann: $1/|\omega|^3$；Blackman: $1/|\omega|^5$。決定寬頻段的平均混疊能量。

Anti-alias budget at hop $R$

跳距 $R$ 的抗混疊預算

For the channel-sum to cancel aliases within tolerance $\epsilon$, you need both:

要在容忍度 $\epsilon$ 內讓通道求和相消混疊，兩件事必須同時成立：

$$(1)\;\; B_m \le \frac{2\pi}{R},\qquad(2)\;\;|W(\omega)|\le\epsilon\,|W(0)|\text{ for all }|\omega|\ge\frac{\pi}{R}$$

(1) controls the main lobe bleed into the adjacent channel; (2) controls the side-lobe contribution from distant bins. Both come from the Nyquist($R$) / anti-alias analysis of §10.8.1.1.

(1) 控制主波瓣外溢到鄰通道；(2) 控制遠處 bin 的旁波瓣貢獻。兩者皆由 §10.8.1.1 的 Nyquist($R$) / 抗混疊分析而來。

$B_m$ and $A_s$ are always in tension. Lower $A_s$ (cleaner aliasing) requires wider $B_m$ (worse resolution). The job of window design is to find the shape that balances the two for your hop $R$ and noise floor.

$B_m$ 與 $A_s$ 永遠互相拉扯。$A_s$ 越低（混疊越乾淨）就 $B_m$ 越寬（解析度越差）。視窗設計就是在你的跳距 $R$ 與雜訊底間取得形狀平衡。
