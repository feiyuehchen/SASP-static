Stage 10-8-2-1 · Properly Anti-Aliasing Window Transforms關卡 10-8-2-1 · 正確抗混疊的視窗轉換SASP §10.8.2.1 ↗

A good anti-aliasing window has its stopband start before $2\pi/R$ and sits below a target attenuation (e.g. $-60$ dB). Kaiser $\beta$ and Dolph-Chebyshev are typical when the design is critical.

好的抗混疊視窗：停帶起點在 $2\pi/R$ 前，且低於目標衰減（如 $-60$ dB）。要求嚴格時常用 Kaiser $\beta$ 或 Dolph-Chebyshev。
