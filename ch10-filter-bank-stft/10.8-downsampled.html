Stage 10-8 · Downsampled STFT Filter Banks關卡 10-8 · 降取樣 STFT 濾波器組SASP §10.8 ↗

Hops $R>1$ downsample each channel's output by $R$, introducing aliasing unless the channel filter is narrow enough. Nyquist(N) / COLA conditions earn their keep here.

跳距 $R>1$ 相當於每通道降 $R$ 倍取樣；除非通道濾波器夠窄否則會產生混疊。Nyquist(N) / COLA 條件就是用於此。
