Stage 10-8 · Downsampled STFT Filter Banks 關卡 10-8 · 降取樣 STFT 濾波器組 SASP §10.8 ↗

So far our STFT FB ran at hop $R=1$ — one output per input sample, $N\times$ redundant. Real systems run at $R>1$ (typical: $R=N/2, N/4, N/8$). This introduces aliasing in each channel. This stage derives the conditions under which reconstruction still works with hop-wise downsampling, and names the new term: "downsampled STFT filter bank".

目前為止我們的 STFT FB 跑 $R=1$ — 每輸入樣本一個輸出、$N$ 倍冗餘。實務系統用 $R>1$（常見：$R=N/2, N/4, N/8$）。這會在每個通道引入混疊。本關推導 hop-wise 降取樣下仍可重建的條件，並正式命名：「降取樣 STFT 濾波器組」。

What downsampling does per channel

每通道降取樣的效果

From §10.1.2, channel $k$'s pre-hop output is $(x * h_k)[n]$. Sampling this at times $n = mR$ is a downsample by $R$. Standard multirate: downsampling by $R$ produces aliased copies of the channel's spectrum spaced by $2\pi/R$:

§10.1.2 指出，第 $k$ 通道的未降取樣輸出為 $(x * h_k)[n]$。對 $n = mR$ 取樣就是降 $R$。標準多速率結果：以 $R$ 降取樣會產生間距 $2\pi/R$ 的混疊副本：

$$Y_k(\omega)\;=\;\frac{1}{R}\sum_{\ell=0}^{R-1} H_k\!\left(\tfrac{\omega - 2\pi\ell}{R}\right)\,X\!\left(\tfrac{\omega - 2\pi\ell}{R}\right)$$

Each channel, taken in isolation, does alias. But channel-by-channel aliasing is allowed to happen as long as the sum over all $k$ cancels it out — that's how FBS with $R>1$ keeps PR.

孤立看每個通道確實有混疊。但通道層級的混疊是被允許的，只要對所有 $k$ 求和後相消 — FBS 在 $R>1$ 下保 PR 就是靠這個。

Hop choice: Nyquist rate of a channel

跳距選擇：每通道的 Nyquist 速率

Each channel has bandwidth roughly $2\pi/N$ (main-lobe width of the prototype window). Sampling at hop $R$ corresponds to a rate of $1/R$, which supports bandwidth up to $2\pi/R$. Lossless requires $2\pi/R \ge 2\pi/N$, i.e. $R \le N$ — the "maximally decimated" regime sits at $R=N$. Most audio engineering prefers $R \le N/2$ for comfort (oversampled).

每通道頻寬約 $2\pi/N$（原型視窗主波瓣寬）。以跳距 $R$ 取樣對應速率 $1/R$，可支撐頻寬 $2\pi/R$。無損要求 $2\pi/R \ge 2\pi/N$，即 $R \le N$；$R=N$ 為「最大降取樣」。音訊工程多選 $R \le N/2$（過取樣）圖方便。

What changes in the condition table

條件表會變什麼

With hop $R$:

取跳距 $R$ 時：

OLA side: need COLA at hop $R$ (window sum over frames is constant) — unchanged.
FBS side: need Nyquist at rate $R$ on the window's spectrum, i.e. $W(2\pi k/R)=0$ for $k\ne 0$.
Extra constraint: the channel bandwidth $\sim 2\pi/N$ must fit inside a $2\pi/R$ sub-band — so $R\le N$. Violating this means aliasing doesn't cancel. OLA 側：要求跳距 $R$ 的 COLA（視窗對 frame 求和為定值）— 不變。
FBS 側：要求視窗頻譜速率 $R$ 的 Nyquist，即 $W(2\pi k/R)=0$、$k\ne 0$。
額外約束：通道頻寬約 $2\pi/N$ 必須落在 $2\pi/R$ 子頻段內 — 故 $R\le N$。違反即混疊不相消。

§10.8.1–10.8.3 預覽

10.8.2 抗混疊：視窗旁波瓣不夠緊時混疊相消失敗。針對特定 $R$ 設計的視窗。

Hop = decimation per channel. The STFT hop is a per-channel downsampler; each channel aliases; alias cancellation across $N$ channels is what gives PR at $R>1$. Pick $R\le N$ (usually $R=N/2$) and a window whose side-lobes don't break the cancellation.

跳距 = 每通道降取樣。STFT 跳距等同每通道降取樣；每通道各自混疊；$N$ 個通道間的混疊相消才給出 $R>1$ 的 PR。取 $R\le N$（常 $R=N/2$）並選旁波瓣不破壞相消的視窗。
