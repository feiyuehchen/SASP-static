Stage 10-8-3-2 · Periodic-Hamming OLA via Poisson Summation 關卡 10-8-3-2 · 以 Poisson 求和導出週期 Hamming OLA SASP §10.8.3.2 ↗

Re-prove Hamming COLA by Poisson summation instead of brute-force algebra. The PSF route turns a time-domain infinite sum into a frequency-domain sample check — often cleaner and always generalisable.

用 Poisson 求和重證 Hamming COLA，取代暴力代數。PSF 路徑把時域無限求和變成頻域取樣檢查，通常更乾淨、且可推廣。

Periodic Hamming

週期 Hamming

$$w[n]\;=\;0.54\,-\,0.46\cos\!\left(\tfrac{2\pi n}{M}\right)$$

Its DTFT is three impulses on $[-\pi, \pi]$: at $\omega=0$ (weight $0.54$) and at $\omega=\pm 2\pi/M$ (weight $-0.23$ each). Compactly:

其 DTFT 是 $[-\pi, \pi]$ 上三個脈衝：$\omega=0$（權重 $0.54$）與 $\omega=\pm 2\pi/M$（各 $-0.23$）。緊湊寫法：

W(ω) · PERIODIC HAMMING
$$W(\omega)\;=\;0.54\,\delta(\omega)\,-\,0.23\bigl[\delta(\omega - \tfrac{2\pi}{M}) + \delta(\omega + \tfrac{2\pi}{M})\bigr]$$

Apply PSF with hop $R = M/2$

套 PSF（跳距 $R = M/2$）

$$\sum_m w[n - mR]\;=\;\frac{1}{R}\sum_k W\!\left(\tfrac{2\pi k}{R}\right)\,e^{j 2\pi k n/R}$$

At $R = M/2$, the sampling grid is $\omega = 2\pi k /(M/2) = 4\pi k/M$. Evaluate $W$ at these:

$R = M/2$ 時，取樣網格 $\omega = 2\pi k /(M/2) = 4\pi k/M$。在此代入 $W$：

$k=0$: $W(0) = 0.54$ (DC term).
$k = \pm 1$: $W(\pm 4\pi/M)$ — but the window's impulses sit at $\omega = \pm 2\pi/M$, not $\pm 4\pi/M$. So $W(\pm 4\pi/M) = 0$.
$k = \pm 2, \pm 3, \ldots$: Also zero — the window's spectrum is zero everywhere outside the three impulses. $k=0$：$W(0) = 0.54$（直流項）。
$k = \pm 1$：$W(\pm 4\pi/M)$ — 但視窗脈衝在 $\omega = \pm 2\pi/M$，非 $\pm 4\pi/M$。故 $W(\pm 4\pi/M) = 0$。
$k = \pm 2, \pm 3, \ldots$：也為零 — 視窗頻譜除三個脈衝外處處為零。

Result

結果

$$\sum_m w[n - mR]\;=\;\frac{1}{R}\cdot 0.54\;=\;\frac{2}{M}\cdot 0.54\quad(\text{const})$$

Constant — COLA exactly. No ripple. The PSF frame nailed what bare-hands algebra only approximated.

定值 — 嚴格 COLA、零漣波。PSF 框架把暴力算只能近似的結果釘死。

Generalisation

推廣

This proof works for any window whose DTFT is a finite set of impulses at $\pm 2\pi p/M$ for small integer $p$ — i.e. the generalised Hamming family (including Hann, with impulses at $0, \pm 2\pi/M$, weights $0.5, -0.25, -0.25$). COLA at $R = M/2, M/4, M/6, \ldots$ holds whenever the sampling grid $4\pi k/M, 8\pi k/M, \ldots$ misses all the impulses except the DC one.

此證明適用於 DTFT 為 $\pm 2\pi p/M$（$p$ 為小整數）有限脈衝集合的任意視窗 — 即廣義 Hamming 家族（含 Hann，脈衝在 $0, \pm 2\pi/M$、權重 $0.5, -0.25, -0.25$）。當取樣網格 $4\pi k/M, 8\pi k/M, \ldots$ 錯過除直流外的所有脈衝時，COLA 在 $R = M/2, M/4, M/6, \ldots$ 成立。

PSF converts "sum over frames" into "sample spectrum at grid". Whenever both sides of Poisson summation are easy to evaluate, PSF is the fastest route to a COLA proof. Memorise this pattern.

PSF 把「對 frame 求和」轉成「在網格上取頻譜樣本」。當 Poisson 求和兩邊都好求時，PSF 是 COLA 證明的最快路徑。這個套路背起來。
