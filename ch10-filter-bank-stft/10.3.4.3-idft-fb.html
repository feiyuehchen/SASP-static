Stage 10-3-4-3 · Inverse DFT and the FB Sum關卡 10-3-4-3 · IDFT 與 FB 總和SASP §10.3.4.3 ↗

Summing all $N$ FB outputs at each time step is the inverse DFT of that time step's STFT column, divided by $N$. Rectangular window + $R=1$ → perfect reconstruction by FBS with trivial cost.

每個時間步長上把 $N$ 個 FB 輸出相加 = 對該時間 STFT 行做 IDFT，再除以 $N$。矩形視窗 + $R=1$ → FBS 完美重建，成本很低。
