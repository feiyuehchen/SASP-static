Stage 10-7 · Portnoff Windows關卡 10-7 · Portnoff 視窗SASP §10.7 ↗

Portnoff showed that if the window length is a multiple of the DFT size $N$, FBS perfect reconstruction still works — with extra design freedom and tighter main lobes. Cost: each FFT frame aliases the long window into $N$ samples.

Portnoff 證明：視窗長度若為 DFT 長度 $N$ 的整數倍，FBS 仍可完美重建 — 設計自由度更大、主瓣更窄。代價：每個 FFT frame 要把長視窗折疊回 $N$ 樣本。
