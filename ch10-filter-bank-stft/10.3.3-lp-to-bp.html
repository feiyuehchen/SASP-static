Stage 10-3-3 · Making a Bandpass Filter from a Lowpass 關卡 10-3-3 · 由低通構造帶通 SASP §10.3.3 ↗

Combine §10.3.1 (running-sum LP) with §10.3.2 (modulation) into one recipe: any lowpass prototype $h_0$ becomes a bandpass centred at $\omega_c$ by $h_c[n]=h_0[n]\,e^{j\omega_c n}$. This is the single building block of every uniform FB in the book.

把 §10.3.1（累計和低通）與 §10.3.2（調變）合成一個配方：任意低通原型 $h_0$ 藉由 $h_c[n]=h_0[n]\,e^{j\omega_c n}$ 就成為中心在 $\omega_c$ 的帶通。本書所有均勻 FB 都靠這一個單一積木。

The recipe

配方

LP → BP
$$h_c[n]\;=\;h_0[n]\,e^{j\omega_c n}\;\;\Longleftrightarrow\;\;H_c(\omega)\;=\;H_0(\omega-\omega_c)$$

Three statements that say the same thing:

以下三句是同一件事：

(1) Multiply $h_0[n]$ pointwise by the complex exponential $e^{j\omega_c n}$.
(2) Shift $H_0$'s spectrum right by $\omega_c$ on the unit circle.
(3) Rotate the pole-zero diagram of $H_0(z)$ by angle $\omega_c$ counter-clockwise. (1) 對 $h_0[n]$ 逐點乘上複指數 $e^{j\omega_c n}$。
(2) 單位圓上把 $H_0$ 的頻譜向右平移 $\omega_c$。
(3) 在 $z$ 平面上把 $H_0(z)$ 的極零點逆時針旋轉角度 $\omega_c$。

Worked example · rectangular LP centred at $\omega_c=\pi/2$

實例 · 中心在 $\omega_c=\pi/2$ 的矩形低通

Take the length-$8$ running sum $h_0[n]=1,1,1,1,1,1,1,1$. Apply $e^{j(\pi/2)n}$, which cycles through $1,j,-1,-j,1,j,-1,-j$. The bandpass $h_c[n]$ becomes exactly that sequence of $\pm 1,\pm j$ values. Its spectrum is the Dirichlet sinc shifted to be centred at $\omega=\pi/2$ — a bandpass with all 7 zeros on the unit circle at $\omega\in\{\pi/2+2\pi k/8 : k=1..7\}$.

取長度 $8$ 累計和 $h_0[n]=1,1,1,1,1,1,1,1$。套上 $e^{j(\pi/2)n}$，它依序輸出 $1,j,-1,-j,1,j,-1,-j$。帶通 $h_c[n]$ 就是這個 $\pm 1,\pm j$ 序列。其頻譜為 Dirichlet sinc 平移至中心 $\omega=\pi/2$ — 一個帶通，7 個零點都在單位圓上 $\omega\in\{\pi/2+2\pi k/8 : k=1..7\}$。

Why $\omega_k=2\pi k/N$ is the magic spacing

為何 $\omega_k=2\pi k/N$ 是那個神奇間距

For the length-$N$ rectangle, $H_0$'s zeros lie at $\omega=2\pi k/N$ for $k=1,\ldots,N-1$ (§10.3.1). Shifting by the same spacing places channel $k$'s peak exactly on channel $k-1$'s first zero. Result: critically sampled coverage of $[0, 2\pi)$, and the $N$ channels together reconstruct the signal perfectly when summed (§10.1.3 FBS).

長度 $N$ 矩形的 $H_0$ 零點在 $\omega=2\pi k/N$（$k=1,\ldots,N-1$；見 §10.3.1）。以相同間距位移，第 $k$ 通道的峰恰好落在第 $k-1$ 通道的第一零點。結果：$[0, 2\pi)$ 被臨界取樣覆蓋，$N$ 個通道相加就精確重建原訊號（§10.1.3 FBS）。

Real-valued variant · cosine modulation

實值版本 · 餘弦調變

Using $\cos(\omega_c n)=\tfrac{1}{2}(e^{j\omega_c n}+e^{-j\omega_c n})$ gives a two-sided bandpass (peaks at $\pm\omega_c$). For real FBs (MDCT, MPEG Layer I/II) this is the preferred modulation — it keeps everything real-valued, at the cost of needing $2\times$ as many channels to cover the same frequency range uniquely.

用 $\cos(\omega_c n)=\tfrac{1}{2}(e^{j\omega_c n}+e^{-j\omega_c n})$ 會得到雙邊帶通（$\pm\omega_c$ 兩個峰）。對實值 FB（MDCT、MPEG Layer I/II）這是首選 — 全部實數運算，代價是需要 $2\times$ 通道才能覆蓋相同頻率範圍。

Every bandpass in this chapter = one prototype × one complex sinusoid. Once you internalise this, a "DFT filter bank" is a one-liner: rect × $N$ equally spaced exponentials. Switching to Hamming / Blackman / Kaiser / Gaussian just changes the first factor.

本章所有帶通 = 一個原型 × 一個複正弦。內化這個觀念，「DFT 濾波器組」就是一句話：矩形 × $N$ 個等距複指數。換成 Hamming / Blackman / Kaiser / Gauss 只是改了前者。
