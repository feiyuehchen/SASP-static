% STFT as a filter bank — direct convolution
for k = 0:N-1
  hk = w .* exp(1j*2*pi*k*(0:N-1)'/N);   % modulated window
  Xk(:,k+1) = conv(x, hk);               % output of bin k
end

Inefficient (loops over $N$ filters) but makes the FB view concrete. The fast version reuses one FFT per time block.

低效（要跑 $N$ 個濾波器），但能具體看到 FB 詮釋。快速版每個時間 block 一次 FFT 就夠。
