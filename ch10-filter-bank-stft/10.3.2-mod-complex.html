Stage 10-3-2 · Modulation by a Complex Sinusoid關卡 10-3-2 · 複正弦調變SASP §10.3.2 ↗

Multiplying an impulse response by $e^{j\omega_0 n}$ shifts its frequency response by $\omega_0$. That is how we turn one prototype lowpass into $N$ different bandpass filters.

脈衝響應乘上 $e^{j\omega_0 n}$ → 頻率響應位移 $\omega_0$。這就是把一個原型低通變成 $N$ 個不同中心的帶通。
