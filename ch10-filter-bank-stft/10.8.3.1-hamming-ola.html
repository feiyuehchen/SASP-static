Stage 10-8-3-1 · Hamming OLA Example 關卡 10-8-3-1 · Hamming OLA 範例 SASP §10.8.3.1 ↗

Compute $\sum_m w_{\text{Hamming}}[n - mR]$ by direct algebra at $R = M/2$, quantify the residual ripple, and show why practitioners still call this "COLA" despite it not being exact.

直接代數計算 $\sum_m w_{\text{Hamming}}[n - mR]$（取 $R = M/2$），量化殘漣波，並說明為何實務仍稱之為「COLA」即使它非嚴格成立。

Hamming window

Hamming 視窗

$$w_{\text{Hamming}}[n]\;=\;0.54\,-\,0.46\cos\!\left(\tfrac{2\pi n}{M}\right),\quad 0\le n < M$$

Split into DC and cosine parts:

拆成直流與餘弦部分：

$$w_{\text{Hamming}}[n]\;=\;0.54\,\mathrm{rect}_M[n]\,-\,0.46\,\mathrm{rect}_M[n]\cos\!\left(\tfrac{2\pi n}{M}\right)$$

OLA at $R = M/2$

$R = M/2$ 的 OLA

The $0.54\,\mathrm{rect}_M$ part at $R=M/2$ is rectangular overlap-add — $0.54 \cdot 2 = 1.08$ everywhere after two adjacent windows add. The cosine part has period $M$, and at hop $M/2$ adjacent frames' cosines are offset by $\pi$ — so they destructively interfere. After adding:

$0.54\,\mathrm{rect}_M$ 在 $R=M/2$ 時為矩形 OLA — 兩相鄰視窗相加後每點 $0.54 \cdot 2 = 1.08$。餘弦部分週期 $M$；跳距 $M/2$ 時相鄰 frame 餘弦差 $\pi$，干涉後相消。加總：

SUM at R = M/2
$$\sum_m w_{\text{Hamming}}[n - mR]\;=\;1.08\,-\,0.92\cos\!\left(\tfrac{2\pi n}{M}\right)\cdot\underbrace{\delta_{\text{sum}}}_{\to 0\text{ ideally}}$$

Ideal cancellation gives constant $1.08$. Due to Hamming's non-zero endpoints ($0.08$ each), the cosine sum doesn't cancel perfectly at the join. Numerically the residual ripple is:

理想相消給定值 $1.08$。由於 Hamming 端點非零（各為 $0.08$），餘弦求和在接合處不完全相消。數值殘漣波為：

$$\text{ripple}\;\approx\;\pm\,0.01\,\text{around }1.08\;\approx\;-40\text{ dB peak-to-peak}$$

Is $-40$ dB audible?

$-40$ dB 可聽嗎？

Not for music content. A $-40$ dB modulation at the hop rate (which is $f_s/R$ Hz, i.e. ~87 Hz for $f_s=44.1$ kHz and $R=512$) would manifest as a faint AM-like artefact — masked by any audio with content louder than the noise floor by $40$+ dB, which covers essentially all musical material.

對音樂內容不會。$-40$ dB 的「跳距速率」調變（$f_s/R$ Hz，如 $f_s=44.1$ kHz、$R=512$ 時約 87 Hz）會以隱約 AM 噪音形式出現，但任何比雜訊底高 $40$ dB 以上的音訊都會把它遮蔽掉，幾乎所有音樂都是。

Fix: periodic Hamming

修正：週期 Hamming

Extend Hamming to periodic by dividing by $M$ instead of $M-1$ in the cosine argument: $w[n] = 0.54 - 0.46\cos(2\pi n/M)$ with endpoints exactly aligned. Then the cosine parts cancel exactly at $R=M/2$ and COLA holds with no ripple. This is the topic of §10.8.3.2.

修正：將 Hamming 改為週期版，餘弦中除以 $M$ 而非 $M-1$：$w[n] = 0.54 - 0.46\cos(2\pi n/M)$，端點恰齊。此時 $R=M/2$ 下餘弦部分嚴格相消，COLA 零漣波。§10.8.3.2 會詳述。

Standard Hamming is "COLA to within $1\%$". Good enough for audio; use periodic Hamming if you need mathematical exactness (e.g. for a round-trip test at machine epsilon).

標準 Hamming 是「$1\%$ 精度的 COLA」。對音訊夠用；需要數學嚴格（如機器 epsilon 的往返測試）請改用週期 Hamming。
