Stage 10-1-1 · Overlap-Add View of the STFT 關卡 10-1-1 · STFT 的重疊相加觀點 SASP §10.1.1 ↗

Show that the STFT can be built and inverted frame-by-frame: window a chunk, FFT it, store it, move the window by $R$ samples, repeat. Reconstruction = IFFT each column of the matrix and overlap-add back. Perfect reconstruction condition = windows must add up to a constant (COLA).

證明 STFT 可以「一 frame 一 frame」正反向計算：加窗一段、FFT、存起來、把視窗前進 $R$ 個樣本、重複。逆向 = 把矩陣每一列 IFFT 再重疊相加回來。完美重建條件 = 視窗加總成定值（COLA）。

Analysis — windowed frame DFT

分析 — 加窗 frame 的 DFT

Let $x_m[n] \triangleq x[n]\,w[n-mR]$ be the windowed frame starting at sample $mR$. Then:

令 $x_m[n] \triangleq x[n]\,w[n-mR]$ 為第 $m$ 個「加窗 frame」（起始於樣本 $mR$），則：

ROW m OF THE STFT
$$X_m[k]\;=\;\sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty} x_m[n]\,e^{-j 2\pi k n / N}\;=\;\mathrm{DFT}_N\{x_m[n]\}[k]$$

Each row of the STFT matrix is literally the FFT of one windowed frame. If $w$ has length $N$ you can do an $N$-point FFT; if you want extra zero-padding for smoother spectra use $L\ge N$ and treat the windowed frame as length-$L$.

STFT 矩陣的每一列就是一個加窗 frame 的 FFT。若 $w$ 長度為 $N$，直接做 $N$ 點 FFT；若想用零填充讓頻譜更平滑，就取 $L\ge N$，把 frame 當成長度 $L$ 即可。

Synthesis — IFFT + overlap-add

合成 — IFFT + 重疊相加

Take the IFFT of row $m$ to recover the windowed frame $\tilde x_m[n]$. If we never modified $X_m[k]$, then $\tilde x_m[n]=x_m[n]=x[n]w[n-mR]$. Summing over all frame positions:

對第 $m$ 列做 IFFT 拿回加窗 frame $\tilde x_m[n]$。若沒動 $X_m[k]$ 則 $\tilde x_m[n]=x_m[n]=x[n]w[n-mR]$。對所有 frame 位置求和：

OLA RECONSTRUCTION
$$\hat x[n]\;=\;\sum_{m} \tilde x_m[n]\;=\;x[n]\underbrace{\sum_{m} w[n-mR]}_{\text{window sum}}$$

The bracketed sum slides the window along the time axis with step $R$ and adds up everything at the current sample $n$. For lossless reconstruction we need this sum to be independent of $n$:

括弧裡的和把視窗以步距 $R$ 沿時間軸滑動、在當前樣本 $n$ 處全部加起來。為了無損重建，此和必須與 $n$ 無關：

COLA CONDITION
$$\sum_{m} w[n-mR]\;=\;\text{const}\quad\text{for all }n$$

When COLA holds, $\hat x[n]=\text{const}\cdot x[n]$ and we recover $x$ (up to a known scale). Many classic windows satisfy COLA at standard hops — rectangular at $R=N$, Hann at $R=N/2$, Hamming at $R\approx 0.23 N$, Blackman at $R=N/3$.

COLA 成立時 $\hat x[n]=\text{const}\cdot x[n]$，$x$ 被原貌還原（只差已知倍數）。經典視窗在標準跳距下都滿足 COLA — rect 在 $R=N$、Hann 在 $R=N/2$、Hamming 在 $R\approx 0.23 N$、Blackman 在 $R=N/3$。

OLA view is time-first. It treats the STFT matrix as a stack of short spectra. This is the view nearly every audio effects plugin ships with — it plays nicely with real-time streaming, block-based modifications, and time-axis rescaling.

OLA 觀點「時間優先」。把 STFT 矩陣看成一疊短時頻譜。這正是幾乎所有音訊 plugin 的預設觀點 — 跟即時串流、分塊修改、時間軸重採樣都完全相容。
