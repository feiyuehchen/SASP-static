Stage 10-3 · The DFT Filter Bank關卡 10-3 · DFT 濾波器組SASP §10.3 ↗

Pick $w$ = rectangular of length $N$. The STFT filter bank becomes the classic DFT filter bank: each channel is a shifted running-sum filter. Cheapest and most symmetric of the family.

取 $w$ = 長度 $N$ 矩形。STFT 濾波器組就成了經典的 DFT 濾波器組：每個通道是位移的累計和濾波器。最便宜、最對稱的成員。
