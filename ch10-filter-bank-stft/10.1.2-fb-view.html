Read each column of the STFT matrix (fixed frequency bin vs time) as the output of a bandpass filter. The filter's impulse response is the analysis window modulated to that frequency.

把 STFT 矩陣每一行（固定 bin 對時間）讀成一個帶通濾波器輸出。濾波器的脈衝響應 = 分析視窗乘上對應頻率的複正弦。
