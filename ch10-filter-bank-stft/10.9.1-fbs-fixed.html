When the modification mask $H[k]$ doesn't change across frames, analysis-mod-synthesis collapses into ordinary LTI filtering. Prove it, note which filters are expressible this way, and note the limitation.

當修改遮罩 $H[k]$ 不隨 frame 變，「分析 → 修改 → 合成」會塌縮成普通 LTI 濾波。本節證明、列出可表示的濾波器種類、並指出限制。

Equivalence with LTI filtering

與 LTI 濾波的等價

Let $H_m[k] = H[k]$ (no $m$-dependence). Multiply the STFT pointwise by $H[k]$ and synthesise:

令 $H_m[k] = H[k]$（不依 $m$）。逐點乘上 STFT 後合成：

$$y[n]\;=\;\text{ISTFT}\{H[k]\,X_m[k]\}\;=\;(x \ast h)[n]$$

where $h[n]$ is the length-$N$ IDFT of $H[k]$. So applying fixed $H[k]$ is the same as convolving $x$ with a length-$N$ FIR filter whose DTFT samples match $H[k]$ on the DFT grid.

其中 $h[n]$ 為 $H[k]$ 的長度 $N$ IDFT。所以套固定 $H[k]$ 等同於把 $x$ 與「DTFT 在 DFT 網格與 $H[k]$ 相符、長度 $N$」的 FIR 濾波器卷積。

When fixed mods are the right choice

何時選固定修改

Fixed EQ / LP / HP / BP / notch: the mask is a fixed magnitude shape; phase is usually forced to be linear or zero.
Comb-filter / chorus (fixed): $H[k]$ is a periodic mask in $k$.
Spectral whitening (fixed): $H[k] = 1/|W(\omega_k)|$ where $|W|$ is a long-term spectrum.
Fixed dereverberation (RIR inverse): $H[k]$ approximates the inverse of a measured room impulse response's DFT. 固定 EQ / LP / HP / BP / notch：遮罩是固定振幅形狀；相位通常取線性或零。
梳狀濾波 / 合唱（固定版）：$H[k]$ 是 $k$ 的週期遮罩。
頻譜白化（固定版）：$H[k] = 1/|W(\omega_k)|$，$|W|$ 為長期頻譜。
固定去混響（RIR 反轉）：$H[k]$ 近似某測得房間脈衝響應 DFT 的反轉。

The "$N$-tap" limitation

「$N$-tap」限制

Fixed $H[k]$ as a DFT mask gives you an LTI FIR filter of length $\le N$. Longer filters need a larger DFT (bigger $N$) or a structural change (polyphase, Portnoff). If your EQ needs sharper transitions than $4\pi/N$ rad/sample allows, go bigger on $N$ or move to the time-varying domain (§10.9.2) with some trade-offs.

固定 $H[k]$ 作為 DFT 遮罩，等同於長度 $\le N$ 的 LTI FIR。要更長得加大 $N$ 或改結構（polyphase、Portnoff）。若 EQ 轉折需要比 $4\pi/N$ rad/sample 更銳利，就需拉大 $N$ 或改走時變（§10.9.2）並承擔取捨。

Circular vs linear convolution

循環卷積 vs 線性卷積

Within a single frame, the DFT gives circular convolution. To make the effective operation linear (which is what we want for real filters), zero-pad the frame to length $\ge M + \text{filter-length} - 1$. Missing this pad is the classic "filter ringing at frame edges" bug.

單 frame 內 DFT 對應循環卷積。欲讓實際運算為線性（實濾波器需要），必須將 frame 零填充至長度 $\ge M + \text{濾波器長度} - 1$。沒填補就是經典的「frame 邊緣 ringing」bug。

Fixed modification = LTI filter. If your effect is time-invariant, implement it as a direct FIR convolution rather than via STFT — it's cheaper, has no frame boundary issues, and doesn't require PR. Use STFT modification only when you actually need frame-rate control (time-varying, per-frame analysis).

固定修改 = LTI 濾波。若效果時不變，直接用 FIR 卷積實作，比 STFT 便宜、無 frame 邊界問題、不需 PR。只在真的需要 frame 速率控制（時變、逐 frame 分析）時才用 STFT 修改。
