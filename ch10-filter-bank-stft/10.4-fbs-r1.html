Stage 10-4 · FBS Window Constraints for R=1 關卡 10-4 · 跳距 R=1 下的 FBS 視窗約束 SASP §10.4 ↗

§10.3.4.3 showed the rectangular window reconstructs exactly under FBS at $R=1$. What about Hann, Hamming, Blackman, Kaiser? Derive the precise necessary & sufficient condition a window must satisfy for filter-bank-summation perfect reconstruction at hop $R=1$. Spoiler: it's the Nyquist(N) condition we'll name in §10.5.

§10.3.4.3 證明矩形視窗在跳距 $R=1$ 下 FBS 可完美重建。那 Hann、Hamming、Blackman、Kaiser 如何？本節推導視窗滿足 filter-bank-summation $R=1$ 完美重建的充要條件。劇透：就是 §10.5 的 Nyquist(N) 條件。

Plug a general window into FBS

把一般視窗代入 FBS

At $R=1$, the STFT bin $k$ at time $n$ is:

$R=1$ 時第 $k$ 個 STFT bin 在時間 $n$：

$$X_n[k]\;=\;\sum_{\ell} x[\ell]\,w[\ell-n]\,e^{-j 2\pi k \ell / N}$$

FBS reconstruction: sum over $k$ with phase $e^{+j 2\pi k n / N}$, then divide by the normalisation we'll derive:

FBS 重建：對 $k$ 加權 $e^{+j 2\pi k n / N}$ 求和，除以待定歸一因子：

$$\hat x[n]\;=\;\frac{1}{C}\sum_{k=0}^{N-1} X_n[k]\,e^{j 2\pi k n / N}\;=\;\frac{1}{C}\sum_{\ell} x[\ell]\,w[\ell-n]\underbrace{\sum_{k=0}^{N-1} e^{j 2\pi k (n-\ell)/N}}_{=\;N\,\delta[(n-\ell)\bmod N]}$$

The inner sum over $k$ is the length-$N$ DFT-basis identity — it's $N$ whenever $(n-\ell)$ is a multiple of $N$ and zero otherwise. Substituting $\ell = n + pN$ (for integer $p$):

內部對 $k$ 求和是長度 $N$ 的 DFT 基底恆等式 — 當 $(n-\ell)$ 是 $N$ 的倍數時為 $N$、其他為 0。代換 $\ell = n + pN$（$p$ 為整數）：

FBS RECONSTRUCTION (GENERAL)
$$\hat x[n]\;=\;\frac{N}{C}\sum_{p\in\mathbb{Z}} x[n + pN]\,w[pN]$$

When does this equal $x[n]$?

何時這等於 $x[n]$？

We want $\hat x[n] = x[n]$ for every $x$. The right-hand side is a sum over shifted copies of $x$ weighted by $w[pN]$. Treating the equation as an identity in the signal $x$ forces the coefficient of $x[n + pN]$ to be $\delta[p]$ — in other words:

我們要對任意 $x$ 都有 $\hat x[n] = x[n]$。右邊是 $x$ 的位移版加權和，權重為 $w[pN]$。把式子視為 $x$ 的恆等式，$x[n + pN]$ 的係數須為 $\delta[p]$，即：

FBS PR CONDITION (time-domain)
$$w[pN]\;=\;\frac{C}{N}\,\delta[p]\quad\text{for all }p\in\mathbb{Z}$$

In plain language: when you sub-sample the window at every $N$-th sample, the result must be a single non-zero impulse (all other samples zero). Choosing the normalisation $C = N\,w[0]$ then forces $\hat x[n] = x[n]$.

白話：把視窗每 $N$ 個樣本取一次樣後，必須只剩一個非零脈衝（其他全零）。取 $C = N\,w[0]$ 即能保證 $\hat x[n] = x[n]$。

What this says about window length

視窗長度會怎樣

If the window has length $\le N$ (only $w[0]$ can be non-zero at a multiple of $N$), the condition is automatically satisfied. This covers the rectangular, Hann, Hamming, Blackman, Kaiser ($\beta$ finite) windows when truncated to length $N$. All of them reconstruct perfectly at $R=1$.

若視窗長度 $\le N$（只有 $w[0]$ 可能在 $N$ 的倍數處非零），條件自動滿足。這涵蓋長度截到 $N$ 的 rect、Hann、Hamming、Blackman、Kaiser（$\beta$ 有限）視窗；全部都能 $R=1$ 完美重建。

For longer windows (length $M > N$, e.g. Portnoff windows in §10.7) the condition becomes non-trivial: you need $w[pN] = 0$ for every non-zero $p$, which is a constraint on the zero-crossings of $w$. Windows designed this way exist (§10.7) but are not standard off-the-shelf.

若視窗較長（$M > N$，如 §10.7 的 Portnoff 視窗），條件變得不平凡：所有 $p \ne 0$ 都要 $w[pN]=0$，這是對 $w$ 零點位置的約束。這類視窗存在（§10.7）但不是現成工具。

FBS wants a window that's zero at every $N$-th sample except sample 0. This is the Nyquist(N) condition on the window itself — and in §10.5 we'll see it's the time-domain dual of the COLA condition. Short windows get it for free; long windows have to be designed for it.

FBS 要求「視窗在每 $N$ 個樣本取樣後只在 0 非零」。這是視窗本身的 Nyquist(N) 條件 — §10.5 會證它正是 COLA 的時域對偶。短視窗免費滿足；長視窗需特別設計。

實驗 · 視窗每 $N$ 個樣本取一次

藍：視窗 $w[n]$。黃點：每 $N$ 個樣本取一次的值 $w[0], w[N], w[2N], \ldots$。$R=1$ 下 FBS 成立要求 $n=0$ 以外的黃點都落在零線上。短視窗（$M\le N$）自動滿足；較長者需要 Portnoff / Nyquist(N) 設計（§10.7）。
