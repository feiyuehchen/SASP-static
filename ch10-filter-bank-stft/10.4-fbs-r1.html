Stage 10-4 · FBS Window Constraints for R=1關卡 10-4 · R=1 時的 FBS 視窗約束SASP §10.4 ↗

At hop $R=1$, the window's spectrum at frequencies $\omega_k=2\pi k/N$ ($k\ne 0$) must be zero for perfect FBS reconstruction. Rectangular and Nyquist-N windows satisfy this automatically.

$R=1$ 下要 FBS 完美重建，視窗在 $\omega_k=2\pi k/N$（$k\ne 0$）處的頻譜值須為零。矩形與 Nyquist-N 視窗自動滿足。
