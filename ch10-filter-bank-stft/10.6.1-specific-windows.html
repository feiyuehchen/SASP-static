  • Rectangular: COLA at $R=N$; Nyquist(N) in the Dirichlet sense.

  • Blackman at $R=N/3$: COLA; Nyquist at hop $N/3$.
  • Blackman（$R=N/3$）：COLA；跳距 $N/3$ 下 Nyquist。
