  • Smooth gain trajectories — abrupt changes cause audible clicks.
  • 增益軌跡要平滑 — 突變會 click 出聲。
  • Pick hop small enough that gain barely changes within one frame.
  • 跳距要夠小，讓單 frame 內增益變化不大。
  • Where possible, update gains only at frame boundaries.
  • 可能時只在 frame 邊界更新增益。
