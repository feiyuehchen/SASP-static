Stage 10-5 · Nyquist(N) Windows 關卡 10-5 · Nyquist(N) 視窗 SASP §10.5 ↗

Name the condition we derived in §10.4 and write it in all four equivalent forms — time-sampled, frequency-sampled, DTFT-periodised, and "impulse-train". Knowing which form to reach for is 90% of window analysis.

替 §10.4 推出的條件命名，並寫成四種等價形式 — 時域取樣、頻域取樣、DTFT 週期化、「脈衝串」形式。選對形式就完成 90% 的視窗分析。

Definition

定義

NYQUIST(N) CONDITION · TIME DOMAIN
$$w[pN]\;=\;\delta[p]\cdot w[0]\quad\text{for all }p\in\mathbb{Z}$$

Equivalently: the window, sub-sampled every $N$ samples, is a single non-zero impulse (the spike at $p=0$). Any window of length $\le N$ gets this trivially — all other multiples are outside the support. Longer windows need explicit design.

等價地：把視窗每 $N$ 個樣本取一次，結果只在 $p=0$ 處非零、其他為零。長度 $\le N$ 的視窗天然成立（其他倍數落在支撐外）；較長者需明確設計。

Four equivalent statements

四種等價敘述

(A) TIME SAMPLE
$$w[pN]\;=\;w[0]\,\delta[p]$$
(B) FREQUENCY SUM
$$\sum_{m=0}^{N-1} W\!\left(\omega + \tfrac{2\pi m}{N}\right)\;=\;N\,w[0]\;\;(\text{constant in }\omega)$$
(C) ALIAS COLLAPSE
$$\text{The length-}N\text{ aliasing of }w\text{ collapses to }w[0]\,\mathrm{rect}_N[n]$$
(D) PSF TWIN
$$W(\omega)\text{ sampled on the }N\text{-point grid }\omega=\tfrac{2\pi k}{N}\text{ gives }N\,w[0]\,\delta[k]$$

Forms (A) and (D) are Poisson-summation duals: a time-domain $N$-sampling ↔ a frequency-domain $N$-point evaluation. Forms (B) and (C) re-state the same thing via aliasing.

(A) 與 (D) 是 Poisson 求和對偶：時域每 $N$ 取樣 ↔ 頻域 $N$ 點取樣。(B) 與 (C) 只是從混疊角度把同一件事再說一次。

Who satisfies Nyquist(N)?

哪些視窗滿足 Nyquist(N)？

Trivially: any window of length $\le N$ (rect, Hann, Hamming, Blackman, Kaiser truncated, Gaussian truncated).
By design: Portnoff windows (§10.7) — length-$M$ windows with $M > N$ whose $N$-sample sub-sampling is forced to be a single impulse by multiplying a longer smooth shape by a reference Nyquist(N) "template".
Not without modification: long Kaiser/Dolph-Chebyshev/raised-cosine windows of length $M > N$ — need Portnoff-style nyquistisation before they work. 天然滿足：長度 $\le N$ 的任意視窗（rect、Hann、Hamming、Blackman、Kaiser 截斷、Gauss 截斷）。
刻意設計：Portnoff 視窗（§10.7）— 長度 $M > N$，靠把平滑長形狀乘上 Nyquist(N) 模板，強制每 $N$ 取樣後只剩單一脈衝。
需額外處理：長度 $M > N$ 的 Kaiser、Dolph-Chebyshev、raised-cosine — 須先 Portnoff 化才可用。

Why "Nyquist(N)"?

為什麼叫「Nyquist(N)」？

A classical Nyquist filter (pulse shaping in communications) has the property that its uniformly sampled impulse response is a single non-zero impulse — so ISI vanishes. Our window has the same property with sub-sampling period $N$. The "(N)" specifies the sub-sampling rate. The terminology lines up with Oppenheim–Schafer and Vaidyanathan.

古典 Nyquist 濾波器（通訊脈衝整形）的脈衝響應在等距取樣下只剩單一脈衝，讓 ISI 消失。我們的視窗在子取樣週期 $N$ 下具同樣性質。"(N)" 指定子取樣率，與 Oppenheim–Schafer、Vaidyanathan 術語一致。

Nyquist(N) = perfect-reconstruction ticket for FBS. The time-domain statement is "kill the time aliasing"; the frequency-domain statement is "all frequency samples except DC are zero" for the sub-sampled window spectrum. §10.6 will prove that Nyquist(N) for FBS and COLA for OLA are the same theorem, on opposite sides of the Fourier mirror.

Nyquist(N) = FBS 完美重建的門票。時域版：「消滅時域混疊」；頻域版：「子取樣後的視窗頻譜除直流外全為零」。§10.6 會證：FBS 的 Nyquist(N) 與 OLA 的 COLA 是同一定理在傅立葉鏡像的兩面。
