Stage 10-5 · Nyquist(N) Windows關卡 10-5 · Nyquist(N) 視窗SASP §10.5 ↗

A Nyquist(N) window is one whose DTFT has zeros at every non-zero multiple of $2\pi/N$. Equivalently: every $N$-th time sample of the window (except at $n=0$) is zero. Every Nyquist(N) window is an FBS-valid prototype at hop $R=N$.

Nyquist(N) 視窗的 DTFT 在 $2\pi/N$ 所有非零倍數處為零。等價地：除 $n=0$ 外，每 $N$ 個時間樣本為零。Nyquist(N) 視窗就是跳距 $R=N$ 的 FBS 有效原型。
