Stage 10-6-2 · The Nyquist Property on the Unit Circle 關卡 10-6-2 · 單位圓上的 Nyquist 性質 SASP §10.6.2 ↗

Recast Nyquist($N$) as a zero-placement statement on the unit circle: the window's Z-transform $W(z)$ must have zeros at every non-zero $N$-th root of unity. This geometric view makes window design by zero-placement obvious, and it is the dialect Ch 12 (polyphase, paraunitary) uses throughout.

把 Nyquist($N$) 重述為單位圓上的零點放置：視窗的 Z 轉換 $W(z)$ 必須在所有非零 $N$ 次單位根處有零。這個幾何視角讓「零點設計」變得一目瞭然，也正是 Ch 12（polyphase、paraunitary）通篇採用的方言。

The statement, in $z$

用 $z$ 表示

NYQUIST(N) IN Z-DOMAIN
$$W(z)\bigg|_{z = e^{j 2\pi k/N}} = 0\quad\text{for every }k\in\{1,\ldots,N-1\}$$

The $N$-th roots of unity are $z_k = e^{j 2\pi k/N}$ for $k=0,\ldots,N-1$. Nyquist($N$) says $W$ is zero at all of them except $z_0 = 1$ (which corresponds to DC). Equivalently, $W(z)$ has the factor $\displaystyle \frac{1 - z^{-N}}{1 - z^{-1}}$ — the running-sum lowpass from §10.3.1 — as a building block.

$N$ 次單位根為 $z_k = e^{j 2\pi k/N}$，$k=0,\ldots,N-1$。Nyquist($N$) 即 $W$ 在這些根處全為零，只剩 $z_0 = 1$（對應直流）。等價地，$W(z)$ 含因式 $\displaystyle \frac{1 - z^{-N}}{1 - z^{-1}}$ — 正是 §10.3.1 的累計和低通 — 作為積木。

Why Nyquist(N) $\Rightarrow$ running-sum factor

為什麼 Nyquist($N$) $\Rightarrow$ 累計和因式

A polynomial (FIR) whose zeros include $\{e^{j 2\pi k/N} : k \ne 0\}$ contains the factor $\prod_{k=1}^{N-1}(1 - e^{j 2\pi k/N}z^{-1}) = \frac{1 - z^{-N}}{1 - z^{-1}}$. So:

若 FIR 多項式的零點包含 $\{e^{j 2\pi k/N} : k \ne 0\}$，則含因式 $\prod_{k=1}^{N-1}(1 - e^{j 2\pi k/N}z^{-1}) = \frac{1 - z^{-N}}{1 - z^{-1}}$。因此：

STRUCTURE THEOREM
$$W(z)\;=\;\frac{1 - z^{-N}}{1 - z^{-1}}\;\cdot\; Q(z)$$

where $Q(z)$ is any polynomial. The running-sum-LP factor provides the Nyquist zeros "for free", and $Q(z)$ carries the side-lobe-shaping freedom. Classical window design is then the art of choosing $Q(z)$.

$Q(z)$ 為任意多項式。累計和低通因式「免費」提供 Nyquist 零點；$Q(z)$ 承載塑形旁波瓣的自由度。古典視窗設計就是挑 $Q(z)$。

Examples

範例

Length-$N$ rectangle: $W(z) = \frac{1-z^{-N}}{1-z^{-1}}$ — so $Q(z) = 1$, the minimal design.
Length-$N$ Hann: linear combination $\frac{1}{2}\cdot \text{rect} - \frac{1}{4}\cdot(\text{rect shifted by }1) - \frac{1}{4}\cdot(\text{rect shifted by }-1)$ — still has the running-sum factor embedded.
Portnoff length $LN$ (§10.7): Nyquist-zeros are manually inserted by summing $L$ shifted copies of a shorter window; gives Nyquist($N$) on a longer window with extra design freedom. 長度 $N$ 矩形：$W(z) = \frac{1-z^{-N}}{1-z^{-1}}$ — 即 $Q(z) = 1$，最簡設計。
長度 $N$ Hann：線性組合 $\frac{1}{2}\cdot \text{rect} - \frac{1}{4}\cdot\text{rect}_{+1} - \frac{1}{4}\cdot\text{rect}_{-1}$ — 仍嵌有累計和因式。
長度 $LN$ 的 Portnoff（§10.7）：手動插入 Nyquist 零點 — 把短視窗的 $L$ 個位移副本加總 — 在更長視窗上保持 Nyquist($N$)，多出的自由度用於塑形。

Connection to perfect reconstruction in Ch 12

與 Ch 12 完美重建的連結

Multirate FB theory (Ch 12) requires the polyphase matrix of an FB to be paraunitary / PR. When the prototype is Nyquist($N$), the polyphase decomposition's DC row is a delta — the structure that lets the inverse bank be derived in closed form. So every paraunitary cosine-modulated FB (MDCT, MLT, MPEG) has Nyquist($N$) baked in.

多速率 FB 理論（Ch 12）要求 FB 的 polyphase 矩陣為 paraunitary / PR。當原型滿足 Nyquist($N$)，polyphase 分解的直流列為 delta — 這個結構讓逆組有閉式解。所以所有 paraunitary 餘弦調變 FB（MDCT、MLT、MPEG）都把 Nyquist($N$) 焊進去。

Nyquist($N$) = zeros on a lattice. Time-domain: sub-sampling collapses to a delta. Frequency-domain: a grid of $N$-point evaluations vanishes except at DC. Z-domain: zeros at every non-zero $N$-th root of unity. All three are geometrically the same picture on different axes of the Fourier cube.

Nyquist($N$) = 格點上的零。時域：子取樣塌成 delta。頻域：$N$ 點取值除直流外歸零。Z 域：每個非零 $N$ 次單位根都是零。三者在傅立葉立方體不同軸上是同一張幾何圖。
