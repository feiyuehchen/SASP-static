Stage 10-1-3 · FBS and Perfect Reconstruction關卡 10-1-3 · FBS 與完美重建SASP §10.1.3 ↗

Sum the outputs of all $N$ bandpass filters of a DFT filter bank at hop $R=1$ — you get the original signal back exactly. That is FBS perfect reconstruction.

把 DFT 濾波器組的 $N$ 個帶通輸出（$R=1$）全部相加，就得到原訊號。這就是 FBS 完美重建。
