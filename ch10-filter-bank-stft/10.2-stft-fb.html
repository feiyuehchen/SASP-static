Concretise the FB interpretation: write out the exact impulse response and frequency response of every channel, describe the standard block diagram (modulate → lowpass → downsample), compare the direct cost vs. the FFT shortcut, and lock in the vocabulary that §§10.3–10.9 will use.

Channel-$k$ filter, in two equivalent forms

第 $k$ 通道濾波器的兩種等價寫法

From §10.1.2, the STFT bin $k$ is the output of a filter whose impulse response is a modulated window. Two parametrisations are in common use:

§10.1.2 導出：STFT 第 $k$ 個 bin 是某個「調變視窗」濾波器的輸出。常見兩種參數化：

FORM A (CAUSAL, W · SINUSOID)
$$h_k[n]\;=\;w[n]\,e^{j\omega_k n},\qquad \omega_k\;=\;\frac{2\pi k}{N}$$
FORM B (NON-CAUSAL, TIME-REVERSED)
$$h_k[n]\;=\;w[-n]\,e^{j\omega_k n}$$

Form A is what a signal-processing engineer writes when implementing the FB causally — the window is the filter's impulse response directly. Form B is what falls out of the analysis formula $X_m[k]=\sum_n x[n]w[n-mR]e^{-j\omega_k n}$ if you want the output at time $mR$ to be literally $(x * h_k)[mR]$. They differ only by a time shift; the magnitude response is the same.

Form A 是工程師「直接把視窗當成濾波器脈衝響應」的因果寫法。Form B 是從分析公式 $X_m[k]=\sum_n x[n]w[n-mR]e^{-j\omega_k n}$ 倒推，要讓輸出在時刻 $mR$ 恰好等於 $(x * h_k)[mR]$ 時需要的非因果版。兩者只差一個時間位移；振幅響應完全一致。

Channel-$k$ frequency response

第 $k$ 通道頻率響應

H_k(ω)
$$H_k(\omega)\;=\;W(\omega - \omega_k)$$

Every channel is a frequency-shifted copy of the prototype lowpass $W(\omega)=\sum_n w[n]e^{-j\omega n}$. This is what makes the STFT a uniform modulated filter bank — same shape, equally spaced centres at $\omega_k=2\pi k/N$.

每個通道都是原型低通 $W(\omega)=\sum_n w[n]e^{-j\omega n}$ 的頻率位移版。這就讓 STFT 變成一個均勻調變濾波器組 — 同一形狀、在 $\omega_k=2\pi k/N$ 等距排列。

Standard block diagram

標準方塊圖

A production STFT FB is usually implemented as demodulate → lowpass → downsample:

產品級 STFT FB 通常實作為「先解調變 → 低通 → 降取樣」：

x[n] ─→ × e-jωkn ─→ w[n] (LP) ─→ ↓R ─→ Xm[k]

Flow: (1) multiply by $e^{-j\omega_k n}$ to slide band $k$ down to baseband. (2) window with $w[n]$ (lowpass). (3) downsample by $R$. Running one of these per $k$ gives every column of the STFT matrix. The sum-over-all-$k$ inverse (FBS) is covered in §10.1.3 and §10.4.

流程：(1) 乘上 $e^{-j\omega_k n}$ 把第 $k$ 頻帶滑到基頻。(2) 用 $w[n]$（低通）加窗。(3) 以 $R$ 降取樣。每個 $k$ 跑一套就得到 STFT 矩陣的所有行。相加逆變換（FBS）見 §10.1.3、§10.4。

Complexity — direct FB vs. FFT-based STFT

複雜度 — 直接 FB vs. FFT-based STFT

Both compute the same numbers; they just arrange the arithmetic differently:

兩者算出相同的數字，只是運算次序不同：

COST PER OUTPUT FRAME (length-N window)
Direct FB (Form A): $N$ channels × $N$-tap FIR = $O(N^2)$ complex multiplies.
FFT STFT: one $N$-point FFT = $O(N \log N)$ multiplies.
Speed-up: factor of $N/\log_2 N$ — e.g. ~100× at $N=1024$. 直接 FB（Form A）：$N$ 通道 × $N$ tap FIR = $O(N^2)$ 複數乘法。
FFT STFT：一次 $N$ 點 FFT = $O(N \log N)$ 乘法。
加速：$N/\log_2 N$ 倍 — $N=1024$ 時約 100×。

FB view ≠ FB implementation. Running $N$ independent FIRs is the slow brute force. In practice we always compute STFT with an FFT and think about the result through the FB lens when designing modifications. The FB view is a conceptual tool; the FFT is the arithmetic tool.

FB 詮釋 ≠ FB 實作。分別跑 $N$ 個 FIR 是蠻力慢法；實務上一律以 FFT 計算 STFT，而在「設計修改」時才用 FB 詮釋來思考。FB 是概念工具；FFT 是計算工具。
