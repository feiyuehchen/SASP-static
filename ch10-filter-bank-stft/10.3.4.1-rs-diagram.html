Stage 10-3-4-1 · System Diagram of the Running-Sum FB 關卡 10-3-4-1 · 累計和 FB 系統圖 SASP §10.3.4.1 ↗

Draw the signal-flow diagram that everyone in this chapter keeps referring to: $N$ parallel demodulate-sum-modulate channels. Seeing it as a picture makes the later polyphase reformulation (Ch 12) obvious.

畫出本章不斷提到的訊號流程圖：$N$ 條平行的「解調變 → 求和 → 再調變」通道。看過這張圖，之後 Ch 12 的 polyphase 改寫就順理成章。

The canonical diagram

標準系統圖

RUNNING-SUM FB (CHANNEL k) 
         ┌──────────────────┐   ┌─────────────┐   ┌──────────────┐
x[n] ──→ │ × e-j ωk n     │──→│ running sum │──→│ × ej ωk n     │──→ Xn[k]
         └──────────────────┘   └─────────────┘   └──────────────┘
          demodulate to DC        length-N LP      re-modulate back

One such pipeline per $k\in\{0,1,\ldots,N-1\}$, running in parallel. Together they form the uniform running-sum FB. Every other FB in this book replaces "running sum" with a shaped window; the demodulate and re-modulate blocks are identical.

每個 $k\in\{0,1,\ldots,N-1\}$ 各一條，全部平行運作。組起來就是均勻累計和 FB。本書其他 FB 只是把中間「累計和」換成更漂亮的視窗；前後的解調變、再調變方塊都一樣。

Block-by-block

逐方塊說明

Demodulate ($\times e^{-j\omega_k n}$): shifts band $k$ down to DC. Cheap: one complex multiply per sample per channel. Can be precomputed as a "twiddle" LUT.
Running sum (length $N$): the length-$N$ lowpass. Cumulative implementation $y[n]=y[n-1]+x[n]-x[n-N]$ costs one add/sub per sample, independent of $N$. But note: only useful if $N$ is modest; otherwise numerical drift requires periodic reset.
Re-modulate ($\times e^{+j\omega_k n}$): undoes the first step, puts the narrow-band signal back at its original centre. Needed if we later sum all channels to reconstruct $x$. 解調變（$\times e^{-j\omega_k n}$）：把第 $k$ 頻帶滑到直流。 便宜：每樣本每通道一次複乘，可事先以「twiddle」LUT 儲存。
累計和（長度 $N$）：長度 $N$ 的低通。遞迴實作 $y[n]=y[n-1]+x[n]-x[n-N]$，每樣本只需 一 次加/減，與 $N$ 無關。 注意：$N$ 太大時浮點漂移會累積，需要週期性歸零。
再調變（$\times e^{+j\omega_k n}$）：抵消第一步， 把窄頻訊號推回原本的中心頻率。若後面要把所有通道相加還原 $x$ 就必須。

What this picture buys us

這張圖帶來什麼好處

(1) Makes the FB interpretation of the DFT literal: each bin is a bandpass pipeline.
(2) Exposes the three places Ch 10 will optimise: the LP block (§10.4–10.6 replace rect with better windows), the demodulator spacing (§10.7 Portnoff), and the hop/downsampling placement (§10.8).
(3) Sets up the polyphase rearrangement (Ch 12) that slides the downsampler leftward of the LP for cheaper compute. (1) 把 DFT 的 FB 詮釋具體畫出：每個 bin 就是一條帶通流水線。
(2) 顯露 Ch 10 後面會優化的三處：LP 方塊（§10.4–10.6 把矩形換成更好的視窗）、解調變間距（§10.7 Portnoff）、降取樣位置（§10.8）。
(3) 為 Ch 12 的 polyphase 改寫鋪路：把降取樣滑到 LP 左側以省計算。

DFT FB = three blocks × $N$ copies. Once you can draw this picture with your eyes closed, everything else in Ch 10/11/12 is "which of these three blocks am I editing, and why?"

DFT FB = 三個方塊 × $N$ 份。閉眼能畫出此圖後，Ch 10/11/12 所有內容都變成「我在改這三個方塊中的哪一個？為什麼？」
