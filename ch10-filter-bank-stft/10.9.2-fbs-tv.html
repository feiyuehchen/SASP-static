Stage 10-9-2 · FBS Time-Varying Modifications 關卡 10-9-2 · FBS 時變修改 SASP §10.9.2 ↗

When $H_m[k]$ depends on $m$, the STFT pipeline implements a time-varying filter bank. Powerful — and risky: you can break PR, create aliasing, or generate "zipper noise" if modifications change too fast. This stage derives the safe-change rate and lists which effects respect it.

當 $H_m[k]$ 隨 $m$ 變，STFT 流水線成為時變濾波器組。威力強 — 但風險大：可能破壞 PR、產生混疊、或若修改變化太快就生成「zipper 雜音」。本節推導安全變化率並列出哪些效果守規矩。

Why time variation is dangerous

為何時變很危險

If $H_m[k]$ changes between frames $m$ and $m+1$, the windowed segments at those times are filtered differently. Overlap-add then combines mismatched pieces of the signal. Three failure modes:

若 $H_m[k]$ 在 frame $m$ 與 $m+1$ 間變化，兩時段加窗片段被以不同方式濾波，OLA 再把不匹配的段落疊起來。三種失敗模式：

Zipper noise: audible stepping when gains change discretely across frames. Fix: interpolate $H_m[k]$ smoothly (linear between frames, or crossfade in time).
Alias injection: frame boundaries in the modification create spectral images at multiples of the frame rate $f_s/R$. Fix: ensure the modification's own spectral bandwidth is $\ll f_s/R$.
PR loss: a sudden change in $H_m[k]$ at hop $m$ means the reconstruction no longer integrates to the input at that locality. Fix: crossfade $H$ over multiple frames. Zipper 雜音：增益跨 frame 離散變化可聽出階梯。解法：平滑內插 $H_m[k]$（frame 間線性、或時域 crossfade）。
混疊注入：修改本身的 frame 邊界產生「幀速率 $f_s/R$ 倍數」的頻譜影像。解法：確保修改的頻寬遠小於 $f_s/R$。
PR 喪失：$H_m[k]$ 在跳距 $m$ 突變，該處重建不再還原輸入。解法：在多個 frame 上 crossfade $H$。

The safe change rate

安全變化率

Rule of thumb: $H_m[k]$ should vary no faster than half the frame rate. If your hop is $R=N/2$, the frame rate is $2 f_s/N$, so the modification shouldn't change with bandwidth above $f_s/N$. For $f_s = 44100$, $N = 1024$: keep modification changes under $\sim 43$ Hz. Anything faster needs finer hop.

經驗法則：$H_m[k]$ 變化速率不應快過幀速率的一半。跳距 $R=N/2$ 時幀速率 $2 f_s/N$，故修改頻寬不得超過 $f_s/N$。以 $f_s = 44100$、$N = 1024$ 為例：修改變化須低於約 43 Hz；更快需要更小跳距。

Examples that respect the rule

守規矩的範例

Auto-tune / pitch shift: pitch tracking runs frame-by-frame; the modification is correctly band-limited.
Spectral de-noiser: noise estimate updates slowly (multi-frame moving average); safe.
Gated compressor: gain envelope is smoothed with a $\sim 10$ ms attack; safe when hop is $\le 5$ ms.
Reverb tail extension: a spectral mask that decays smoothly over many frames; safe. Auto-tune / 變調：音高追蹤逐 frame 執行；修改帶寬受限。
頻譜降噪：噪音估計緩慢更新（跨多 frame 移動平均）；安全。
門式壓縮器：增益包絡以 $\sim 10$ ms 起音平滑；跳距 $\le 5$ ms 時安全。
殘響尾延伸：頻譜遮罩跨多 frame 平滑衰減；安全。

Examples that violate the rule

違規範例

Per-frame transient gate (hard switch): instant $1 \to 0$ change produces artefacts. Fix: crossfade.
Frame-level pitch quantisation (hard snap): discrete pitch shifts per frame → zipper. Fix: target-pitch interpolation.
Spectral freeze with hard on/off: freezing one frame's spectrum and pasting it forward forever is audible if the transition is sudden. Fix: fade-in over several frames. 逐 frame 瞬態閘（硬開關）：$1 \to 0$ 瞬變產生瑕疵。解法：crossfade。
Frame 級音高量化（硬 snap）：逐 frame 離散變調 → zipper。解法：目標音高內插。
硬開/關頻譜凍結：凍結單 frame 頻譜並永遠貼上，若轉換突然會被聽出。解法：跨多 frame 淡入。

Keep modification bandwidth below half the frame rate. This is the DSP equivalent of "make your control signal continuous". Fast mod = small hop; slow mod = any hop. Design the mod shape and the STFT hop together.

修改頻寬要低於幀速率的一半。這是 DSP 版的「讓控制訊號連續」。快修改需小跳距；慢修改任意跳距皆可。修改形狀與 STFT 跳距要一起設計。
