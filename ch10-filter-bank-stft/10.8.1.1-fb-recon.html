Stage 10-8-1-1 · Filter-Bank Reconstruction at Hop $R$ 關卡 10-8-1-1 · 跳距 $R$ 的濾波器組重建 SASP §10.8.1.1 ↗

Derive the FBS reconstruction formula at general hop $R$, the $R>1$ generalisation of the $R=1$ result in §10.3.4.3. Expose where alias terms come from and confirm when they cancel.

推導一般跳距 $R$ 的 FBS 重建公式，是 §10.3.4.3 $R=1$ 結果的 $R>1$ 推廣。點出混疊項的來源，並確認它們何時相消。

Start with the per-channel output spectrum

從每通道輸出頻譜開始

Channel $k$'s downsampled output (before re-modulation) has spectrum

第 $k$ 通道降取樣後（未再調變）輸出頻譜為

$$Y_k(\omega)\;=\;\frac{1}{R}\sum_{\ell=0}^{R-1} W\!\left(\tfrac{\omega - 2\pi\ell}{R} - \omega_k\right)\,X\!\left(\tfrac{\omega - 2\pi\ell}{R}\right)$$

$\ell=0$ is the desired term, $\ell=1,\ldots,R-1$ are aliases.

$\ell=0$ 是目標項，$\ell=1,\ldots,R-1$ 是混疊。

Sum over channels with re-modulation

對所有通道再調變並求和

Multiply $Y_k$ by $e^{+j\omega_k n}$ and sum over $k$. The crucial identity:

把 $Y_k$ 乘上 $e^{+j\omega_k n}$ 並對 $k$ 求和。關鍵恆等式：

$$\sum_{k=0}^{N-1} e^{-j\omega_k (n - n_0)}\,\cdot\,\text{(anything not dependent on }k\text{)}\;=\;N\,\delta[(n - n_0)\bmod N]\,\cdot\,\text{(same)}$$

This collapses channel-sum into a Kronecker delta, leaving only the aliases that happen to fall on multiples of $N$ in frequency. Whether those leftover aliases cancel depends on the window's side-lobe behaviour at $\omega = 2\pi\ell/R$.

此恆等式把對 $k$ 的求和塌縮成 Kronecker delta，只留下頻率上恰好落在 $N$ 倍數的混疊項。這些殘餘是否相消，取決於視窗在 $\omega = 2\pi\ell/R$ 的旁波瓣行為。

Alias cancellation

混疊相消

SUFFICIENT CONDITION
$$W\!\left(\tfrac{2\pi\ell}{R}\right)\;=\;0\quad\text{for }\ell=1,2,\ldots,R-1$$

If the window's spectrum is zero at every non-zero multiple of $2\pi/R$, every alias term vanishes and reconstruction is perfect. This is exactly the Nyquist($R$) condition (§10.5) — same theorem, different hop. Short windows ($M\le R$) automatically satisfy it; longer windows need the COLA-product condition from §10.8.1.

若視窗頻譜在所有非零 $2\pi/R$ 倍數處為零，每個混疊項歸零、重建完美。這正是 Nyquist($R$) 條件（§10.5）— 同一條定理、不同跳距。短視窗（$M\le R$）自動滿足；長視窗須靠 §10.8.1 的 COLA-product 條件。

Putting it all together

總和

HOP-R FBS RECONSTRUCTION
$$\hat x[n]\;=\;\frac{1}{N\,w[0]}\sum_m w_s[n - mR]\,\sum_{k=0}^{N-1} X_m[k]\,e^{j 2\pi k (n - mR)/N}$$

For $w_s = 1$ you recover OLA; for $w_s = w$ you recover WOLA with $w^2$ COLA. The Nyquist($R$) assumption guarantees the inner sum over $k$ returns $N\,w[0]\,x[n]$ in the $\ell=0$ term, with aliases cancelling.

$w_s = 1$ 時得 OLA；$w_s = w$ 時得 WOLA（$w^2$ COLA）。Nyquist($R$) 假設確保對 $k$ 的內部求和在 $\ell=0$ 項給出 $N\,w[0]\,x[n]$，混疊相消。

Alias cancellation is the whole story at $R>1$. $R-1$ alias copies are generated by downsampling each channel, and the channel-sum cancels them when Nyquist($R$) holds. No cancellation, no PR.

$R>1$ 的全部故事就是混疊相消。每通道降取樣產生 $R-1$ 份混疊，通道求和在 Nyquist($R$) 成立時相消。無相消即無 PR。
