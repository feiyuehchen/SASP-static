Stage 10-6 · Duality of COLA and Nyquist(N) 關卡 10-6 · COLA 與 Nyquist(N) 的對偶 SASP §10.6 ↗

Prove the single most important structural statement of Ch 10: the OLA perfect-reconstruction condition (COLA on window, hop $R$) and the FBS perfect-reconstruction condition (Nyquist(N) on window, at DFT length $N$) are Poisson-summation duals of each other. Same theorem, two Fourier faces. Everything else in Ch 10/11/12 hangs from this.

證明 Ch 10 最重要的結構性敘述：OLA 完美重建條件（視窗 + 跳距 $R$ 滿足 COLA）與 FBS 完美重建條件（視窗 + DFT 長度 $N$ 滿足 Nyquist(N)）互為 Poisson 求和對偶。同一條定理、傅立葉兩面。Ch 10/11/12 之後都靠它。

State both conditions

兩條件並列

OLA · COLA @ HOP R
$$\sum_m w[n - mR]\;=\;\text{const in }n$$
FBS · NYQUIST(N)
$$w[pN]\;=\;w[0]\,\delta[p]$$

Poisson summation in one equation

Poisson 求和（一行表達）

PSF
$$\sum_m w[n - mR]\;=\;\frac{1}{R}\sum_k W\!\left(\tfrac{2\pi k}{R}\right)\,e^{j 2\pi k n/R}$$

The left side is the time-domain OLA sum; the right side is a Fourier series whose coefficients are the window spectrum $W(\cdot)$ sampled at frequencies $\omega=2\pi k/R$.

左邊是時域 OLA 和；右邊是傅立葉級數，係數為視窗頻譜 $W(\cdot)$ 在頻率 $\omega=2\pi k/R$ 的取樣值。

COLA ⇔ Nyquist(R)

COLA ⇔ Nyquist(R)

Apply PSF: the left side being "constant in $n$" means every coefficient of the Fourier series on the right is zero for $k\ne 0$. That is exactly:

套 PSF：左邊「對 $n$ 為定值」即右邊傅立葉級數 所有 $k\ne 0$ 的係數為零。也就是：

COLA (TIME) ⇔ W(2π k/R) = 0 for k≠0
$$\sum_m w[n - mR]=\text{const}\;\;\Longleftrightarrow\;\;W\!\left(\tfrac{2\pi k}{R}\right)=0\;\text{for every }k\ne 0$$

That right-side condition is literally "the window's spectrum sampled at every $R$-point grid vanishes except at DC" — the frequency-domain Nyquist condition with rate $R$. So COLA-at-hop-$R$ is exactly Nyquist($R$) on the window's spectrum. Swap the roles of time and frequency (i.e. transpose the whole argument), and Nyquist(N) on the window's time-domain samples turns into COLA-at-hop-$N$ on the window's spectrum.

右邊條件正是「視窗頻譜每 $R$ 點取樣、除直流外皆零」— 頻域版 Nyquist 條件，速率 $R$。所以「跳距 $R$ 的 COLA」恰等於視窗頻譜的 Nyquist($R$)。時頻互換（把論證整個轉置），視窗時域樣本的 Nyquist($N$) 就變成視窗頻譜跳距 $N$ 的 COLA。

一張總表

Each row is an equivalence (PSF). Each column is the PR condition for OLA (left) or FBS (right). The top-left and bottom-right are time-domain statements; top-right and bottom-left are frequency-domain.

每一列是等價（PSF）。每一欄是 OLA（左）或 FBS（右）的 PR 條件。左上、右下為時域；右上、左下為頻域。

PR is a single theorem with two names. Pick a window. At hop $R$, the question "does OLA reconstruct?" and "does FBS reconstruct (with $N=R$)?" have the same answer — because they are the same Poisson-summation statement. Designing a window for one automatically satisfies the other.

PR 是同一條定理的兩個名字。給定視窗，在跳距 $R$ 下「OLA 能否還原」與「$N=R$ 時 FBS 能否還原」答案相同 — 因為它們是同一個 Poisson 求和敘述。為其中一者設計的視窗自動滿足另一者。

實驗 · 視窗時域樣本 vs. 頻譜樣本

Top: time samples of $w[n]$, yellow dots at $n = pR$. Bottom: spectrum $|W(\omega)|$ in dB, yellow dots at $\omega = 2\pi k/R$. COLA at hop $R$ holds when bottom yellow dots (except the one at $\omega=0$) sit at $-\infty$ dB. Nyquist($R$) holds when top yellow dots (except $n=0$) sit at zero. The duality says these two statements are the same.

上：$w[n]$ 的時域樣本，黃點為 $n = pR$。下：頻譜 $|W(\omega)|$（dB），黃點為 $\omega = 2\pi k/R$。跳距 $R$ 的 COLA 成立 ⇔ 下方黃點（$\omega=0$ 以外）落在 $-\infty$ dB。Nyquist($R$) 成立 ⇔ 上方黃點（$n=0$ 以外）落在零。對偶性即「兩句話相同」。
