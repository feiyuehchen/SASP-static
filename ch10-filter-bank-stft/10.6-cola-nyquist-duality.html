Stage 10-6 · Duality of COLA and Nyquist Conditions關卡 10-6 · COLA 與 Nyquist 的對偶SASP §10.6 ↗

COLA (time-domain sum of shifted windows = constant) and Nyquist(N) (frequency-domain samples at $2\pi k/N$ are zero) are Fourier duals. A window is COLA at hop $R=N$ iff its transform is Nyquist(N).

COLA（平移視窗的時域和為常數）與 Nyquist(N)（頻域樣本在 $2\pi k/N$ 為零）互為傅立葉對偶。視窗在跳距 $R=N$ 下 COLA ⇔ 其轉換為 Nyquist(N)。
