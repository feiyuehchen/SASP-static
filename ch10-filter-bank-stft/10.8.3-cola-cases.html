Stage 10-8-3 · Constant-Overlap-Add (COLA) Cases 關卡 10-8-3 · COLA 案例 SASP §10.8.3 ↗

Three concrete worked examples — Hamming, periodic-Hamming via Poisson summation, and Kaiser — showing how to verify COLA at hop $R$ by hand. Pick the one matching your needs from §§10.8.3.1–10.8.3.3.

三個具體範例 — Hamming、以 Poisson 求和導出的週期 Hamming、Kaiser — 展示如何手算驗證跳距 $R$ 的 COLA。§10.8.3.1–10.8.3.3 三小節各走一種。

The three cases at a glance

三個案例概覽

§10.8.3.1 Hamming OLA. Directly sum $\sum_m \text{Hamming}[n - mR]$ at $R=M/2$; show the result is $0.54 M/2$ plus a $\sim 1\%$ ripple. Quantify the ripple and discuss when it's audible.
§10.8.3.2 Periodic-Hamming OLA from PSF. Use Poisson summation to convert the time-domain sum into a frequency-domain sampling check: only the DC Fourier coefficient is non-zero for the periodic Hamming. Cleanest proof in the book.
§10.8.3.3 Kaiser OLA. Kaiser's COLA is approximate; compute the ripple as a function of $\beta$ and $R/M$ and pick a hop that keeps it below your tolerance. §10.8.3.1 Hamming OLA。直接求 $\sum_m \text{Hamming}[n - mR]$，取 $R=M/2$；結果為 $0.54 M/2$ 加 $\sim 1\%$ 漣波。量化漣波並討論何時可聽。
§10.8.3.2 由 PSF 導出週期 Hamming OLA。用 Poisson 求和把時域總和轉為頻域取樣檢查：週期 Hamming 只有直流 Fourier 係數非零。本書最乾淨的證明。
§10.8.3.3 Kaiser OLA。Kaiser 的 COLA 為近似；以 $\beta$ 與 $R/M$ 計算漣波，挑選能低於容忍度的跳距。

Why three cases, not one?

為何要講三個而非一個？

Each case illustrates a different proof technique that generalises beyond the specific window:

每個案例示範一種可推廣的證明技巧：

Direct sum (Hamming): works for any finite-support window where $R$ divides $M$ — brute algebra, no cleverness. Use when you have a specific window and want to quantify the residual ripple.
PSF (Periodic Hamming): works for any periodic window. Converts time-domain sum → frequency-domain samples. Most theoretical.
Kaiser (approximate): demonstrates how to handle windows where strict COLA is impossible but ripple is acceptably small — the template for "engineering COLA" in modern codecs. 直接求和（Hamming）：適用於任何有限支撐視窗且 $R$ 整除 $M$ — 純代數暴力，無巧勁。用於特定視窗要量化殘漣波時。
PSF（週期 Hamming）：適用於任何週期視窗。把時域總和轉為頻域取樣。最具理論深度。
Kaiser（近似）：展示嚴格 COLA 不可行、但漣波可接受的情境 — 現代編解碼「工程版 COLA」的模板。

COLA is rarely exact, almost always good enough. Read §10.8.3.1 for bare-hands work, §10.8.3.2 for the elegant PSF framing, §10.8.3.3 for engineering trade-offs. All three return the same conclusion: at $R=M/2$, Hamming / Kaiser / Blackman-Harris give ripple well below audible.

COLA 很少嚴格成立，幾乎總是「夠好」。§10.8.3.1 看暴力算；§10.8.3.2 看優雅 PSF；§10.8.3.3 看工程取捨。三者殊途同歸：$R=M/2$ 下 Hamming / Kaiser / Blackman-Harris 的漣波遠低於可聽閾值。
