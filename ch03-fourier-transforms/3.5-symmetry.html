Stage 3-5 · Symmetry for Real Signals 關卡 3-5 · 實訊號的對稱性 SASP §3.5 ↗

Every real-valued signal has a spectrum with Hermitian (conjugate) symmetry. That is why we only ever plot the positive half.

每個實值訊號的頻譜都具有 Hermitian（共軛）對稱性 — 這就是為什麼我們只畫正半頻率就夠了。

HERMITIAN SYMMETRY
$$x[n]\in\mathbb{R}\;\;\Longrightarrow\;\;X(-\omega)\;=\;X^{*}(\omega)$$

The proof is again one line. If $x[n]$ is real, then in the DTFT $X(\omega)=\sum x[n] e^{-j\omega n}$, taking the conjugate flips the sign of $\omega$. So $X^*(\omega)=\sum x[n] e^{j\omega n}=X(-\omega)$.

證明也只有一行。$x[n]$ 為實時，$X(\omega)=\sum x[n]e^{-j\omega n}$ 取共軛只是把 $\omega$ 的符號翻掉。 所以 $X^*(\omega)=\sum x[n]e^{j\omega n}=X(-\omega)$。

Three equivalent statements

三種等價說法

  • Magnitude is even: $|X(-\omega)|=|X(\omega)|$.
  • 振幅為偶函數：$|X(-\omega)|=|X(\omega)|$。
  • Phase is odd: $\angle X(-\omega)=-\angle X(\omega)$.
  • 相位為奇函數：$\angle X(-\omega)=-\angle X(\omega)$。
  • Real part is even, imaginary part is odd.
  • 實部為偶、虛部為奇。

Practical consequence: one side of the spectrum carries all the information. DFT spectrum plots of audio always show $\omega\in[0,\pi]$, not $[-\pi,\pi]$ — the negative half is just the mirror image.

實務後果：頻譜一邊就夠了。音訊 DFT 頻譜圖都只畫 $\omega\in[0,\pi]$， 不畫 $[-\pi,\pi]$ — 負半頻譜就是鏡像，沒新資訊。
