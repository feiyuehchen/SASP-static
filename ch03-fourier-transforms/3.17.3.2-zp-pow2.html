Stage 3-17-3-2 · Zero-Pad to Next Power of 2 關卡 3-17-3-2 · 補零至下個 2 的冪 SASP §3.17.3.2 ↗

Radix-2 FFTs run in $O(M\log M)$ only when $M$ is a power of 2. Picking the next power of 2 above $N$ is the cheapest way to get fast spectra.

Radix-2 FFT 只有在 $M$ 是 2 的冪次時才達到 $O(M\log M)$。把 $M$ 取在 $N$ 之上、最接近的 2 次冪是最便宜的快速頻譜方式。

Modern FFT libraries (FFTW, PocketFFT, cuFFT, etc.) handle arbitrary composite sizes efficiently — for them, a power of 2 is not mandatory, but powers of 2 (or 5-smooth numbers) are usually still the fastest. If you write your own radix-2 from scratch, padding to a power of 2 is essentially required.

現代 FFT 函式庫（FFTW、PocketFFT、cuFFT 等）對任意合數長度都很快 — 不強制 2 次冪，但 2 次冪（或 5-smooth 數）通常最快。若你自己實作 radix-2，補到 2 次冪幾乎必要。

PICK M
$$M\;=\;2^{\lceil\log_2 N\rceil}$$

For smoother spectral displays, bump up by one or more powers: $M=2^{\lceil\log_2 N\rceil + k}$ with $k\in\{1,2,3\}$ for $2\times$, $4\times$, $8\times$ interpolation.

若想讓頻譜圖更平滑，再往上取幾個 2 次冪：$M=2^{\lceil\log_2 N\rceil + k}$，$k\in\{1,2,3\}$ 分別對應 $2\times$、$4\times$、$8\times$ 內插。

Bumping $M$ from $N$ to $2N$ costs about twice the FFT time; going to $8N$ costs about 11× (because $M\log M$). Check your real-time budget before defaulting to high padding.

$M$ 從 $N$ 拉到 $2N$ 時 FFT 約慢 2 倍；$8N$ 慢約 11 倍（$M\log M$）。預設開大補零前先看即時預算。
