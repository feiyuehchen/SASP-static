Stage 3-16-2 · Spectral Roll-Off 關卡 3-16-2 · 頻譜滾降 SASP §3.16.2 ↗

How quickly a spectrum falls at high frequencies tells you how smooth the signal is in time.

頻譜在高頻衰減得多快，直接對應訊號在時域有多平滑。

The rule

規則

  • Signal with a jump discontinuity → $|X|\sim 1/|\Omega|$ (e.g. a rectangle pulse).
  • 訊號有跳變不連續 → $|X|\sim 1/|\Omega|$（例如矩形脈衝）。
  • Signal continuous but has kinks (1st derivative jumps) → $|X|\sim 1/|\Omega|^2$ (triangular pulse).
  • 訊號連續但一階導有跳變（折點）→ $|X|\sim 1/|\Omega|^2$（三角脈衝）。
  • Signal has $k$ continuous derivatives and the $k+1$-th is finite → $|X|\sim 1/|\Omega|^{k+2}$.
  • 訊號到第 $k$ 階導都連續、第 $k+1$ 階有界 → $|X|\sim 1/|\Omega|^{k+2}$。
  • Infinitely smooth (e.g. Gaussian) → decay faster than any polynomial (exponential roll-off).
  • 無限平滑（例如高斯）→ 比任何多項式還快（指數衰減）。

Consequence for audio: hard clipping creates jumps → audible broadband distortion. Soft limiting smooths the edges → distortion harmonics fall faster → sounds less harsh.

對音訊的後果：硬截波（hard clip）製造跳變 → 全頻帶可聽失真。 軟限幅（soft limit）把邊緣磨平 → 失真諧波衰減更快 → 聽起來比較不刺耳。
