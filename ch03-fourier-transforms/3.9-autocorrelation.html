Stage 3-9 · Autocorrelation 關卡 3-9 · 自相關 SASP §3.9 ↗

Autocorrelation is cross-correlation of a signal with itself. Its DTFT is the power spectrum.

自相關就是訊號與自己做互相關。它的 DTFT 就是功率頻譜。

AUTOCORRELATION
$$r_{xx}[k]\;=\;\sum_{n} x[n]\,x[n+k]$$
↔
$$r_{xx}[k]\;\longleftrightarrow\;|X(\omega)|^{2}\;\triangleq\;S_{xx}(\omega)$$

$|X(\omega)|^2$ is the power spectrum. Three consequences:

$|X(\omega)|^2$ 就是功率頻譜。三個直接後果：

  • $r_{xx}[0]=\sum|x[n]|^2$ = total energy.
  • $r_{xx}[0]=\sum|x[n]|^2$ = 總能量。
  • $r_{xx}$ is even and has its maximum at $k=0$ (Schwarz inequality).
  • $r_{xx}$ 是偶函數，且最大值在 $k=0$（Schwarz 不等式）。
  • $S_{xx}(\omega)\ge 0$ everywhere — power spectra cannot go negative.
  • $S_{xx}(\omega)\ge 0$ 處處成立 — 功率頻譜不會為負。

Why it is useful

為什麼好用

Autocorrelation is blind to phase. Two signals with the same $|X|$ and arbitrary phase have the same $r_{xx}$. This is why we use power spectra to describe random signals: phase is meaningless for noise. It also makes autocorrelation a natural tool for pitch estimation — periodicities in $x$ show up as peaks in $r_{xx}$.

自相關無視相位。兩個 $|X|$ 相同但相位任意的訊號，$r_{xx}$ 完全一樣。 這就是為什麼描述隨機訊號都用功率頻譜 — 雜訊的相位沒有意義。 也因此自相關天生適合做基頻估計：$x$ 中的週期性在 $r_{xx}$ 上形成尖峰。
