Cross-correlation in time becomes one spectrum conjugated and multiplied by the other.

時域的互相關 ↔ 一個頻譜共軛後乘上另一個頻譜。

CROSS-CORRELATION
$$r_{xy}[k]\;=\;\sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty} x[n]\,y[n+k]$$
THEOREM
$$r_{xy}[k]\;\longleftrightarrow\;X^{*}(\omega)\,Y(\omega)$$

Cross-correlation is like convolution but without flipping the second signal. Its spectrum differs from the convolution spectrum by one complex conjugate — at DC (and for real signals) the two are often indistinguishable in magnitude. This is why correlation is used as a matched filter for pattern-matching.

互相關 = 不翻轉的卷積。它的頻譜和卷積頻譜只差一個複共軛 — 在 DC（或實訊號）上振幅往往看不出差別。這就是為什麼相關法常被拿來當 匹配濾波器， 用來偵測已知訊號模板。

Sanity check — two special cases

健全性檢查 — 兩個特例

  • If $y=x$, $r_{xx}$ is autocorrelation (next page) and its DTFT is $|X(\omega)|^2$ — a non-negative real spectrum.
  • 若 $y=x$，$r_{xx}$ 是自相關（下一頁），DTFT 為 $|X(\omega)|^2$ — 處處非負的實頻譜。
  • If $y$ is an uncorrelated signal, $r_{xy}[k]$ stays close to zero for all lags; $|X^{*}Y|$ is close to zero on average.
  • 若 $y$ 與 $x$ 無相關，$r_{xy}[k]$ 對所有位移 $k$ 都接近 0；$|X^{*}Y|$ 平均也接近 0。
