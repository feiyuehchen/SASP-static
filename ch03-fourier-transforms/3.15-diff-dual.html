Stage 3-15 · Differentiation Theorem Dual 關卡 3-15 · 微分定理對偶 SASP §3.15 ↗

Multiplying a signal by $n$ (weighting by time) corresponds in frequency to differentiating the spectrum.

訊號乘上 $n$（以時間加權）等於頻域對頻譜微分。

MULTIPLY BY n ↔ d/dω
$$n\,x[n]\;\longleftrightarrow\;j\,\frac{d X(\omega)}{d\omega}$$

Proof: differentiate $X(\omega)=\sum x[n]\,e^{-j\omega n}$ with respect to $\omega$; each term picks up $-j n$. Rearranging gives the pair. This is the dual of the continuous-time differentiation theorem (differentiating $x(t)$ multiplies the spectrum by $j\Omega$).

證明：對 $X(\omega)=\sum x[n]\,e^{-j\omega n}$ 逐項對 $\omega$ 微分，每項多出 $-jn$。 重排即上式。這是連續時間「時域微分 ↔ 頻域乘 $j\Omega$」的對偶版。

Three everyday uses

三個實用場合

1. Group-delay estimation. $-d\angle X/d\omega$ gives group delay. To compute it without unwrapping phase, use $\operatorname{Re}\{j\,X'/X\}/$nothing... — see §11.5.
2. Moments from char functions. Statistical moments are derivatives of the characteristic function at $\omega=0$.
3. Phase-vocoder interpolation. Frame-to-frame phase prediction uses this dual to interpolate $\omega$-derivative of phase. 1. 群延遲估計。$-d\angle X/d\omega$ 即群延遲。無須解包相位的算法要用 $j\,X'/X$ — 見 §11.5。
2. 由特徵函數求矩。統計矩等於特徵函數在 $\omega=0$ 的各階導數。
3. 相位聲碼器內插。frame 間相位預測靠此對偶內插相位的 $\omega$ 導數。

Multiply ↔ differentiate. One of the two "multiplication in a domain = differentiation in the other" pairs (the other is the continuous differentiation theorem itself). Memorise the sign: $n\to j\,d/d\omega$ (not $-j$).

相乘 ↔ 微分。「一邊相乘 = 另一邊微分」的兩對之一（另一對是連續時間微分定理）。記住符號：$n\to j\,d/d\omega$（不是 $-j$）。

✦ Mini-Boss ✦

✦ 關底小魔王 ✦

Q. $X(\omega)$ is perfectly flat (constant). What does $n\cdot x[n]$ transform to?

問：若 $X(\omega)$ 為常數（平直頻譜），$n\cdot x[n]$ 的 DTFT 是？

Lab · Multiply by n, watch spectrum differentiate

實驗 · 乘上 n，看頻譜被微分

Top: the (possibly weighted) time signal. Bottom: its DTFT magnitude. The Gaussian's DTFT is another Gaussian; weighting by $n$ differentiates it (first derivative has a zero at DC); weighting by $n^2$ is the second derivative (dip at DC).

上：（可能加權後的）時域訊號。下：其 DTFT 振幅。高斯的 DTFT 仍為高斯；乘 $n$ 即微分（直流處變為零）；乘 $n^2$ 為二次微分（直流處凹陷）。
