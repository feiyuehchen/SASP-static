Stage 3-15 · Differentiation Theorem Dual 關卡 3-15 · 微分定理對偶 SASP §3.15 ↗

Multiplying a signal by $n$ (weighting by time) corresponds in frequency to differentiating.

訊號乘上 $n$（以時間加權）對應到頻域的微分。

MULTIPLY-BY-n ↔ d/dω
$$n\,x[n]\;\longleftrightarrow\;j\,\frac{d X(\omega)}{d\omega}$$

Proof: differentiate $X(\omega)=\sum x[n]\,e^{-j\omega n}$ with respect to $\omega$. Each term picks up a factor $-j n$. Rearranging gives the stated pair. This is the dual of the continuous-time differentiation theorem (differentiating $x(t)$ multiplies the spectrum by $j\Omega$).

證明：對 $X(\omega)=\sum x[n]\,e^{-j\omega n}$ 逐項對 $\omega$ 微分，每項多出 $-jn$。重排一下就得上式。 這是連續時間「時域微分 ↔ 頻域乘 $j\Omega$」的對偶版。

Uses

應用

  • Computing moments of a distribution from derivatives of its characteristic function.
  • 用特徵函數的導數計算分布的動差（統計訊號處理常用）。
  • Cepstral analysis — taking log of $X(\omega)$ and differentiating lets you estimate group delay and formant locations.
  • 倒頻譜分析 — 對 log $X(\omega)$ 微分可估計群延遲與共振峰位置。
  • Phase-vocoder algorithms often use this dual to interpolate frame phases.
  • 相位 vocoder 常藉由此對偶來內插 frame 的相位。
