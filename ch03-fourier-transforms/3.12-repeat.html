Stage 3-12 · Repeat (Scaling) Operator 關卡 3-12 · 重複 (縮放) 算子 SASP §3.12 ↗

Repeating means tiling copies of a function along its own axis. It is the dual of stretching (§3.11) and the direct precursor to sampling on the other side.

重複 = 沿軸把函數一份一份鋪開，是拉伸（§3.11）的對偶，也是另一邊取樣的直接前身。

REPEAT BY L (TIME)
$$\operatorname{Repeat}_L\{x\}[n]\;=\;\sum_{m=-\infty}^{\infty} x[n-m L]$$

A length-$N$ signal $x$ repeated by $L=N$ produces an infinite periodic signal of period $N$. Its DTFT collapses onto the $N$-point frequency grid:

長度 $N$ 的 $x$ 以 $L=N$ 重複產生週期 $N$ 的無限序列。其 DTFT 塌到 $N$ 點頻率網格上：

REPEAT-L ↔ SAMPLE-L
$$\operatorname{Repeat}_L\{x\}\;\longleftrightarrow\;\frac{2\pi}{L}\sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} X\!\left(\tfrac{2\pi k}{L}\right)\,\delta\!\left(\omega - \tfrac{2\pi k}{L}\right)$$

On the unit circle this says: pick up the values of $X(\omega)$ only at $\omega=2\pi k/L$ — the DFT bins — and zero elsewhere. This is why the DFT of a length-$N$ signal equals $N$ samples of its DTFT.

單位圓上只取 $X(\omega)$ 在 $\omega=2\pi k/L$（DFT bin）的值、其餘為零。 這就是為什麼長度 $N$ 訊號的 DFT 等於它 DTFT 的 $N$ 點取樣。

Proof sketch

證明要點

Periodic extension $\tilde x[n] = \sum_m x[n-mL]$ is the convolution of $x$ with a Dirac comb $\sum_m \delta[n-mL]$. Convolution in time = multiplication in frequency (§3.7). The comb's DTFT is another comb at spacing $2\pi/L$. Multiplying $X(\omega)$ by this comb samples $X$ at those frequencies.

週期延拓 $\tilde x[n] = \sum_m x[n-mL]$ 等於 $x$ 與 Dirac 梳 $\sum_m \delta[n-mL]$ 的卷積。 時域卷積 = 頻域相乘（§3.7）。梳的 DTFT 仍是梳、間距 $2\pi/L$。 把 $X(\omega)$ 乘上此梳，即得到 $X$ 在那些頻率的取樣。

Sampling ↔ Repeating. The two most important operations in multirate DSP are Fourier-dual. If you see "sampling" in one domain, write "repeating" in the other without thinking.

取樣 ↔ 重複。多速率 DSP 裡最重要的兩個操作互為 Fourier 對偶。一邊看到「取樣」，另一邊直接寫「重複」— 不用多想。
