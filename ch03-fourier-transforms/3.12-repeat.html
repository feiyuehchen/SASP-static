Stage 3-12 · Repeat (Scaling) Operator 關卡 3-12 · 重複 (縮放) 算子 SASP §3.12 ↗

Repeating means tiling copies of a function along its own axis. It is the dual of stretching.

重複 = 沿軸把函數一份一份鋪開，是拉伸的對偶操作。

REPEAT BY L (TIME)
$$\operatorname{Repeat}_L\{x\}[n]\;=\;\sum_{m=-\infty}^{\infty} x[n-m L']$$

In the frequency domain the same operator tiles $L$ copies of $X(\omega)$ along the frequency axis every $2\pi/L$. Since $X(\omega)$ is already $2\pi$-periodic, this just collapses it down to a sampled version on $\omega=\tfrac{2\pi}{L}k$ — giving us the DFT.

在頻率域上，同一個算子把 $X(\omega)$ 每 $2\pi/L$ 就鋪一份，總共 $L$ 份。 由於 $X(\omega)$ 本來就是 $2\pi$-週期，重複之後就塌成在 $\omega=\tfrac{2\pi}{L}k$ 上取樣的版本 — 那正是 DFT。

Sampling in one domain is repeating in the other. A train of impulses in time has a train of impulses in frequency — this is the Poisson summation formula, which shows up in Chapter 9.

一邊取樣 = 另一邊重複。時域的脈衝列對應頻域的脈衝列 — 這就是 Poisson 求和公式，第 9 世界會用到。
