Stage 3-17-2 · Interpolating a DFT 關卡 3-17-2 · DFT 的內插 SASP §3.17.2 ↗

Give me $X[k]$ for $k=0,\ldots,N-1$. I want the DTFT value at an arbitrary $\omega$.

給我 $X[k]$（$k=0,\ldots,N-1$）。我要任意 $\omega$ 處的 DTFT 值。

WEIGHTED SUM OF BINS
$$X(\omega)\;=\;\sum_{k=0}^{N-1} X[k]\,g_k(\omega)$$

The weights $g_k(\omega)$ are shifted Dirichlet kernels centred at each bin, with a linear-phase correction that accounts for the mismatch between "centred" and "causal" indexing conventions. The important takeaway is that the interpolation is exact — no information is lost going from the $N$ DFT samples to any intermediate frequency, as long as the original sequence was indeed length $N$.

權重 $g_k(\omega)$ 是以每個 bin 為中心的 Dirichlet 核，再加上一個線性相位修正項 （對應「中心化」與「因果」兩種索引慣例的差別）。重點是：內插是精確的 — 只要原序列真的是長度 $N$，從 $N$ 個 DFT 樣本到任何中間頻率都不會丟資訊。

In practice

實務作法

Two common shortcuts avoid computing Dirichlet kernels by hand:

兩種常見捷徑可以免手算 Dirichlet 核：

  • Zero pad in time → FFT at size $M\gg N$ → the new bins already lie between the old ones.
  • 時域補零 → 在長度 $M\gg N$ 做 FFT → 新 bin 就落在舊 bin 之間。
  • Chirp-z transform (or Goertzel) — evaluate $X(\omega)$ directly at a handful of chosen frequencies without gridding the whole circle.
  • Chirp-z 轉換（或 Goertzel）— 直接在若干選定頻率算 $X(\omega)$，不必把整個圓都計算。
