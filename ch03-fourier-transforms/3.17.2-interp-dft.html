Stage 3-17-2 · Interpolating a DFT 關卡 3-17-2 · DFT 的內插 SASP §3.17.2 ↗

Given $X[k]$ for $k=0,\ldots,N-1$, compute the DTFT $X(\omega)$ at an arbitrary $\omega$ — exactly, not approximately.

給 $X[k]$（$k=0,\ldots,N-1$），在任意 $\omega$ 精確計算 DTFT $X(\omega)$ — 不是近似、是精確。

WEIGHTED SUM OF BINS
$$X(\omega)\;=\;\sum_{k=0}^{N-1} X[k]\,g_k(\omega)$$

The weights $g_k(\omega)$ are shifted Dirichlet kernels centred at each bin, with a linear-phase correction that accounts for "centred" vs. "causal" indexing conventions. The takeaway: interpolation is exact — no information is lost going from the $N$ DFT samples to any intermediate frequency, as long as the original sequence was indeed length $N$.

權重 $g_k(\omega)$ 是以每個 bin 為中心的 Dirichlet 核，加上線性相位修正（對應「中心化」vs.「因果」索引慣例）。 結論：內插是精確的 — 只要原序列真的是長度 $N$，從 $N$ 個 DFT 樣本到任意中間頻率都不會丟資訊。

Two shortcuts

兩種捷徑

Zero-pad in time → FFT at $M\gg N$ → new bins already lie between the old ones. Cheap if you want all frequencies densely.
Chirp-z transform / Goertzel → evaluate $X(\omega)$ directly at a handful of chosen frequencies without griding the whole circle. Cheap if you want a few specific frequencies. 時域補零 → 長度 $M\gg N$ 做 FFT → 新 bin 落在舊 bin 之間。想要所有頻率密集取樣時最便宜。
Chirp-z 轉換 / Goertzel → 直接在少數選定頻率算 $X(\omega)$，不必把整個圓都算。想要少量指定頻率時最便宜。

DFT bins are a sub-sample of the DTFT. Not an approximation — a down-sample. The missing values between bins can always be recovered via Dirichlet-weighted sums (or equivalently, zero-padding).

DFT bin 是 DTFT 的子取樣。不是近似，是降取樣。bin 之間遺漏的值永遠可以用 Dirichlet 權重和（或等價地，補零）還原。
