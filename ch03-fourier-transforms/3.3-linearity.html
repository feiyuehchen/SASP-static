Stage 3-3 · Linearity of the DTFT 關卡 3-3 · DTFT 的線性 SASP §3.3 ↗

Show that the DTFT respects scaling and addition: build the spectrum of a mixed sound from the spectra of its parts.

證明 DTFT 對縮放與加法都是「老實人」：混合聲音的頻譜，等於每段頻譜各自加總。

The statement

定理本身

LINEARITY
$$\text{DTFT}\{\alpha x[n]+\beta y[n]\}\;=\;\alpha\,X(\omega)+\beta\,Y(\omega)$$

It is a one-line consequence of the sum being a linear operator. $\sum_n (\alpha x_n + \beta y_n) e^{-j\omega n} = \alpha \sum_n x_n e^{-j\omega n} + \beta \sum_n y_n e^{-j\omega n}$. Done.

因為求和本身就是線性算子： $\sum_n(\alpha x_n+\beta y_n)e^{-j\omega n} =\alpha\sum_n x_n e^{-j\omega n}+\beta\sum_n y_n e^{-j\omega n}$。 證完。

Why we care

為什麼這條定理重要

Almost everything that follows leans on linearity:

後面幾乎所有東西都靠這條：

  • Decompose a signal into partials; DTFT each one separately, then sum.
  • 把訊號拆成partials， 各自取 DTFT 再相加。
  • Multiply each spectrum by a different filter response and recombine in the time domain — overlap-add is exactly this.
  • 不同頻譜各自乘上不同濾波器響應，再回到時域相加 — 重疊相加（OLA）就是這件事。
  • Cross-synthesis, additive synthesis, sines+noise+transients — all assume the spectrum of a sum is the sum of spectra.
  • 交叉合成、加法合成、sines+noise+transients — 全都建立在「和的頻譜 = 頻譜之和」上。

Magnitude is NOT linear in general: $|X+Y|\le|X|+|Y|$ (triangle inequality), with equality only when $X$ and $Y$ point in the same complex direction. Adding signals of equal amplitude but opposite phase can leave perfect silence — that is destructive interference, perfectly explained by linearity of the complex spectra.

注意：振幅本身不線性 — $|X+Y|\le|X|+|Y|$（三角不等式）， 等號只在 $X$、$Y$ 複數方向相同時成立。等振幅但反相位的訊號相加可以是完全靜音 — 這個破壞性干涉，正好可以由「複頻譜的線性」完美解釋。

實驗 · α·x + β·y 在時域與頻域

The orange curve is $|\alpha X(\omega)+\beta Y(\omega)|$ computed straight from the mixed time signal. The dashed yellow line is $|\alpha X(\omega)|+|\beta Y(\omega)|$ computed from the parts separately, then added in the spectrum. They agree everywhere — that is linearity.

橙色曲線是直接對混合訊號取 DTFT 後的 $|\alpha X(\omega)+\beta Y(\omega)|$。 黃色虛線是分別算 $|\alpha X|$ 與 $|\beta Y|$ 後在頻域相加。兩者重合 — 這就是線性。 試著把 θy 拉到 180° 並讓兩個頻率相同：可以看見破壞性干涉。
