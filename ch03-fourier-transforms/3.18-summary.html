Concepts to keep

要帶走的觀念

  • Real signals have Hermitian-symmetric spectra; magnitude is even, phase is odd.
  • 實訊號頻譜 Hermitian 對稱：振幅偶、相位奇。
  • Shift adds linear phase; scaling in time scales frequency inversely.
  • 位移 = 加線性相位；時間縮放 = 頻率反向縮放。
  • Convolution in one domain is multiplication in the other. This one fact runs the rest of the book.
  • 一邊卷積 = 另一邊相乘。這條走遍全書。
  • Downsampling folds; always low-pass first.
  • 降取樣會折疊；先低通。
  • Zero padding interpolates the display but does not improve resolution.
  • 補零讓顯示變密但解析度不變。
