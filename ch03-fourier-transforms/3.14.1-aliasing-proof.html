Stage 3-14-1 · Proof of the Aliasing Theorem 關卡 3-14-1 · 混疊定理的證明 SASP §3.14.1 ↗

Derive $Y(\omega)=\tfrac{1}{M}\sum_{m=0}^{M-1} X((\omega-2\pi m)/M)$ from the definition $y[n]=x[nM]$ — four clean steps, no hand-waving.

從定義 $y[n]=x[nM]$ 出發，以四步乾淨推出 $Y(\omega)=\tfrac{1}{M}\sum_{m=0}^{M-1} X((\omega-2\pi m)/M)$ — 無跳步、無含糊。

Step 1 · Insert a sampling comb

第 1 步 · 插入取樣梳狀

Define $\tilde{x}[n] = x[n]\cdot p[n]$ where $p[n]=1$ if $n$ is a multiple of $M$, else $0$. This keeps the target samples in place and zeros everything else. Downsampling is then: compress the time axis so the remaining samples sit on consecutive integers.

定義 $\tilde{x}[n] = x[n]\cdot p[n]$，其中 $p[n]=1$（$n$ 為 $M$ 倍數）、否則 $0$。 這把目標樣本保留原位、其他歸零。降取樣即：壓縮時間軸，讓剩下的樣本佔連續整數。

Step 2 · Fourier-expand the comb

第 2 步 · 對梳狀做 Fourier 展開

$$p[n]\;=\;\frac{1}{M}\sum_{m=0}^{M-1} e^{j 2\pi m n / M}$$

This is the finite Fourier series of an impulse train at spacing $M$. Each term is a complex sinusoid; its DTFT is a single delta on the unit circle at $\omega=2\pi m/M$.

這是間距 $M$ 脈衝串的有限 Fourier 級數。每項是複正弦；其 DTFT 為單位圓上 $\omega=2\pi m/M$ 的 delta。

Step 3 · Multiplication ↔ convolution

第 3 步 · 乘法 ↔ 卷積

Multiplying $x[n]$ by $p[n]$ is convolution in frequency. Since $p$'s DTFT is a delta comb at $\{2\pi m/M\}$, convolving with $X$ places $M$ shifted copies:

$x[n]\cdot p[n]$ 在頻域等於與 $p$ 的 DTFT（$\{2\pi m/M\}$ 上的 delta 梳）卷積。 卷積 = 把 $X$ 放 $M$ 份位移拷貝：

$$\tilde X(\omega)\;=\;\frac{1}{M}\sum_{m=0}^{M-1} X\!\left(\omega-\tfrac{2\pi m}{M}\right)$$

Step 4 · Rescale to the new sample rate

第 4 步 · 縮放到新取樣率

Finally, change the time index from $n$ to $n'=n/M$. The frequency axis stretches by $M$: $\omega_\text{old}=\omega_\text{new}/M$. Substituting, we obtain the final aliasing formula.

最後把時間索引從 $n$ 改成 $n'=n/M$。頻率軸拉伸 $M$ 倍：$\omega_\text{old}=\omega_\text{new}/M$。代回去即得最終式。

RESULT
$$Y(\omega)\;=\;\frac{1}{M}\sum_{m=0}^{M-1} X\!\left(\frac{\omega-2\pi m}{M}\right)\qquad\square$$

The $M$ copies come from the $M$ Fourier-series terms of the sampling comb. Each term creates one shifted copy of $X$; overlap of these copies is exactly aliasing.

$M$ 份副本來自取樣梳狀的 $M$ 個 Fourier 級數項。每項貢獻一份位移的 $X$；副本彼此重疊就是混疊。

問：哪一步首先引入 $M-1$ 份多餘副本？
