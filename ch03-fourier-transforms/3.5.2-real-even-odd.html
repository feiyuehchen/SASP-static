Stage 3-5-2 · Real Even / Odd Signals 關卡 3-5-2 · 實偶 / 實奇訊號 SASP §3.5.2 ↗

Real-even time → real-even spectrum. Real-odd time → purely imaginary odd spectrum.

實偶時訊號 → 實偶頻譜。實奇時訊號 → 純虛奇頻譜。

REAL EVEN SIGNAL
$$x[-n]=x[n]\in\mathbb{R}\;\;\Rightarrow\;\;X(\omega)\in\mathbb{R},\;\;X(-\omega)=X(\omega)$$
REAL ODD SIGNAL
$$x[-n]=-x[n]\in\mathbb{R}\;\;\Rightarrow\;\;X(\omega)\in j\mathbb{R},\;\;X(-\omega)=-X(\omega)$$

The neat consequence: a purely even waveform (a cosine burst, centred rectangular pulse, Gaussian) has zero phase. A purely odd waveform (sine burst, anti-symmetric ramp) has phase fixed at $\pm\pi/2$. That is why cosine-windowed signals are often called "zero-phase" and sine windows "quadrature".

漂亮的結論：純偶波形（餘弦波包、中心化矩形脈衝、高斯）相位全是零。 純奇波形（正弦波包、反對稱斜坡）相位固定在 $\pm\pi/2$。 這就是為什麼餘弦加窗訊號常被叫「零相位」、正弦加窗是「 正交相位」。
