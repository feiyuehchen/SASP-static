Stage 3-16-1 · CT Scaling Theorem 關卡 3-16-1 · 連續時間縮放定理 SASP §3.16.1 ↗

Squeeze in time ↔ stretch in frequency, and the total area (energy) stays the same.

時域壓窄 ↔ 頻域拉開，總面積（能量）不變。

CT SCALING
$$x(at)\;\longleftrightarrow\;\frac{1}{|a|}\,X\!\left(\frac{j\Omega}{a}\right)\qquad a\ne 0$$

If $a>1$, time is compressed by $a$ and frequency is dilated by $a$ — a short event has a wide spectrum. If $a<1$, time is dilated and frequency compresses — a long slow signal has a narrow spectrum. The $1/|a|$ prefactor keeps total energy the same.

$a>1$：時間被壓 $a$ 倍、頻率被放 $a$ 倍 — 短事件有寬頻譜。 $a<1$：時間被放、頻率被壓 — 長慢訊號有窄頻譜。前面的 $1/|a|$ 保證能量不變。

This is the uncertainty principle

這就是不確定性原理

The scaling theorem is the mathematical reason time and frequency resolution trade off. A pulse of width $T$ always has a spectrum of width $\sim 1/T$. You cannot cheat this — the Gaussian pulse hits the theoretical minimum. (Full proof: Appendix B.17 and D.)

縮放定理正是「時頻解析度互相取捨」的數學根源。寬度 $T$ 的脈衝，頻譜寬度永遠是 $\sim 1/T$ 等級。 無法鑽漏洞 — 只有高斯脈衝能達到理論下限。（完整證明見附錄 B.17 與 D。）
