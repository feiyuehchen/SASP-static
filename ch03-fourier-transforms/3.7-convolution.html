Stage 3-7 · Convolution Theorem 關卡 3-7 · 卷積定理 SASP §3.7 ↗

Convolution in time becomes multiplication in frequency. This single fact underwrites digital filtering, echo effects, FFT convolution, and almost everything in this book.

時域卷積 ↔ 頻域相乘。這一條就撐起了數位濾波、回音效果、FFT 卷積，幾乎這整本書都站在它上面。

TIME-DOMAIN CONVOLUTION
$$(x * h)[n]\;=\;\sum_{m=-\infty}^{\infty} x[m]\,h[n-m]$$
THEOREM
$$x * h\;\longleftrightarrow\;X(\omega)\,H(\omega)$$

For an LTI filter $h$, the output spectrum is just the input spectrum multiplied by the filter's frequency response $H(\omega)$. Designing filters becomes: design the shape of $H(\omega)$ and compute $h[n]$ via an inverse transform.

對 LTI 濾波器 $h$，輸出頻譜就是輸入頻譜乘上該濾波器的 頻率響應 $H(\omega)$。設計濾波器 = 畫出 $H(\omega)$ 的形狀，再反 FT 算出 $h[n]$。

Dual statement

對偶版本

MULTIPLICATION IN TIME
$$x[n]\,w[n]\;\longleftrightarrow\;\frac{1}{2\pi}\bigl(X * W\bigr)(\omega)$$

This dual is how windowing causes spectral leakage: multiplying by a window $w$ convolves the spectrum with $W(\omega)$.

這個對偶就是「視窗造成頻譜洩漏」的原因 — 時域乘上視窗 $w$，等於頻域卷積上 $W(\omega)$。
