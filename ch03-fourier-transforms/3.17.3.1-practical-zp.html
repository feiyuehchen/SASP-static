Zero padding is routine — a few tiny pitfalls to keep in mind.

補零很日常，但有幾個小陷阱要避開。

  • Pad after windowing, not before. Windowing a zero-padded signal can taper the zeros, which ruins the spectrum.
  • 先加視窗，再補零，不能反過來。先補零再加窗會把補上的零也弄成斜坡，毀掉頻譜。
  • Match the zero sample values to the DC assumption. If your signal has a non-zero DC component, padding with zeros introduces an edge. Subtract the mean first if you care.
  • 補零值要與 DC 假設一致。訊號有非零平均值時補零會產生邊緣。在意的話就先扣平均。
  • Amplitude scaling depends on convention. Some libraries scale the FFT output by $1/M$, others by $1/\sqrt{M}$, others not at all. Padding does not change the scaling, but check what your tool does.
  • 振幅縮放要看函式庫約定。有的 FFT 輸出除以 $M$、有的除以 $\sqrt{M}$、有的不除。補零不改變縮放，但先查清楚你用的工具。
  • Padding hides leakage, not eliminates it. The leakage shape is set by your window, not by the FFT length.
  • 補零只是把洩漏畫得清楚，不會消除洩漏。洩漏形狀由視窗決定，與 FFT 長度無關。
