Stage 3-17-3-4 · ZP for Spectral Peaks 關卡 3-17-3-4 · 以補零內插頻譜波峰 SASP §3.17.3.4 ↗

Zero-padding is the cheapest way to squeeze accurate peak locations out of a finite FFT. The ZP factor controls how close to the true peak frequency we can land.

想從有限長 FFT 擠出精準的波峰位置，補零是最便宜的做法。補零倍率決定我們能逼近真實峰值頻率到什麼程度。

How accurate?

能多準？

If you stop at the peak bin of a ZP-by-$L$ FFT, your peak-frequency accuracy is about $\pi / (LN)$ rad/sample (half a bin width). If you further fit a parabola through the three samples around the peak (the "quadratic interpolation" trick from Chapter 6), accuracy improves to about $\pi/(LN\cdot Q)$ where $Q$ grows with window quality — typically good enough that $L=4$ or $L=8$ is plenty.

直接取 ZP-$L$ 後的 FFT 峰 bin，精度約為 $\pi/(LN)$ rad/sample（半個 bin 寬）。 再用「波峰附近三點拋物線內插」（第 6 章的 quadratic interpolation）， 精度進一步變成 $\pi/(LN\cdot Q)$，其中 $Q$ 隨視窗品質提升 — 通常 $L=4$ 或 $L=8$ 就已經非常精準。

1. Window. 2. Zero-pad to $L\cdot N$. 3. FFT. 4. Find bin of max magnitude. 5. Parabolic fit with the two neighbours. This is the standard pitch/partial tracker recipe used in spectral modeling synthesis.

1. 加窗。2. 補零到 $L\cdot N$。3. FFT。4. 找振幅最大 bin。5. 與左右鄰居做拋物線擬合。 這就是頻譜建模合成（SMS）裡標準的基頻 / partial 追蹤管線。
