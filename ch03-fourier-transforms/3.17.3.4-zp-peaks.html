Stage 3-17-3-4 · ZP for Spectral Peaks 關卡 3-17-3-4 · 以補零內插頻譜波峰 SASP §3.17.3.4 ↗

Zero-padding is the cheapest way to squeeze accurate peak locations out of a finite FFT. The ZP factor controls how close to the true peak frequency we can land.

想從有限長 FFT 擠出精準的波峰位置，補零是最便宜的做法。補零倍率決定逼近真實峰值頻率的程度。

How accurate?

能多準？

Stop at the peak bin of a ZP-by-$L$ FFT → accuracy $\approx \pi/(LN)$ rad/sample (half a bin width). Fit a parabola through the three samples around the peak (the quadratic peak interpolation trick from Chapter 6) → accuracy $\approx \pi/(LN\cdot Q)$ where $Q$ grows with window quality.

在 ZP-$L$ 後的 FFT 直接取峰 bin → 精度約 $\pi/(LN)$ rad/sample。 再用拋物線內插（第 6 章技巧）→ 精度約 $\pi/(LN\cdot Q)$，$Q$ 隨視窗品質提升。

SMS / pitch-track pipeline: 1. Window. 2. Zero-pad to $L\cdot N$. 3. FFT. 4. Find bin of max magnitude. 5. Parabolic fit with the two neighbours.

SMS / 基頻追蹤流水線：1. 加窗。2. 補零到 $L\cdot N$。3. FFT。4. 找振幅最大 bin。5. 與左右鄰居做拋物線擬合。

Parabolic formula (Smith-Serra)

拋物線公式（Smith-Serra）

$$\Delta k\;=\;\tfrac{1}{2}\cdot\frac{Y_{-1}-Y_{+1}}{Y_{-1}-2Y_0+Y_{+1}}$$

Where $Y_{-1}, Y_0, Y_{+1}$ are the log-magnitudes of the peak bin's left neighbour, itself, and right neighbour. The sub-bin offset $\Delta k\in(-0.5, 0.5)$. Peak frequency becomes $\omega_{\text{peak}}=2\pi(k_0+\Delta k)/M$.

$Y_{-1}, Y_0, Y_{+1}$ 為峰 bin 左鄰、本身、右鄰的 log 振幅。子 bin 偏移 $\Delta k\in(-0.5, 0.5)$。峰頻率 $\omega_{\text{peak}}=2\pi(k_0+\Delta k)/M$。
