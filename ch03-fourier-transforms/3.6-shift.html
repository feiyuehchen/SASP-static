Stage 3-6 · Shift Theorem 關卡 3-6 · 位移定理 SASP §3.6 ↗

Shifting a signal in time multiplies its spectrum by a linear-phase exponential. Magnitude is unchanged.

時間軸上平移訊號 → 頻譜乘上一個線性相位指數。振幅不變。

SHIFT THEOREM
$$x[n-n_0]\;\longleftrightarrow\;e^{-j\omega n_0}\,X(\omega)$$

Proof: substitute $m=n-n_0$ in the DTFT sum. The extra factor $e^{-j\omega n_0}$ pops out and the rest is just $X(\omega)$.

證明：在 DTFT 求和式中換元 $m=n-n_0$，額外冒出 $e^{-j\omega n_0}$，剩下就是 $X(\omega)$。

What it means physically

物理意義

  • Magnitude spectrum $|X(\omega)|$ is translation-invariant — the "fingerprint" of a sound doesn't care when it started.
  • 振幅頻譜 $|X(\omega)|$ 不受時間平移影響 — 聲音的「頻譜指紋」跟何時開始播放無關。
  • Phase picks up a linear term: $\angle Y(\omega)=\angle X(\omega)-\omega n_0$. That linear phase slope is the time delay.
  • 相位多一條線：$\angle Y(\omega)=\angle X(\omega)-\omega n_0$。這條斜率就是時間延遲。
  • This is why a group delay of $n_0$ samples looks like a slope $-n_0$ line on the phase plot.
  • 這就是為什麼「 群延遲 $n_0$ 樣本」在相位圖上是一條斜率 $-n_0$ 的直線。
