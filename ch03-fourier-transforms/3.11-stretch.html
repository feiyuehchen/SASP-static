Stage 3-11 · Stretch Operator 關卡 3-11 · 拉伸算子 SASP §3.11 ↗

Stretching means inserting zeros between samples — the digital equivalent of holding each sample $L$ times as long.

拉伸 = 在樣本之間插零 — 相當於把每個樣本在時間上撐 $L$ 倍長。

STRETCH BY L
$$\operatorname{Stretch}_L\{x\}[n]\;=\;\begin{cases}x[n/L],& n/L\in\mathbb{Z}\\ 0, & \text{otherwise}\end{cases}$$

The effect on the spectrum: the DTFT of the stretched signal is just the original DTFT compressed by $L$ along $\omega$. A copy of the original spectrum now tiles the $2\pi$-periodic circle $L$ times — those extra copies are spectral images.

頻譜端的效果：拉伸後訊號的 DTFT 就是原 DTFT 沿 $\omega$ 壓縮 $L$ 倍。 於是 $2\pi$ 周期的圓上就同時出現 $L$ 份原頻譜 — 多出來的幾份就叫 頻譜鏡像。

STRETCH ↔ FREQUENCY COMPRESSION
$$\operatorname{Stretch}_L\{x\}\;\longleftrightarrow\;X(L\omega)$$

This is the first half of the DSP upsampling process. The second half (kill the images with a low-pass filter) lives in World 12.

這是升取樣的前半段。後半段（用低通濾波器把鏡像壓掉）在第 12 世界會處理。
