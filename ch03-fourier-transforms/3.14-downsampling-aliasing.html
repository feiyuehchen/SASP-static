Stage 3-14 · Downsampling & Aliasing 關卡 3-14 · 降取樣與混疊 SASP §3.14 ↗

Keep every $M$-th sample and throw the rest away. If the spectrum has energy above $\pi/M$, that energy folds back down into the remaining band — aliasing.

每 $M$ 個樣本留一個。如果頻譜在 $\pi/M$ 以上還有能量，這些能量會折回低頻段 — 就是混疊。

DOWNSAMPLE BY M
$$y[n]\;=\;x[nM]$$
ALIASING THEOREM
$$Y(\omega)\;=\;\frac{1}{M}\sum_{m=0}^{M-1} X\!\left(\frac{\omega-2\pi m}{M}\right)$$

The sum collects $M$ shifted, stretched copies of the spectrum and adds them. If the original $X(\omega)$ is zero outside $|\omega|\le\pi/M$, the copies do not overlap and reconstruction is perfect. Otherwise they interfere — that interference is what we hear as a "chipmunk" effect or see as moiré on an image.

和式把 $M$ 份被位移與拉伸過的頻譜疊起來。若原本 $X(\omega)$ 在 $|\omega|>\pi/M$ 為零， 這些份不會重疊，還可完美還原。否則重疊部分就是混疊 — 聽起來像「花栗鼠」音色、看起來像圖片上的摩爾紋。

How to avoid it

如何避免

  • Low-pass filter the signal to $|\omega|\le\pi/M$ before discarding samples. This is the decimation filter.
  • 在丟棄樣本前，先把訊號低通到 $|\omega|\le\pi/M$ — 這個濾波器叫 降取樣（抗混疊）濾波器。
  • The proof that the aliasing sum formula is correct lives on the next page (3.14.1).
  • 為什麼混疊公式會是這個加總形式，下一頁（3.14.1）證明。
