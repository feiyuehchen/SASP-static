Stage 3-13 · Stretch / Repeat (Scaling) Theorem 關卡 3-13 · 拉伸/重複 (縮放) 定理 SASP §3.13 ↗

Stretching in time = repeating in frequency. And vice versa.

時域拉伸 = 頻域重複。反之亦然。

SCALING THEOREM
$$\operatorname{Stretch}_L\{x\}[n]\;\longleftrightarrow\;\operatorname{Repeat}_L\{X\}(\omega)$$

Combining §3.11 (stretch) with §3.12 (repeat): stretching by $L$ compresses the spectrum by $L$, but because the spectrum is already $2\pi$-periodic, the compressed version repeats $L$ times across the original period. Rewritten in the "repeat" language, we get the theorem above.

把 §3.11（拉伸）與 §3.12（重複）合起來：時域拉伸 $L$ 倍 → 頻譜壓縮 $L$ 倍； 但頻譜原本就 $2\pi$-週期，壓縮後自然在原本的週期內重複 $L$ 次。 用「重複」的語言寫出來，就是上面的定理。

What "stretch" means on a sequence

對序列而言「拉伸」是什麼意思

$\operatorname{Stretch}_L\{x\}[n]$ is zero insertion: keep $x[k]$ at index $n=kL$, fill zeros everywhere else. Time axis gets $L$ times longer. This is the operation an audio upsampler does before filtering.

$\operatorname{Stretch}_L\{x\}[n]$ 即「插零」：把 $x[k]$ 放在 $n=kL$、其他位置填 $0$。 時間軸變 $L$ 倍長。這是音訊升取樣器在濾波前的動作。

Upsampling needs an anti-imaging filter. Zero-inserting stretches the time signal but leaves $L-1$ spectral copies — spectral images. Follow the zero-inserter with a sharp low-pass at $\pi/L$ to keep only the baseband copy.

升取樣要接抗鏡像濾波器。插零會在頻域留下 $L-1$ 份 頻譜鏡像。 在插零後加一個截止 $\pi/L$ 的銳利低通，只保留基頻那一份。
