Stage 3-13 · Stretch / Repeat (Scaling) Theorem 關卡 3-13 · 拉伸/重複 (縮放) 定理 SASP §3.13 ↗

Stretching in time = repeating in frequency. And vice versa.

時域拉伸 = 頻域重複。反之亦然。

SCALING THEOREM (DTFT)
$$\operatorname{Stretch}_L\{x\}[n]\;\longleftrightarrow\;\operatorname{Repeat}_L\{X\}(\omega)$$

Combine the two previous pages: stretching by $L$ compresses the spectrum by $L$, but because the spectrum is already $2\pi$-periodic, the compressed version repeats $L$ times across the original period. Writing the same fact in the "repeat" language gives the theorem above.

把前兩頁合起來：時域拉伸 $L$ 倍 → 頻譜被壓縮 $L$ 倍，但頻譜本就有 $2\pi$ 週期， 壓縮後自然在原本的週期內重複 $L$ 次。把這事實用「重複」的語言寫出來，就是上面的定理。

This theorem is why digital upsampling by $L$ needs an anti-imaging low-pass filter after zero insertion — to kill the $L-1$ unwanted spectral copies.

這條定理解釋了為什麼數位升取樣 $L$ 倍後要接一個抗鏡像低通濾波器 — 用來壓掉多出來的 $L-1$ 份頻譜。
