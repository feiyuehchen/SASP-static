Stage 3-17-4-1 · Matlab/Octave fftshift 關卡 3-17-4-1 · Matlab/Octave 的 fftshift SASP §3.17.4.1 ↗

Swaps the two halves of an array. Converts between "centred" and "causal" indexing for both signals and spectra.

把一個陣列前後兩半對調。在「中心化」與「因果」索引之間切換 — 訊號、頻譜都通用。

FOR EVEN LENGTH M
$$\texttt{fftshift}(x)\;=\;\bigl[\,x[M/2],\,x[M/2+1],\,\ldots,\,x[M-1],\,x[0],\,x[1],\,\ldots,\,x[M/2-1]\,\bigr]$$

Typical uses

常見用法

  • Plot a DFT centred at 0: plot(omega, fftshift(abs(X))) puts DC in the middle.
  • 以 0 為中心畫 DFT：plot(omega, fftshift(abs(X)))，DC 放中間。
  • Prepare a centred signal for zero-phase analysis: x_zp = fftshift([centered; zeros(M-N,1)]).
  • 為零相位分析準備中心化訊號：x_zp = fftshift([centered; zeros(M-N,1)])。
  • ifftshift is the inverse; equals fftshift for even $M$, differs for odd $M$. Use whichever matches direction.
  • ifftshift 是反向；偶數 $M$ 兩者相同、奇數 $M$ 不同。依方向挑。

In the browser: const y = [...x.slice(M/2), ...x.slice(0, M/2)]. That is all the utility really does.

瀏覽器裡：const y = [...x.slice(M/2), ...x.slice(0, M/2)]。就這麼簡單。
