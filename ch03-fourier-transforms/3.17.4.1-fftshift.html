Swaps the two halves of an array. Converts between "centred" and "causal" indexing for both signals and spectra.

把一個陣列前後兩半對調。在「中心化」與「因果」索引之間切換 — 訊號、頻譜都通用。

FOR EVEN LENGTH M
$$\texttt{fftshift}(x)\;=\;\bigl[\,x[M/2],\,x[M/2+1],\,\ldots,\,x[M-1],\,x[0],\,x[1],\,\ldots,\,x[M/2-1]\,\bigr]$$

Typical uses

常見用法

  • Plot a DFT centred at 0: plot(omega, fftshift(abs(X))) puts DC in the middle.
  • 以 0 為中心畫 DFT：plot(omega, fftshift(abs(X)))，DC 放中間。
  • Prepare a centered signal for zero-phase analysis: x_zp = fftshift( [centered_data; zeros(M-N,1)] ).
  • 為零相位分析準備中心化訊號：x_zp = fftshift( [centered_data; zeros(M-N,1)] )。
  • ifftshift is the inverse and equals fftshift for even $M$ but differs for odd $M$. Use whichever matches the direction you need.
  • ifftshift 是反向操作，對偶數 $M$ 和 fftshift 一樣；奇數 $M$ 時不同。請挑對方向使用。

In the browser: const y = [...x.slice(M/2), ...x.slice(0, M/2)]. That is all the utility really does.

在瀏覽器裡：const y = [...x.slice(M/2), ...x.slice(0, M/2)]。其實就是這樣。
