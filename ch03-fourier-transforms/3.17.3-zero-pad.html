Stage 3-17-3 · Zero Padding in the Time Domain 關卡 3-17-3 · 時域補零 SASP §3.17.3 ↗

Append zeros to a length-$N$ sequence and do a length-$M$ FFT with $M\gg N$. Result: the same DTFT, sampled on a finer frequency grid.

在長度 $N$ 的序列後面補零、做長度 $M$（$M\gg N$）的 FFT。 得到的就是同一條 DTFT，但取樣更密。

Why it works

為什麼行得通

Zero padding does not add new time information. The DTFT of a length-$N$ signal is completely determined by those $N$ samples. But the $M$-point DFT evaluates the DTFT at $M$ equally-spaced frequencies $2\pi k/M$ — more $M$, more sample points, denser plot.

補零不會增加時間資訊 — 長度 $N$ 訊號的 DTFT 早被那 $N$ 個樣本決定。 但長度 $M$ 的 DFT 是在 $M$ 個均勻頻率 $2\pi k/M$ 上取樣 — $M$ 越大、樣本點越多、頻譜圖越密。

What it does NOT do

它不會做的事

  • It does not improve resolution. The ability to separate two close sinusoids is set by the signal length $N$, not by $M$. Zero padding just makes the interpolation smoother.
  • 不會提高解析度。能分離兩個相近正弦波的能力，由原訊號長度 $N$ 決定，與 $M$ 無關。補零只是讓內插更平滑。
  • It does not add information — zero samples contain nothing.
  • 不會多出資訊 — 零樣本本身沒資訊。

"Zero padding increases frequency resolution" is wrong. It increases bin density, which is what you need to read the shape of the main lobe and pick out peaks with better accuracy.

「補零會提高頻率解析度」是誤解。它提高的是bin 密度，用來看清主瓣形狀、提高波峰定位精度。
