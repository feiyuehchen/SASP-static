Stage 3-17 · Spectral Interpolation 關卡 3-17 · 頻譜內插 SASP §3.17 ↗

A DFT samples the spectrum at $N$ uniform points. Sometimes that is not enough — we need the value at a frequency between two bins. Good news: with a finite-length signal, the full DTFT is completely determined, and interpolation is exact.

DFT 在 $N$ 個均勻頻率點上對頻譜取樣。有時不夠用 — 我們要兩個 bin 之間某個頻率的值。好消息：有限長訊號的整條 DTFT 完全可被那 $N$ 個樣本決定，內插是精確的。

Three ways

三種作法

  • Ideal (Dirichlet kernel): the theoretically exact interpolator for a finite-length sequence.
  • 理想內插（Dirichlet 核）：有限長序列對應的精確內插器。
  • Interpolating a DFT — write the continuous DTFT as a sum over DFT values.
  • DFT 內插 — 把連續 DTFT 寫成 DFT 值的求和。
  • Zero padding — the lazy but excellent way: pad zeros in time, FFT, read denser bins.
  • 補零 — 懶但好用：時域補零、FFT、讀更密的 bin。
  • Zero-phase zero padding — do the above without introducing an artificial phase slope.
  • 零相位補零 — 做補零但不引入假的線性相位。

Frequency-domain peaks rarely sit exactly on a DFT bin. To measure the actual peak frequency of a sinusoid (and therefore its pitch) we must interpolate between bins. Peak-interpolation accuracy governs how good a pitch detector, a vocoder, or a spectral modeling system can get.

頻峰幾乎不會剛好落在 DFT bin 上。要量出正弦波真正的峰值頻率（也就是音高），一定得在 bin 之間內插。 內插精度決定了基頻偵測器、vocoder、頻譜建模系統的上限。
