Stage 3-16 · Continuous-Time Fourier Theorems 關卡 3-16 · 連續時間傅立葉定理 SASP §3.16 ↗

The continuous-time (CT) cousins of the theorems you just saw. Appendix B has the full catalogue; here we visit two that are most useful for understanding the discrete side: scaling and spectral roll-off.

剛學完的定理的連續時間 (CT) 對應版。附錄 B 有完整列表；這裡先停靠兩站，對理解離散版最有幫助 — 縮放與頻譜滾降。

CONTINUOUS-TIME FOURIER TRANSFORM
$$X(j\Omega)=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}x(t)e^{-j\Omega t}\,dt$$

Two stops

兩站

  • 3.16.1 Scaling theorem: $x(at)\leftrightarrow\tfrac{1}{|a|}X(j\Omega/a)$. Squeezing in time stretches in frequency (and vice versa).
  • 3.16.1 縮放定理：$x(at)\leftrightarrow\tfrac{1}{|a|}X(j\Omega/a)$。 時域壓窄 → 頻域拉開（反之亦然）。
  • 3.16.2 Spectral roll-off: the smoother a signal, the faster its spectrum decays. Discontinuities → $1/\Omega$ roll-off; kinks → $1/\Omega^2$; and so on.
  • 3.16.2 頻譜滾降：訊號越平滑，頻譜衰減越快。不連續 → $1/\Omega$；折點 → $1/\Omega^2$；依此類推。

Audio sampling, anti-aliasing and resampling all live inside a continuous-time signal model that gets sampled. Mastering these two CT results is enough to interpret the rest of the discrete theory later.

音訊的取樣、抗混疊、重新取樣其實都在一個連續時間模型中發生，再被取樣成離散。 只要先搞懂這兩條 CT 結果，之後的離散理論就很好理解。
