Stage 3-5-1 · DTFT of Real Signals 關卡 3-5-1 · 實訊號的 DTFT SASP §3.5.1 ↗

A real signal's DTFT splits into an even real part plus an odd imaginary part. The split mirrors how the signal itself decomposes into an even part and an odd part.

實訊號的 DTFT 拆成「實部（偶） + 虛部（奇）」。這個拆法剛好對應訊號本身的偶+奇分解。

The even-odd map

偶奇對應

TIME EVEN/ODD ↔ FREQ REAL/IMAG
$$x_e[n]=\tfrac{1}{2}(x[n]+x[-n])\;\longleftrightarrow\;\operatorname{Re}\{X(\omega)\}$$
$$x_o[n]=\tfrac{1}{2}(x[n]-x[-n])\;\longleftrightarrow\;j\,\operatorname{Im}\{X(\omega)\}$$

Because a real $x[n]$ satisfies Hermitian 對稱 $X^*(\omega)=X(-\omega)$, the real part of $X(\omega)$ must be even in $\omega$ and the imaginary part must be odd. Reading backwards: $x_e$ makes $\operatorname{Re}X$; $x_o$ makes $\operatorname{Im}X$.

由於實 $x[n]$ 滿足Hermitian 對稱 $X^*(\omega)=X(-\omega)$，$\operatorname{Re}X$ 對 $\omega$ 為偶、$\operatorname{Im}X$ 為奇。 反看：$x_e$ 產生 $\operatorname{Re}X$；$x_o$ 產生 $\operatorname{Im}X$。

Magnitude / phase consequences

振幅 / 相位的推論

$$|X(-\omega)|=|X(\omega)|,\qquad \angle X(-\omega)=-\angle X(\omega)$$

Magnitude is even, phase is odd. Audio spectrum plotters exploit this: for real audio we only display $\omega\in[0,\pi]$ because the negative half is the mirror image.

振幅是偶、相位是奇。音訊頻譜圖就利用這點：實音訊只畫 $\omega\in[0,\pi]$，負半邊是鏡像。

Storing real audio in the frequency domain costs half the memory of a complex signal. $N$ time samples ↔ $N/2+1$ unique complex bins (the rest are conjugate mirrors).

實音訊在頻域儲存只需複訊號的一半記憶體。$N$ 個時間樣本 ↔ $N/2+1$ 個獨立複 bin（其餘為共軛鏡像）。

✦ 關底小魔王 ✦

Q. A real signal has $\operatorname{Im}\{X(\omega_0)\}=+0.7$. What is $\operatorname{Im}\{X(-\omega_0)\}$?

問：某實訊號 $\operatorname{Im}\{X(\omega_0)\}=+0.7$，$\operatorname{Im}\{X(-\omega_0)\}$ 為何？

Lab · Watch Re/Im symmetries appear

實驗 · 看實部/虛部的對稱如何出現

A shifted impulse gives both, with the even symmetry on Re and odd symmetry on Im.

上：$x[n]$。中：$\operatorname{Re}X$。下：$\operatorname{Im}X$。試試偶 / 奇：純餘弦 $\operatorname{Im}X\equiv 0$；純正弦 $\operatorname{Re}X\equiv 0$。位移脈衝兩者皆非零，Re 偶、Im 奇。
