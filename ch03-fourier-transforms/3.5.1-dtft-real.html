Stage 3-5-1 · DTFT of Real Signals 關卡 3-5-1 · 實訊號的 DTFT SASP §3.5.1 ↗

A real signal's DTFT splits into an even real part plus an odd imaginary part. The split mirrors how the signal splits into an even and an odd component.

實訊號的 DTFT 可以拆成「實部（偶）+ 虛部（奇）」。這個拆法剛好對應訊號本身的偶 + 奇分解。

EVEN/ODD TIME ↔ EVEN/ODD FREQ
$$x_{\text{even}}[n]=\tfrac{1}{2}(x[n]+x[-n])\;\longleftrightarrow\;\operatorname{Re}\{X(\omega)\}$$
$$x_{\text{odd}}[n]=\tfrac{1}{2}(x[n]-x[-n])\;\longleftrightarrow\;j\,\operatorname{Im}\{X(\omega)\}$$

Because $X^*(\omega)=X(-\omega)$ for real $x[n]$, the real part of $X(\omega)$ must be even in $\omega$ and the imaginary part must be odd. Reading it backwards: the even part of the signal produces the real part of the spectrum, the odd part produces the imaginary part.

因為實 $x[n]$ 滿足 $X^*(\omega)=X(-\omega)$，$\operatorname{Re}X$ 必須是 $\omega$ 的偶函數、 $\operatorname{Im}X$ 必須是奇函數。反過來看：訊號的偶部分產生頻譜實部、奇部分產生頻譜虛部。

If you only care about the magnitude spectrum of real audio, you can throw away the sign of the imaginary part with no loss — and you can store or transmit only half the DFT bins.

如果你只在意實音訊的振幅頻譜，虛部的符號可以丟掉都沒差 — 而且 DFT bin 也只要存一半就夠了。
