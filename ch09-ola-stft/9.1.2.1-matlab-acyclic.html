L = Nx + Nh - 1;
Y = fft(x, L) .* fft(h, L);
y = ifft(Y);          % linear convolution, first L samples

y is already the right length. Matlab's conv(x,h) is equivalent but does time-domain multiply-add, which is slower for large inputs.
