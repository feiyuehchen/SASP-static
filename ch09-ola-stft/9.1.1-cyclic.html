Stage 9-1-1 · Cyclic FFT Convolution關卡 9-1-1 · 循環 FFT 卷積SASP §9.1.1 ↗

$$\text{IFFT}\{X[k]\cdot H[k]\}\;=\;(x\circledast h)[n]$$

Multiplying DFT bins gives cyclic convolution (indices mod $N$), not linear convolution. To recover linear you must zero-pad both inputs to at least $N_x+N_h-1$.

DFT bin 相乘得到循環卷積（索引 mod $N$），不是線性卷積。要還原線性得把兩序列補零到至少 $N_x+N_h-1$。
