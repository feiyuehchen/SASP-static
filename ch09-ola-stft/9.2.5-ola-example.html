Stage 9-2-5 · Example of OLA Convolution關卡 9-2-5 · OLA 卷積範例SASP §9.2.5 ↗

Convolving a 10-second audio file with a 1-second room impulse response via OLA at 1024-sample frames takes a fraction of the time of direct convolution, and the result is bit-identical (to machine precision).

在 1024 樣本 frame 下以 OLA 卷積 10 秒音訊與 1 秒房間脈衝響應，比直接卷積快許多倍；結果在機器精度下完全一致。
