Stage 9-2-4 · Acyclic Convolution via OLA關卡 9-2-4 · 用 OLA 做非循環卷積SASP §9.2.4 ↗

For each frame: zero-pad to $L\ge N_{\text{frame}}+N_h-1$, multiply its DFT with $H[k]$, IFFT, add to the output at the correct offset. This is the standard "fast convolution with a long signal" pipeline.

對每個 frame：補零到 $L\ge N_{\text{frame}}+N_h-1$，DFT 乘 $H[k]$、IFFT、在正確偏移處加到輸出上。這就是「長訊號快速卷積」的標準管線。
