For filter length $N_h$ roughly $\ge 30$–$50$, the FFT path wins. Short filters ($N_h<16$) are almost always cheaper in direct time-domain form.

濾波器長 $N_h$ 大致 $\ge 30$–$50$ 後 FFT 路線贏。$N_h<16$ 的短濾波器幾乎都用直接時域較便宜。
