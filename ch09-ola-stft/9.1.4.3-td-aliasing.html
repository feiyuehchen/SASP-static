Stage 9-1-4-3 · Example 2: Time-Domain Aliasing關卡 9-1-4-3 · 範例 2：時域混疊SASP §9.1.4.3 ↗

If you forget to pad, the IFFT output's tail wraps onto its head — textbook "time-domain aliasing." The plot looks fine in the middle but the first and last few samples are corrupted. Always pad to at least $N_x+N_h-1$.

忘了補零時，IFFT 輸出尾端會繞回前端 — 經典「時域混疊」。中段看起來沒事但頭尾幾個樣本壞了。補到至少 $N_x+N_h-1$。
