function y = fftconv(x, h)
  L = 2^nextpow2(numel(x) + numel(h) - 1);
  Y = fft(x, L) .* fft(h, L);
  y = real(ifft(Y));
  y = y(1 : numel(x) + numel(h) - 1);
end

Rounding $L$ up to a power of 2 keeps the FFT fast. Trim back to the true linear-convolution length afterwards.

把 $L$ 拉到 2 次冪讓 FFT 快。結束後裁回真實線性卷積長度。
