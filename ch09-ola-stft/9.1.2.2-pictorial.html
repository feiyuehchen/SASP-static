Visualise linear convolution as: for each $x[n]$, put a scaled copy of $h$ starting at $n$, then sum the stack vertically. Cyclic convolution wraps any $n$ beyond $N-1$ back to $n-N$, causing overlap — which is why zero-padding is needed.

把線性卷積想成：對每個 $x[n]$，在位置 $n$ 放一份比例的 $h$，再垂直相加。循環卷積則把超過 $N-1$ 的部分繞回 $n-N$，造成重疊 — 所以要補零。
