Stage 9-1-4-1 · Audio FIR Filters關卡 9-1-4-1 · 音訊 FIR 濾波器SASP §9.1.4.1 ↗

Audio FIRs range from 32 taps (simple pre-emphasis) to 48000+ (FFT-based room impulse-response convolution). Anything $\ge 64$ is essentially always done via FFT convolution in real-time systems.

音訊 FIR 可能從 32 抽頭（簡單預強調）到 48000+（FFT 卷積房間脈衝響應）。即時系統中長度 $\ge 64$ 幾乎都用 FFT 卷積。
