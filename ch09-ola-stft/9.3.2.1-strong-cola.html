Stage 9-3-2-1 · Strong COLA關卡 9-3-2-1 · 強 COLASASP §9.3.2.1 ↗

Weak COLA only guarantees identity reconstruction (frames passed through). Strong COLA (all non-DC samples of the window's DTFT at hop multiples are zero) is required for per-frame modification to be free of aliasing artefacts.

弱 COLA 只保證原封輸出能完美重建。若要任意逐 frame 修改而不產生混疊破音，須滿足強 COLA（視窗 DTFT 在跳距倍數處、非 DC 樣本皆為零）。
