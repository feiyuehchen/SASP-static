Stage 9-3-2 · Frequency-Domain COLA Constraints關卡 9-3-2 · 頻域 COLA 約束SASP §9.3.2 ↗

For a given window $w$, COLA holds at hop $R$ iff $W(\omega_k)=0$ for every non-DC harmonic $\omega_k = 2\pi k/R$. Hann at $R=N/2$ passes; Hamming at $R=N/2$ passes up to a small constant; rectangular at $R=N$ trivially passes.

給定視窗 $w$，跳距 $R$ 滿足 COLA 的條件：$W(\omega_k)=0$ 對每個非 DC 諧波 $\omega_k=2\pi k/R$。Hann、$R=N/2$ 通過；Hamming、$R=N/2$ 通過（差小常數）；矩形、$R=N$ 平凡通過。
