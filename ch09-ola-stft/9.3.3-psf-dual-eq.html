Stage 9-3-3 · PSF Dual and Graphical Equalisers關卡 9-3-3 · PSF 對偶與圖形等化器SASP §9.3.3 ↗

A graphical EQ sums several overlapping band-filter outputs. For the sum to be flat at unity band gains, the filter shapes must satisfy a frequency-domain COLA — the Poisson-dual condition.

圖形等化器把多個重疊頻帶濾波器輸出相加。所有增益為 1 時總和為平直的條件，就是這些濾波器形狀滿足頻域 COLA — 即 Poisson 對偶條件。
