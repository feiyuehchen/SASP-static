Stage 9-2 · Convolving with Long Signals關卡 9-2 · 長訊號卷積SASP §9.2 ↗

For a signal much longer than the filter (or processed in real-time), split into blocks and FFT-convolve each block with the filter, then stitch outputs back — that is overlap-add.

訊號遠比濾波器長（或即時處理）時，把它切 block、對每 block 做 FFT 卷積、再拼回 — 這就是重疊相加。
