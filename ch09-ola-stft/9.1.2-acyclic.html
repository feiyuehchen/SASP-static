Stage 9-1-2 · Acyclic FFT Convolution關卡 9-1-2 · 非循環 FFT 卷積SASP §9.1.2 ↗

Zero-pad $x$ and $h$ to length $L\ge N_x+N_h-1$ (usually next power of 2). FFT both. Multiply bin-by-bin. IFFT. First $N_x+N_h-1$ samples are the linear convolution.

把 $x$、$h$ 補零到 $L\ge N_x+N_h-1$（通常取 2 次冪）。各自 FFT、逐 bin 相乘、IFFT。前 $N_x+N_h-1$ 個樣本就是線性卷積。
