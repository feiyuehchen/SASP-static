Stage 9-1 · Convolution of Short Signals關卡 9-1 · 短訊號卷積SASP §9.1 ↗

Direct time-domain convolution of two length-$N$ sequences costs $O(N^2)$ multiply-adds. Doing it through FFTs costs $O(N\log N)$ — a huge win once $N$ is beyond a few dozen.

長度 $N$ 的兩序列直接時域卷積要 $O(N^2)$；透過 FFT 變 $O(N\log N)$。當 $N$ 超過幾十就大贏。
