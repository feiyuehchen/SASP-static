Stage 9-5 · Time-Varying OLA Modifications關卡 9-5 · 時變 OLA 修改SASP §9.5 ↗

Per-frame modifications that change over time (auto-tune, dynamic EQ, transient shaping) make the output pass through a time-varying filter. Strong COLA windows bound the aliasing introduced by the changes.

逐 frame 修改會隨時間變化（auto-tune、動態 EQ、瞬態塑形）時，輸出過了一個時變濾波器。強 COLA 視窗限制修改引入的混疊。
