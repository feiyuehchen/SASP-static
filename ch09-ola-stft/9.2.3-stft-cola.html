Stage 9-2-3 · STFT of a COLA Decomposition關卡 9-2-3 · COLA 分解的 STFTSASP §9.2.3 ↗

Apply DFT to each frame, then inverse-DFT, then overlap-add. If COLA holds, the recovered signal is bit-for-bit the original (within floating-point noise). Nothing else is needed for identity processing.

對每個 frame 做 DFT 再反 DFT，最後 OLA。COLA 成立時還原訊號與原訊號逐樣本相同（至浮點誤差）。恆等處理不需其他步驟。
