Stage 9-5-1 · Block-Diagram Interpretation關卡 9-5-1 · 方塊圖解釋SASP §9.5.1 ↗

A time-varying STFT modifier can be drawn as a bank of LTI filters (one per $H_m[k]$ snapshot) cross-faded by overlapping windows. Smooth tapers → smooth cross-fades → no audible clicks.

時變 STFT 修改可畫成一組 LTI 濾波器（每個 $H_m[k]$ 一個快照）透過重疊視窗交叉淡入淡出。收尾平滑 → 淡變平滑 → 聽不到 click。
