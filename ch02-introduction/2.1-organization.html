Stage 2-1 · Organization of the Book 關卡 2-1 · 本書的組織 SASP §2.1 ↗

Know the shape of the mountain before you start climbing — four big camps plus a supply tent of appendices.

開始爬山前，先看一眼整座山的輪廓 — 四個主要營地加上一頂補給帳篷（附錄）。

The four camps

四個主要營地

SASP is arranged like a climb up the same mountain in four stages, each one building on the previous:

SASP 的排法就像分四階段攻頂同一座山，每一階都踩在前一階的肩膀上：

  1. Spectrum analysis of short signals. Start with the Fourier transform itself, windows, FIR design, and how windowed sinusoids and noise look in the frequency domain. Worlds 3–7.
  2. 短訊號的頻譜分析。從傅立葉轉換、 視窗函數、 FIR 濾波器設計，到加窗正弦波與雜訊在頻域長什麼樣。第 3–7 世界。
  3. Time-frequency representations. Sliding the spectrum analysis along time — the STFT — and turning it into a picture (spectrogram) you can read. World 8.
  4. 時頻表示。把頻譜分析沿著時間滑動 — 也就是 STFT — 再把結果畫成可以一眼看懂的 聲譜圖。第 8 世界。
  5. STFT as a processing tool. Two equally-valid ways to read the STFT — as overlap-add of FFT frames, or as a bank of bandpass filters — plus convolution via FFT, modifications, and applications like pitch tracking and cross-synthesis. Worlds 9–11.
  6. 用 STFT 做訊號處理。用兩種等價的方式看 STFT — 當作一連串 FFT 的 重疊相加 (OLA)，或當作一組 帶通濾波器組 — 加上 FFT 卷積、頻域修改、以及基頻追蹤、交叉合成之類的應用。第 9–11 世界。
  7. Multirate and wavelets. Generalise everything: what if the frequency bands don't have to be equally-spaced? Polyphase, QMF/CQF, perfect reconstruction, wavelets, MPEG. World 12.
  8. 多取樣率與小波。把之前的概念推廣：頻帶如果不一定要等寬呢？ 多相位分解、 QMF/CQF、 完美重建、 小波、MPEG。第 12 世界。

The supply tent

補給帳篷

Finally, a pile of appendices covers the reusable machinery: notation, continuous-time Fourier theorems, statistics, Gaussian function properties, Bark frequency warping, Matlab listings, a history of spectral audio modeling, and the classic PARSHL sinusoidal-modeling program.

最後有一疊附錄，裝滿到處都會用到的工具：符號約定、 連續時間傅立葉定理、 統計訊號處理、 高斯函數性質、 Bark 頻率尺度 的變換、Matlab 範例、頻譜音訊建模的歷史、以及經典的 PARSHL 正弦建模程式。

The worlds on the map follow the book's order, but you can jump around freely. Concepts that depend on earlier ones will flag their prerequisites at the top. The tooltip-underlined terms are your glossary — hover to recall any definition, in either language.

地圖上的世界依原書順序，但你可以隨意跳關。依賴前面觀念的頁面，最上面會標明前置技能。 劃虛線底線的術語就是你的字典 — 滑鼠移上去隨時查中英定義。
