Stage 2-2-2 · STFT & Time-Frequency Displays 關卡 2-2-2 · STFT 與時頻顯示 SASP §2.2.2 ↗

Music, speech, any real sound — the frequency content changes over time. A spectrogram is a picture of those changes: time on one axis, frequency on the other.

音樂、語音、任何真實聲音 — 頻率內容都會隨時間變化。聲譜圖就是這個變化的圖：一軸是時間，另一軸是頻率。

Sliding FFT → spectrogram

滑動 FFT → 聲譜圖

Chop the signal into short overlapping frames, window each frame, take its FFT, and stack the magnitudes side by side as image columns. That image is a spectrogram — and the underlying operation is the Short-Time Fourier Transform (STFT).

把訊號切成一段段重疊的短 frame，每段加視窗、取 FFT，把振幅當圖像的直欄一條條排過去。 排出來的圖就是聲譜圖； 底下的運算就叫 短時距傅立葉轉換（STFT）。

STFT
$$X_m(\omega)\;=\;\sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty} x[n]\,w[n-mR]\,e^{-j\omega n}$$

$m$ indexes time frames, $R$ is the hop size, $w$ is the window. Two knobs, one trade-off:

$m$ 是 frame 索引；$R$ 是跳距； $w$ 是視窗。兩顆旋鈕，一個取捨：

  • Short window → sharp time resolution, fuzzy frequency.
  • 短視窗 → 時間解析度尖、頻率解析度糊。
  • Long window → sharp frequency, fuzzy time.
  • 長視窗 → 頻率尖、時間糊。
  • Small hop $R$ → more frames (smooth, expensive).
  • 跳距 $R$ 小 → frame 更多（畫面平滑、計算量大）。

The trade-off has a formal name — the time-frequency uncertainty principle — and it is impossible to dodge.

這個取捨有個正式的名字：時頻不確定性原理 — 是繞不開的數學事實。

Lab · Chirp spectrogram

實驗 · 線性掃頻的聲譜圖

A linear chirp sweeps from $f_0=200\text{Hz}$ up to $f_1$. The diagonal streak on the spectrogram IS the chirp. Shrink the window: streak becomes sharper in time, fuzzier in frequency. Enlarge it: the opposite.

一段線性掃頻從 $f_0=200\text{Hz}$ 升到 $f_1$。 聲譜圖上那條斜線就是它。視窗變窄 → 斜線時間變尖、頻率變糊；變寬 → 相反。
