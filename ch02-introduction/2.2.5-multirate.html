The STFT slices frequency into equal bands. That is not always what your ear — or MPEG — wants.

STFT 把頻率切成等寬頻帶。但你的耳朵 — 還有 MPEG — 不一定想要這樣切。

Why escape uniform bands?

為什麼要跳出等寬？

Human hearing has critical bands that are narrow at low frequency and wide at high frequency. Audio coders like MP3 and AAC match their analysis to that. Wavelets take it further: analyse each octave with a filter of its own bandwidth.

人耳有臨界頻帶 — 低頻窄、高頻寬。MP3、AAC 等音訊編碼器都讓分析方式貼合這個聽感。 小波 再進一步：每個八度用自己頻寬的濾波器去分析。

The building blocks

組成零件

  • Upsampling / downsampling — change the sample rate.
  • 升/降取樣 — 改變取樣率。
  • Polyphase decomposition — cuts the computation after decimation or before interpolation to only the terms that actually contribute.
  • 多相位分解 — 在降取樣後（或升取樣前）只算真正會貢獻輸出的乘加，省掉一堆冗餘。
  • QMF / CQF banks — two-channel filter banks with perfect reconstruction.
  • QMF / CQF 濾波器組 — 完美重建的兩通道濾波器組。
  • Paraunitary multi-channel filter banks — the modern foundation.
  • Paraunitary 多通道濾波器組 — 現代理論基礎。
  • Wavelet filter banks — dyadic trees built from PR pairs, giving log-frequency resolution.
  • 小波濾波器組 — 用完美重建對堆成的二進樹，給出對數頻率解析度。
  • MPEG-style cosine-modulated filter banks — pseudo-QMF and PR cosine banks, used in real codecs.
  • MPEG 餘弦調變濾波器組 — Pseudo-QMF 與 PR 餘弦濾波器組，實際編碼器在用的那種。
