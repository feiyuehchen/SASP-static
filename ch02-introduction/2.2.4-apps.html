Stage 2-2-4 · STFT Applications 關卡 2-2-4 · STFT 應用 SASP §2.2.4 ↗

Six big use-cases. Each gets its own deep dive in World 11; this page is the trailer.

六個主要用途。每一個在第 11 世界都有深入關卡；這頁只是預告片。

  • F0 (pitch) estimation. Find the harmonic stack in the STFT and back out the fundamental frequency. Needed for auto-tune, karaoke scoring, music transcription.
  • 基頻估計。 從 STFT 抓諧波堆疊、推回基頻。自動調音、卡拉 OK 打分、音樂轉譜都要用。
  • Cross-synthesis. Take the spectral envelope of one sound and the excitation of another, multiply frame by frame. That is how "talking guitar" effects and the classic vocoder work.
  • 交叉合成。 把某段聲音的頻譜包絡，跟另一段的激發訊號逐 frame 相乘。 就是「會說話的吉他」與經典 vocoder 的原理。
  • Spectral envelope extraction. Cepstral smoothing or linear prediction both strip the fine structure off a spectrum to leave a smooth vocal-tract-like curve.
  • 頻譜包絡萃取。 用倒頻譜平滑或 線性預測 把頻譜細節拔掉，剩下一條像聲道形狀的平滑曲線。
  • Sines + Noise + Transients (SMS). Model sounds as a sum of tracked sinusoidal partials plus a residual noise plus sharp transients. Super flexible for time-scaling, pitch-shifting and creative sound design.
  • 頻譜建模合成（SMS）。 把聲音拆成被追蹤的正弦 partials、殘餘雜訊、以及尖銳瞬態。 要做時間伸縮、變調、創意音效都超有彈性。
  • Time-scale modification. Speed up or slow down a sound without changing its pitch — by manipulating the STFT frame timing before resynthesis.
  • 時間尺度變化（TSM）。 把聲音變快或變慢，但音高不變 — 做法是在再合成前重新安排 STFT frame 的時序。
  • Chirplets & FFT filter banks. Specialised analyses for signals whose frequency content sweeps (chirps) or needs octave-spaced decomposition.
  • Chirplet 與 FFT 濾波器組。針對頻率會掃動的訊號、 或需要八度間隔分解的分析工具。
