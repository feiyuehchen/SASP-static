Stage 5-2 · Lowpass Filter Design Specifications 關卡 5-2 · 低通濾波器設計規格 SASP §5.2 ↗

A realisable lowpass is specified by four numbers.

可實作的低通濾波器由四個數字規格決定。

SPECIFICATION
$$\begin{array}{ll} \omega_p & \text{passband edge} \\ \omega_s & \text{stopband edge}\;(\omega_s>\omega_p) \\ \delta_p & \text{max passband ripple (linear)} \\ \delta_s & \text{max stopband ripple (linear)} \end{array}$$

Between $\omega_p$ and $\omega_s$ is the transition band. Its width, $\omega_s-\omega_p$, is what costs you filter length: narrower transition → more taps.

$\omega_p$ 到 $\omega_s$ 之間是過渡帶。其寬度 $\omega_s-\omega_p$ 決定濾波器長度 — 過渡越窄，抽頭越多。

Stopband ripple $\delta_s=10^{-A/20}$ where $A$ is stopband attenuation in dB. A spec of $A=60$ dB means $\delta_s=0.001$.

停帶漣波 $\delta_s=10^{-A/20}$，$A$ 為 dB 衰減。$A=60$ dB → $\delta_s=0.001$。
