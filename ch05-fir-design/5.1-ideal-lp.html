Stage 5-1 · The Ideal Lowpass Filter 關卡 5-1 · 理想低通濾波器 SASP §5.1 ↗

Pass everything below $\omega_c$, block everything above. On paper it is a rectangle in frequency. In time it is a sinc — which extends to infinity.

$\omega_c$ 以下全通、以上全擋。紙上是頻域的矩形，時域對應就是一條往兩邊無限延伸的 sinc。

IDEAL H(ω), h[n]
$$H_d(\omega)=\begin{cases}1,& |\omega|<\omega_c\\ 0,& \omega_c<|\omega|\le\pi\end{cases}\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad h_d[n]=\frac{\omega_c}{\pi}\,\mathrm{sinc}\!\left(\frac{\omega_c n}{\pi}\right)$$

Since $h_d[n]$ has infinite support and is non-causal, no finite-length real filter can realise it. Every FIR design method in this chapter is a trade-off for approximating it.

因為 $h_d[n]$ 無限長且非因果，任何有限長實濾波器都做不到。本章每個 FIR 設計方法都是在做某種取捨的近似。
