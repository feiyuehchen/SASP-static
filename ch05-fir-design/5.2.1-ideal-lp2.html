Stage 5-2-1 · Ideal Lowpass, Revisited 關卡 5-2-1 · 再看理想低通 SASP §5.2.1 ↗

Take the specification with $\omega_p=\omega_s=\omega_c$ and $\delta_p=\delta_s=0$. The transition band has zero width and no ripple is allowed. That is the ideal lowpass — now we can see why no finite-length filter can meet it: an infinite sinc would be needed.

把規格特殊化成 $\omega_p=\omega_s=\omega_c$ 且 $\delta_p=\delta_s=0$：過渡帶寬度為 0、不容許任何漣波。這正是理想低通 — 所以任何有限長濾波器達不到它：會要用無限長的 sinc。

Design methods trade among $\omega_p,\omega_s,\delta_p,\delta_s,N$ (filter length). The design problem is: given any four, minimise the fifth.

設計法的取捨就在 $\omega_p,\omega_s,\delta_p,\delta_s,N$（濾波器長度）之間。典型問題：給定四個量，最小化第五個。
