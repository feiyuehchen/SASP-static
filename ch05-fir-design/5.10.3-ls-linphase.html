Stage 5-10-3 · LS Linear-Phase FIR 關卡 5-10-3 · 最小平方線性相位 FIR SASP §5.10.3 ↗

Forcing symmetry on the coefficients collapses the $L^2$ optimisation to a single $\lceil N/2\rceil\times\lceil N/2\rceil$ linear system. No iterations, no Remez — just solve and ship.

對係數強制對稱，$L^2$ 最佳化就變成一個 $\lceil N/2\rceil$ 階的線性系統。不需要迭代、不需要 Remez — 解一次就好。

$$R\,\boldsymbol{a}=\boldsymbol{r},\quad R_{kl}=\int_{\Omega}\cos(k\omega)\cos(l\omega)W(\omega)\,d\omega,\quad r_k=\int_{\Omega}H_d(\omega)\cos(k\omega)W(\omega)\,d\omega$$

Where $\Omega$ is the union of the pass- and stopbands (not the transition band). Discretise the integrals, solve, read out the symmetric FIR.

$\Omega$ 是通帶 + 停帶的聯集（不含過渡帶）。把積分離散化、解線性系統、讀出對稱 FIR。
