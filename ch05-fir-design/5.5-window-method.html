Stage 5-5 · Window Method for FIR Design 關卡 5-5 · FIR 設計的視窗法 SASP §5.5 ↗

Step 1: compute $h_d[n]$ (the ideal infinite impulse response). Step 2: multiply by a taper window from World 4. Done.

步驟 1：算理想（無限長）脈衝響應 $h_d[n]$。步驟 2：乘上第 4 世界裡的收尾視窗。結束。

$$h[n]=w[n]\cdot h_d[n]$$

The convolution theorem tells us this smooths the ideal spectrum by the window's spectrum. Choose the window to trade main-lobe (transition-band) width for side-lobe depth (stopband attenuation):

卷積定理告訴我們：時域相乘 = 頻域卷積。視窗的頻譜決定過渡帶與停帶衰減的取捨：

  • Hann: ~44 dB attenuation, moderate transition.
  • Hann：約 44 dB 衰減、中等過渡。
  • Hamming: ~53 dB, similar transition.
  • Hamming：約 53 dB、過渡相近。
  • Blackman: ~75 dB, ~2× wider transition.
  • Blackman：約 75 dB、過渡寬約 2 倍。
  • Kaiser $\beta$: tunable for any target attenuation.
  • Kaiser $\beta$：可調以達任意目標衰減。
