Stage 5-5-2-2 · Window Method vs Optimal Chebyshev 關卡 5-5-2-2 · 視窗法對比最優 Chebyshev SASP §5.5.2.2 ↗

Kaiser-windowed FIR is typically within 2–3 dB of the optimal Chebyshev design for the same filter length. Given the implementation simplicity, it is usually the right first choice.

同長度下，Kaiser 視窗法的結果通常在最優 Chebyshev 2–3 dB 以內。實作簡單，通常是第一選擇。

You would upgrade to the Parks-McClellan / Remez minimax design (§5.10.2) when every last dB of stopband rejection matters or when you need a truly equi-ripple passband response.

真的在意最後幾 dB 的停帶衰減、或需要完全等漣波的通帶時，才升級到 Parks-McClellan / Remez minimax（§5.10.2）。
