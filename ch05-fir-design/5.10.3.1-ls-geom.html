Stage 5-10-3-1 · Geometric Interpretation of Least Squares 關卡 5-10-3-1 · 最小平方的幾何解釋 SASP §5.10.3.1 ↗

The $L^2$-optimal filter is the orthogonal projection of the ideal response onto the space of length-$N$ linear-phase FIR filters. Just like fitting a line in regression.

$L^2$ 最優濾波器 = 把理想響應正交投影到長度 $N$ 的線性相位 FIR 空間上。就像回歸裡擬合直線。

In this view the "basis vectors" are $\cos(k\omega)$ and the "inner product" is the weighted integral over $\Omega$. Filters beyond the space cannot be reached; projection is the closest you can get.

這個詮釋下，基底為 $\cos(k\omega)$，內積是 $\Omega$ 上的加權積分。空間之外的濾波器搆不到，投影給出最接近的那一個。
