CONTINUOUS TIME
$$\mathcal{H}\{x\}(t)=\frac{1}{\pi}\,\text{P.V.}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\frac{x(\tau)}{t-\tau}d\tau$$
FREQUENCY DOMAIN (THE EASY WAY)
$$X_H(j\Omega)=-j\operatorname{sgn}(\Omega)\cdot X(j\Omega)$$
DISCRETE IDEAL IMPULSE RESPONSE
$$h_H[n]=\begin{cases}\dfrac{2}{\pi n}, & n \text{ odd}\\ 0, & n \text{ even}\end{cases}$$

The DTFT of this ideal sequence is $-j\operatorname{sgn}(\omega)$ on $(-\pi,\pi)$ — exactly what we want. Notice the odd-$n$-only pattern; that means even-indexed taps are zero.

這條理想序列的 DTFT 就是在 $(-\pi,\pi)$ 上的 $-j\operatorname{sgn}(\omega)$，正是我們要的。只有奇數 $n$ 非零，偶數 tap 為零。
