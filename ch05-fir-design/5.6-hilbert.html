Stage 5-6 · Hilbert Transform Design 關卡 5-6 · Hilbert 轉換器設計 SASP §5.6 ↗

Design an FIR whose ideal frequency response is $-j\operatorname{sgn}(\omega)$ — i.e. shifts positive frequencies by $-90°$ and negative frequencies by $+90°$. Useful for analytic signal construction and single-sideband modulation.

設計理想頻率響應為 $-j\operatorname{sgn}(\omega)$ 的 FIR — 正頻率相位減 90°、負頻率相位加 90°。在分析訊號建構、SSB 調變中很有用。

Unlike the ideal lowpass, the Hilbert ideal has a jump at $\omega=0$ as well as at $\omega=\pm\pi$. FIR designs must tolerate the cusp while still delivering a wide useful pass band.

和理想低通不同，Hilbert 理想在 $\omega=0$ 和 $\omega=\pm\pi$ 都有跳變；FIR 設計必須忍受這些尖角、仍提供寬的可用通帶。
