Stage 5-10-6 · Non-Linear Phase FIR 關卡 5-10-6 · 非線性相位 FIR SASP §5.10.6 ↗

Linear phase is nice for audio (no waveform distortion), but for the same magnitude response a non-linear-phase FIR can be shorter, lower-latency, or both. Useful in low-latency live processing where phase fidelity is less critical than delay.

線性相位對音訊很好（不扭曲波形），但同樣振幅響應下，非線性相位 FIR 可以更短、延遲更低、甚至兩者兼具。低延遲即時處理時值得考慮。
