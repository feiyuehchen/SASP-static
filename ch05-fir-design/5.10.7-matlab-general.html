% Matlab: cfirpm for complex / non-linear-phase FIR
h = cfirpm(N-1, bands, @lowpass_fresp);

% Or use CVX for an explicit SOCP:
cvx_begin
  variable h(N) complex
  minimize(max(abs(H_grid * h - Hd_grid)))
cvx_end

CVX makes the SOCP readable. cfirpm is specialised and fast for common band specifications.
