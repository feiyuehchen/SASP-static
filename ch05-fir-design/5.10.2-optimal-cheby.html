Stage 5-10-2 · Optimal Chebyshev FIR 關卡 5-10-2 · 最優 Chebyshev FIR SASP §5.10.2 ↗

Minimise the maximum weighted error between the designed and desired responses. Characterised by the alternation theorem: the optimum has at least $N/2+2$ alternating equi-ripple extrema.

最小化目標與設計響應間加權誤差的最大值。由交替定理刻畫：最優解至少有 $N/2+2$ 個等漣波交替極值。

MINIMAX PROBLEM
$$\min_{h}\;\max_{\omega}\;W(\omega)\,|H_d(\omega)-H(\omega)|$$

$W(\omega)$ weights the error band-by-band: higher weight = lower error there. Matlab's firpm (Parks-McClellan) solves this by Remez exchange (§4.13.8). Output is optimal in the Chebyshev / minimax sense and is universally called "optimal FIR design".

$W(\omega)$ 逐頻帶加權誤差 — 權重越高、該帶誤差越小。Matlab 的 firpm 用 Remez 交換（§4.13.8）求解。 結果在 Chebyshev/minimax 意義下最優，俗稱「最優 FIR 設計」。
