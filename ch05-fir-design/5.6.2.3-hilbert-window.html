Lab · Windowed Hilbert response

實驗 · 視窗化 Hilbert 響應

Magnitude climbs from 0 near $\omega=0$, reaches ~1 in the useful band, and falls back near $\omega=\pi$. Wider useful band ⇒ narrower dips near 0 and π.

振幅從 $\omega=0$ 附近的 0 爬升、在可用頻段約為 1、在 $\omega=\pi$ 附近又下降。可用頻段越寬，0 與 π 附近的凹陷就越窄。
