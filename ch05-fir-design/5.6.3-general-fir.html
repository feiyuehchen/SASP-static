Stage 5-6-3 · More General FIR 關卡 5-6-3 · 更一般化的 FIR SASP §5.6.3 ↗

The same window recipe handles arbitrary targets: differentiators, multi-band EQs, pre-emphasis, fractional-delay filters. Recipe is always: ideal IR → window → done.

同一套視窗食譜適用任何目標：微分器、多頻帶 EQ、預強調、分數延遲濾波器。流程永遠是「理想 IR → 視窗 → 結束」。

The differentiator is especially common in audio: $H_d(\omega)=j\omega$ for $|\omega|<\omega_c$, $h_d[n]=\tfrac{(-1)^n}{n}$ for $n\ne 0$, $h_d[0]=0$. Window and go.

最常見的是微分器：$H_d(\omega)=j\omega$（$|\omega|<\omega_c$ 內），$h_d[n]=\tfrac{(-1)^n}{n}$（$n\ne 0$），$h_d[0]=0$。加窗即可。
