Stage 5-9 · Min-Phase & Causal Cepstra 關卡 5-9 · 最小相位與因果倒頻譜 SASP §5.9 ↗

A sequence is minimum-phase iff its complex cepstrum is causal (zero for $n<0$).

序列是最小相位，等價於其複倒頻譜為因果（$n<0$ 處為 0）。

COMPLEX CEPSTRUM
$$\hat{h}[n]=\mathcal{F}^{-1}\{\ln H(\omega)\}$$

Forcing $\hat h[n]=0$ for $n<0$ (and doubling the positive half to preserve magnitude) implements the "exponentiate back" step of the previous page. Numerically stable as long as $|H(\omega)|$ never vanishes (add a tiny floor if needed).

把 $\hat h[n]=0$（$n<0$）、正時間部分乘 2 保持振幅，就是上一頁的「指數還原」。只要 $|H(\omega)|$ 不為 0（若接近可加一個小地板）就數值穩定。
