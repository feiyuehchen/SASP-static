Stage 5-10-5 · More General Real FIR 關卡 5-10-5 · 更一般化的實 FIR SASP §5.10.5 ↗

Everything so far assumed a symmetric (linear-phase) FIR. If we drop that, the design space doubles but so does the flexibility — you can now shape magnitude and phase independently.

前面都假設對稱（線性相位）FIR。拿掉這限制後設計空間翻倍，但靈活性也翻倍 — 可以獨立塑造振幅與相位。

Complex filters (asymmetric real $h[n]$) still have real coefficients but their frequency response is complex with non-linear phase. Design via SOCP (§5.10.8) or non-linear optimisation (§5.10.9).

複濾波器（非對稱實 $h[n]$）係數仍為實，頻率響應卻是非線性相位的複數。用 SOCP（§5.10.8）或非線性最佳化（§5.10.9）設計。
