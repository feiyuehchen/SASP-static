Stage 5-7 · Generalized Window Method 關卡 5-7 · 廣義視窗法 SASP §5.7 ↗

When the desired spectrum is known only on a dense grid (not a formula), the "window method" generalises to: IFFT the desired magnitude, window the result.

若目標頻譜只能在密格點給出（無封閉式），視窗法可廣義為「對目標做 IFFT、再乘上視窗」。

$$h[n]=w[n]\cdot\underbrace{\text{IFFT}\{H_d(e^{j 2\pi k/M})\}[n]}_{\text{target IR}}$$

Works for multi-band equalisers, arbitrary-shaped filters, frequency-warped responses — anything you can sample. Zero-pad the grid to at least $2N$ before IFFT to avoid time-domain aliasing.

多頻帶 EQ、任意形狀濾波器、頻率扭曲響應都能用。把頻率格點至少補零到 $2N$ 再 IFFT，避免時域混疊。
