Stage 5-10-8 · Second-Order Cone Problems 關卡 5-10-8 · 二次錐問題 SASP §5.10.8 ↗

SOCP STANDARD FORM
$$\min_{x}\;c^{T}x\quad\text{s.t.}\quad \|A_i x+b_i\|_2\le d_i^{T}x+e_i,\;\; i=1,\ldots,m$$

Generalises LP (LP is the case where all Euclidean norms degenerate to scalars). Modern interior-point solvers (ECOS, MOSEK, Gurobi) do SOCP at roughly LP speed. Critical for complex-valued minimax FIR.

推廣自 LP（LP 是所有歐氏範數退化為純量的情形）。現代內點法求解器（ECOS、MOSEK、Gurobi）速度接近 LP。複值 minimax FIR 設計必備。
