Stage 5-5-2 · Bandpass Example 關卡 5-5-2 · 帶通範例 SASP §5.5.2 ↗

A bandpass ideal response is the difference of two lowpasses. Its impulse response is the difference of two sincs.

理想帶通 = 兩個低通之差。其脈衝響應 = 兩個 sinc 之差。

$$h_{BP}[n]=\frac{\sin(\omega_H n)-\sin(\omega_L n)}{\pi n}\quad\text{for }n\ne 0$$
