Stage 5-4 · Frequency-Sampling Method 關卡 5-4 · 頻率取樣法 SASP §5.4 ↗

Sample the target $H_d(\omega)$ at $N$ equally-spaced points and take the inverse DFT. The resulting FIR exactly matches the target at those frequencies — but ripples between them.

在 $N$ 個均勻頻率點上取樣目標 $H_d(\omega)$，反 DFT 得到 FIR 係數。這條濾波器在那些頻率上精確等於目標，但在中間會振盪。

FREQUENCY SAMPLING
$$h[n]=\frac{1}{N}\sum_{k=0}^{N-1}H_d\!\left(\frac{2\pi k}{N}\right)e^{j 2\pi k n/N}$$

Handy when the desired response is naturally specified on a frequency grid (e.g. graphic-EQ band gains). Not great for sharp transitions because it pins the response only at bin centres.

當目標響應本來就定義在頻率格點上時很好用（例如圖形等化器的頻帶增益）。對銳利過渡帶不理想，因為只在 bin 中心上被釘死。
