Some design goals — IIR filters, allpass sections, target magnitude AND group delay simultaneously — are not convex. Fall back to generic nonlinear optimisation: fminunc, fmincon, fminsearch in Matlab; scipy.optimize in Python.

某些設計目標不是凸的 — 例如 IIR、全通段、同時要求振幅與群延遲。改用一般非線性最佳化：Matlab 的 fminunc、fmincon、fminsearch；Python 的 scipy.optimize。

Trade-offs: solutions are only locally optimal; restart from several random seeds and keep the best. Gradient information (when available) speeds convergence dramatically.

取捨：解只是局部最優；從多個隨機起點試、留最好的。能提供梯度的話收斂會快很多。
