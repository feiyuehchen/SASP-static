Stage 5-10-1-2 · Filter Design using Lp Norms 關卡 5-10-1-2 · 用 Lp 範數設計濾波器 SASP §5.10.1.2 ↗

Iteratively Reweighted Least Squares (IRLS) solves the $L^p$ problem by repeated $L^2$ solves with adjusted weights. Converges to the $L^p$ minimum for $1\le p\le\infty$.

IRLS（迭代加權最小平方）把 $L^p$ 問題變成多次加權 $L^2$ 解。對 $1\le p\le\infty$ 可收斂到 $L^p$ 最優。

Each iteration updates the weights by the previous error magnitude to the power $p-2$, then resolves the weighted normal equations. Simple, robust, and flexible when you want a custom $p$.

每次迭代以前次誤差的 $p-2$ 次方作為新權重，重解加權 normal equation。 實作簡單、穩健、想要自訂 $p$ 時很靈活。
