Stage 5-6-2-2 · Kaiser in Hilbert Design 關卡 5-6-2-2 · Hilbert 設計中使用 Kaiser SASP §5.6.2.2 ↗

The Hilbert ideal has a discontinuity at both ends of the frequency axis. Choose the Kaiser β so that the error near those transitions stays under your target ripple.

Hilbert 理想在頻率軸兩端都有不連續，選 Kaiser β 讓這兩處的誤差都在目標漣波以下。

A useful band of Hilbert response lies between $\omega_l$ and $\pi-\omega_l$ for some cut-in $\omega_l$. Outside that band the FIR is allowed to drift from the ideal. Increasing β shrinks $\omega_l$ at the cost of more taps.

有用頻段介於 $\omega_l$ 與 $\pi-\omega_l$ 之間；這之外允許偏離理想。β 越大 $\omega_l$ 越小，但 tap 數增加。
