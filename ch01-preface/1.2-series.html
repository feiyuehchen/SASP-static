SASP is one volume in Julius Smith's open-access DSP book series hosted at CCRMA, Stanford.

SASP 是 Julius Smith 在史丹佛 CCRMA 開放存取的 DSP 書系之一。

The series at a glance

系列一覽

The four primary volumes — linked from the original site — each assume the previous one in rough order:

主要四卷，依序閱讀較為順暢：

  • Mathematics of the DFT — foundations: complex numbers, linear algebra, the DFT.
  • Mathematics of the DFT — 基礎：複數、線性代數、DFT。
  • Introduction to Digital Filters — LTI systems, transfer functions, stability, z-plane.
  • Introduction to Digital Filters — LTI 系統、轉移函數、穩定性、z 平面。
  • Physical Audio Signal Processing — digital waveguides, physical models of instruments and rooms.
  • Physical Audio Signal Processing — 數位波導、樂器與空間的物理模型。
  • Spectral Audio Signal Processing (this one) — STFT, windows, filter banks, spectral modeling.
  • Spectral Audio Signal Processing（本書） — STFT、視窗、濾波器組、頻譜建模。

All four are free to read online at ccrma.stanford.edu/~jos, along with additional shorter documents on physical modelling and virtual acoustics. If you are totally new to digital signal processing, starting with "Mathematics of the DFT" is recommended before SASP.

四卷都可在 ccrma.stanford.edu/~jos 上線上閱讀， 另有幾份關於物理建模與虛擬聲學的短篇文件。如果你完全是 DSP 新手，建議先讀 Mathematics of the DFT 再來 SASP。
