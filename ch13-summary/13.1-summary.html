Stage 13-1 · Summary & Conclusions關卡 13-1 · 總結與結論SASP §13 ↗

Twelve worlds, one thread: every spectral-audio method reduces to pick a basis + project + modify + reconstruct. DFT gave us the basis; windows and overlap-add controlled resolution/leakage; FFT filter banks and multirate polyphase formalised the idea at scale; STFT, SMS, MPEG, and wavelets each tuned the basis and tiling to their signal class. The theory below all of this is perfect reconstruction under a paraunitary polyphase matrix.

十二世界、一條主線：所有頻譜音訊方法都可化約為「選基底 → 投影 → 修改 → 重建」。DFT 給了基底；視窗與疊加決定解析度與洩漏；FFT 濾波器組與多取樣率把想法規模化；STFT、SMS、MPEG、小波各自為自身訊號類別調整了基底與時頻切法。底下的總理論則是「paraunitary 多相矩陣下的完美重建」。
