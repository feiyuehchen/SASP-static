Stage 12-9-1-3 · Normalized Fourier Basis (L²(ℝ))關卡 12-9-1-3 · 連續 Fourier 歸一化基底SASP §12.9.1.3 ↗

Continuous analogue: $e_\omega(t)=\tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}}e^{j\omega t}$ with $\langle u,v\rangle=\int u\bar v\,dt$. Not in $L^2(\mathbb{R})$, but works as a generalised (distributional) basis — the Fourier transform.

連續類比：$e_\omega(t)=\tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}}e^{j\omega t}$，內積 $\langle u,v\rangle=\int u\bar v\,dt$。雖不屬 $L^2(\mathbb{R})$ 但作為廣義（分佈意義）基底成立 — 就是連續傅立葉變換。
