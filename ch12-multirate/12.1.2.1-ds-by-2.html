For M=2, the alias sum is $\tfrac{1}{2}[X(e^{j\omega/2})+X(e^{j(\omega-2\pi)/2})]$. Any energy above $\pi/2$ in the original folds into $[0,\pi]$ — this is why an anti-alias lowpass at $\pi/2$ is mandatory before $\downarrow 2$.

M=2 時混疊為 $\tfrac{1}{2}[X(e^{j\omega/2})+X(e^{j(\omega-2\pi)/2})]$。原訊號超過 $\pi/2$ 的能量會折回 $[0,\pi]$ — 這就是 $\downarrow 2$ 前必須加 $\pi/2$ 低通抗混疊的原因。
