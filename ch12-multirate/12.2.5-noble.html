Stage 12-2-5 · Multirate Noble Identities關卡 12-2-5 · 多取樣率 Noble 恆等式SASP §12.2.5 ↗

Two priceless swaps: $H(z^M)\cdot\downarrow M = \downarrow M\cdot H(z)$ and $\uparrow L\cdot H(z^L)=H(z)\cdot\uparrow L$. Both let you push a filter across a rate change so it operates at the cheaper rate.

兩條寶貴恆等式：$H(z^M)\cdot\downarrow M = \downarrow M\cdot H(z)$ 與 $\uparrow L\cdot H(z^L)=H(z)\cdot\uparrow L$。兩者都能把濾波器推到取樣率變更的另一側，使它跑在較便宜的取樣率上。
