Stage 12-4 · Perfect Reconstruction Filter Banks關卡 12-4 · 完美重建濾波器組SASP §12.4 ↗

A filter bank is PR if $\hat x[n]=c\,x[n-d]$ for some scale $c$ and delay $d$. Achieving PR with non-trivial frequency selectivity requires careful joint design of all 2N (analysis+synthesis) filters.

若 $\hat x[n]=c\,x[n-d]$（某縮放 $c$、延遲 $d$），則濾波器組稱為完美重建。要同時兼顧頻率選擇性與 PR，必須一併設計 2N 個分析/合成濾波器。
