Stage 12-7-1 · Pseudo-QMF Cosine-Modulated FB關卡 12-7-1 · Pseudo-QMF 餘弦調變組SASP §12.7.1 ↗

Each of the 32 bands is a cosine-modulated version of one prototype lowpass: $h_k[n]=h[n]\cos(\tfrac{(2k+1)\pi}{64}(n-16))$. Aliasing between adjacent bands cancels to first order — acceptable for perceptual audio coding.

32 個頻帶都是同一原型低通的餘弦調變版：$h_k[n]=h[n]\cos(\tfrac{(2k+1)\pi}{64}(n-16))$。相鄰頻帶的混疊互相抵消至一階 — 對感知音訊編碼而言足夠。
