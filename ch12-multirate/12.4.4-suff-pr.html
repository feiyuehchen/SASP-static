Stage 12-4-4 · Sufficient Condition for PR關卡 12-4-4 · 完美重建的充分條件SASP §12.4.4 ↗

Sufficient condition: analysis polyphase $\mathbf{E}(z)$ and synthesis polyphase $\mathbf{R}(z)$ satisfy $\mathbf{R}(z)\mathbf{E}(z)=z^{-d}\mathbf{I}$. Design $\mathbf{R}$ as a (possibly non-causal, delayed) inverse of $\mathbf{E}$.

充分條件：分析多相 $\mathbf{E}(z)$ 與合成多相 $\mathbf{R}(z)$ 滿足 $\mathbf{R}(z)\mathbf{E}(z)=z^{-d}\mathbf{I}$。把 $\mathbf{R}$ 設計成 $\mathbf{E}$ 的（可能非因果、帶延遲的）反矩陣即可。
