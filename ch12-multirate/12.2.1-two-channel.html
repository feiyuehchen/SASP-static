Stage 12-2-1 · Two-Channel Polyphase Case關卡 12-2-1 · 兩通道多相分解SASP §12.2.1 ↗

For M=2, every filter splits into even-indexed $E_0(z^2)$ and odd-indexed $E_1(z^2)$ with $H(z)=E_0(z^2)+z^{-1}E_1(z^2)$. The two-channel case is the building block behind QMF, CQF, and Haar banks.

M=2 時每個濾波器都分成偶數項 $E_0(z^2)$ 和奇數項 $E_1(z^2)$：$H(z)=E_0(z^2)+z^{-1}E_1(z^2)$。兩通道情形是 QMF、CQF、Haar 濾波器組的基石。
