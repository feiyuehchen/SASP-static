Stage 12-9-1 · Geometric Signal Theory關卡 12-9-1 · 幾何訊號理論SASP §12.9.1 ↗

Every signal space transform is "project onto each basis vector". Fourier, DFT, STFT, DWT — all share the structure $X_k=\langle x,e_k\rangle$, just different $e_k$'s and different inner products.

每個訊號空間變換都是「投影到各基底向量」。Fourier、DFT、STFT、DWT — 骨架都是 $X_k=\langle x,e_k\rangle$，只是基底 $e_k$ 與內積各不相同。
