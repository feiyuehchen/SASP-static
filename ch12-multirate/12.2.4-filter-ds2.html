Stage 12-2-4 · Filter-then-Downsample (Revisited)關卡 12-2-4 · 再訪「先濾波再降取樣」SASP §12.2.4 ↗

Using polyphase, a lowpass-then-$\downarrow M$ can be implemented as $\downarrow M$ first, then M parallel sub-filters $E_k(z)$, combined by delay+sum. The sub-filters run at the low rate, saving a factor of M multiplies.

用多相觀點，低通接 $\downarrow M$ 可以改寫為：先 $\downarrow M$，再分成 M 個並行子濾波器 $E_k(z)$，用延遲加總合併。子濾波器都跑在低取樣率，直接省 M 倍乘法。
