Stage 12-3 · Critically Sampled PR Filter Banks關卡 12-3 · 臨界取樣完美重建組SASP §12.3 ↗

Critical sampling means the total output rate equals the input rate — no redundancy. Perfect reconstruction asks the synthesis to undo both aliasing from decimation and the analysis filters themselves, despite each channel being aliased.

臨界取樣指整體輸出率等於輸入率，不帶任何冗餘。完美重建要求合成能同時抵銷抽取產生的混疊與分析濾波器本身的影響 — 即使每個通道都被混疊了。
