World 12 · Multirate Filter Banks第 12 世界 · 多取樣率濾波器組SASP Ch 12 ↗

Multirate processing is the theoretical ground under every analysis bank we've seen: STFT, MPEG codecs, wavelets — they're all special cases of polyphase perfect-reconstruction filter banks. This world builds up the up/down-sampler algebra, polyphase matrices, paraunitary banks, and ends with wavelets as a dyadic specialisation.

多取樣率處理是前面所有分析組的理論基礎：STFT、MPEG 編解碼、小波 — 都是多相完美重建濾波器組的特例。本世界從升/降取樣代數、多相矩陣、paraunitary 組一路建起，最後以小波作為二元特例收尾。
