DWT implementation: iterate a 2-channel PR bank on the lowpass output only. After J levels you get 1 lowpass + J bandpasses, each at half the rate of the one above. Total work ≈ 2× one bank pass.

DWT 實作：對 2 通道完美重建組的低通輸出重複套用。經 J 層後得 1 個低通 + J 個帶通，每下一層取樣率減半。總運算量約為單次組通過的 2 倍。
