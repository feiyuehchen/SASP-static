Hop > 1 reduces redundancy and CPU. Pick hop $R\le N$: $R=N$ is critical sampling (no overlap); $R=N/2$ matches the common 50% OLA window. PR conditions depend on both filter shape and hop.

跳距 > 1 降低冗餘與計算量。選跳距 $R\le N$：$R=N$ 為臨界取樣（無重疊）；$R=N/2$ 對應常見 50% OLA。完美重建條件同時取決於濾波器形狀與跳距。
