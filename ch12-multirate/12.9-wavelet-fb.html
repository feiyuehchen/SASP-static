Stage 12-9 · Wavelet Filter Banks關卡 12-9 · 小波濾波器組SASP §12.9 ↗

Wavelets sacrifice uniform bandwidth for octave-band resolution: narrow filters for low frequencies (long window), wide filters for high frequencies (short window). Built from recursive 2-channel PR banks.

小波放棄均勻頻寬，換取按八度分頻的解析度：低頻用窄濾波器（長視窗），高頻用寬濾波器（短視窗）。由遞迴的 2 通道完美重建組堆出來。
