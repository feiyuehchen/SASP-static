Stage 12-2-3 · Type II Polyphase Decomposition關卡 12-2-3 · 第二型多相分解SASP §12.2.3 ↗

Type II reverses the indexing: $H(z)=\sum_{k=0}^{N-1} z^{-(N-1-k)} R_k(z^N)$. Useful for synthesis banks where the natural direction is reversed, and for expressing commutator structures cleanly.

第二型把索引反過來：$H(z)=\sum_{k=0}^{N-1} z^{-(N-1-k)} R_k(z^N)$。在合成組中方向天然相反，用它描述 commutator 結構會乾淨許多。
