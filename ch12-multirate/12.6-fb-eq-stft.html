Stage 12-6 · Filter Banks Equivalent to STFTs關卡 12-6 · 等價於 STFT 的濾波器組SASP §12.6 ↗

Any STFT is a uniform-modulated FB; conversely, any uniform modulated FB with appropriate window is an STFT. Portnoff generalised this to non-integer hops / longer windows via polyphase.

任意 STFT 就是均勻調變濾波器組；反之，任何均勻調變濾波器組配上合適視窗，都是一個 STFT。Portnoff 透過多相，把這對應推廣到非整數跳距、較長視窗。
