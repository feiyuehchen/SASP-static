Stage 12-3-8 · Orthogonal 2-Channel Filter Banks關卡 12-3-8 · 正交兩通道組SASP §12.3.8 ↗

Orthogonal banks have a unitary polyphase matrix: $\mathbf{E}^\dagger(e^{j\omega})\mathbf{E}(e^{j\omega})=\mathbf{I}$. Equivalently, $h_0$ and $h_1$ form an orthonormal basis for each translate. This is the doorway to orthogonal wavelets (Daubechies).

正交組的多相矩陣是么正的：$\mathbf{E}^\dagger(e^{j\omega})\mathbf{E}(e^{j\omega})=\mathbf{I}$。等價地，$h_0$ 與 $h_1$ 的所有平移構成正交基。這是通往正交小波（Daubechies）的大門。
