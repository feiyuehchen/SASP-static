Stage 12-2 · Polyphase Decomposition關卡 12-2 · 多相分解SASP §12.2 ↗

Any $H(z)$ can be split into M polyphase components $E_k(z^M)$, each carrying the samples at phase $k$ of M: $H(z)=\sum_{k=0}^{M-1} z^{-k} E_k(z^M)$. This lets you move filter work either before or after up/down-sampling.

任何 $H(z)$ 都可拆成 M 個多相分量 $E_k(z^M)$，每個只含相位 $k$ 的樣本：$H(z)=\sum_{k=0}^{M-1} z^{-k} E_k(z^M)$。有了它，濾波工作可以搬到升/降取樣的前後兩側。
