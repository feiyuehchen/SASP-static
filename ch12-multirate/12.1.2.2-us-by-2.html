Stage 12-1-2-2 · Example: Upsampling by 2關卡 12-1-2-2 · 範例：升取樣 2SASP §12.1.2.2 ↗

Upsample-by-2 gives zero-stuffed $[x_0,0,x_1,0,\ldots]$ whose spectrum has two identical copies of $X$ in $[0,2\pi)$. The second copy is an image; remove it with a lowpass at $\pi/2$ to complete the interpolation.

$\uparrow 2$ 產生零填充序列 $[x_0,0,x_1,0,\ldots]$，其頻譜在 $[0,2\pi)$ 內有兩份相同的 $X$。第二份就是「映像」；用 $\pi/2$ 低通去掉它就完成插值。
