Stage 12-1 · Upsampling and Downsampling關卡 12-1 · 升取樣與降取樣SASP §12.1 ↗

Two primitives unlock every multirate system. Upsampling inserts L-1 zeros between samples (stretch); downsampling by M throws M-1 out of every M samples away. Everything else — polyphase, QMF, wavelets — is built by wrapping filters around these.

兩個原語解鎖所有多取樣率系統：升取樣在每對樣本間插入 L-1 個零（拉伸），降取樣每 M 個樣本丟掉 M-1 個。之後的 polyphase、QMF、小波，都只是在這兩個操作外面包上濾波器。
