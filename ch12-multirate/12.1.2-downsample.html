Stage 12-1-2 · Downsampling (Decimation) Operator關卡 12-1-2 · 降取樣（抽取）算子SASP §12.1.2 ↗

Downsample by M keeps every M-th sample: $y[n]=x[nM]$. Spectrum: sum of M stretched aliases $\tfrac{1}{M}\sum_{k=0}^{M-1} X(e^{j(\omega-2\pi k)/M})$. Without an anti-alias filter, frequencies above $\pi/M$ fold back and destroy information.

降取樣 $\downarrow M$：只保留每第 M 個樣本 $y[n]=x[nM]$。頻譜為 M 個伸縮混疊之和 $\tfrac{1}{M}\sum_{k=0}^{M-1} X(e^{j(\omega-2\pi k)/M})$。若沒有抗混疊濾波，$\pi/M$ 以上的頻率會折回來毀掉訊號。
