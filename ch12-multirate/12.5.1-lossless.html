Stage 12-5-1 · Lossless Filters關卡 12-5-1 · 無損濾波器SASP §12.5.1 ↗

A SISO system is lossless iff $|H(e^{j\omega})|=1$ everywhere (all-pass). For MIMO banks the generalisation is paraunitary. Lossless systems preserve $\ell^2$-energy — the very property wavelet analysts demand.

單輸入單輸出系統為無損 iff $|H(e^{j\omega})|=1$（即全通）。多輸入多輸出組的對應概念就是 paraunitary。無損系統保持 $\ell^2$ 能量 — 這正是小波分析要求的性質。
