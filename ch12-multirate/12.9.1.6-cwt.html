Stage 12-9-1-6 · Continuous Wavelet Transform關卡 12-9-1-6 · 連續小波轉換SASP §12.9.1.6 ↗

CWT uses a two-parameter family: $\psi_{a,b}(t)=\tfrac{1}{\sqrt{a}}\psi(\tfrac{t-b}{a})$ — scale $a$, shift $b$. Analysis $W(a,b)=\langle x,\psi_{a,b}\rangle$. Massively redundant but gives a clean continuous picture.

CWT 用雙參數族：$\psi_{a,b}(t)=\tfrac{1}{\sqrt{a}}\psi(\tfrac{t-b}{a})$ — 尺度 $a$、平移 $b$。分析 $W(a,b)=\langle x,\psi_{a,b}\rangle$。極冗餘但給出乾淨的連續時頻圖像。
