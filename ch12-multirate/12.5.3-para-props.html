Stage 12-5-3 · Properties of Paraunitary FBs關卡 12-5-3 · Paraunitary 組的性質SASP §12.5.3 ↗

Key properties: (1) $|\det\mathbf{H}(z)|=1$ on the unit circle; (2) columns of $\mathbf{H}(e^{j\omega})$ are orthonormal; (3) synthesis $=$ para-conjugate of analysis; (4) energy exactly split across sub-bands.

主要性質：(1) 單位圓上 $|\det\mathbf{H}(z)|=1$；(2) $\mathbf{H}(e^{j\omega})$ 的各行互為正交；(3) 合成 = 分析的 para-共軛；(4) 能量在各子頻帶精確劃分。
