Stage 12-4-6 · Polyphase View of the STFT關卡 12-4-6 · STFT 的多相觀點SASP §12.4.6 ↗

The STFT is a special filter bank whose analysis polyphase $\mathbf{E}(z)$ is a diagonal matrix of window polyphase components times the DFT matrix. Seeing STFT through polyphase exposes why hop and window length jointly control PR.

STFT 是一種特殊濾波器組：它的分析多相矩陣 $\mathbf{E}(z)$ 等於「視窗多相分量的對角陣」乘上 DFT 矩陣。從多相觀點看 STFT，便知跳距與視窗長度共同決定了能否完美重建。
