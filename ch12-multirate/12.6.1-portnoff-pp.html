Stage 12-6-1 · Polyphase Analysis of Portnoff STFT關卡 12-6-1 · Portnoff STFT 的多相分析SASP §12.6.1 ↗

Portnoff's trick: windows longer than the FFT, implemented by folding time-aliased window segments into one FFT period via polyphase. Polyphase matrix is thus $\mathbf{W}_\text{aliased}\cdot\mathbf{F}$.

Portnoff 的招：視窗可比 FFT 長，透過多相把時間混疊的視窗段折進一個 FFT 週期。多相矩陣因此為 $\mathbf{W}_\text{aliased}\cdot\mathbf{F}$。
