Stage 12-3-6 · Linear-Phase QMF Banks關卡 12-3-6 · 線性相位 QMF 組SASP §12.3.6 ↗

Symmetric-FIR $H_0$ gives linear phase — visually aligned band-pass images without phase distortion. Cost: amplitude distortion is unavoidable unless $H_0$ is very short; modern designs trade length for flatness of $|T(e^{j\omega})|$.

對稱 FIR 的 $H_0$ 給線性相位 — 各頻帶影像視覺上不走樣。代價：振幅失真無法避免（除非 $H_0$ 極短）；現代設計就是用濾波器長度換 $|T(e^{j\omega})|$ 的平坦度。
