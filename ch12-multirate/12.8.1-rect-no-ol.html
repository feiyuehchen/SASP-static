Stage 12-8-1 · Rect Window, No Overlap關卡 12-8-1 · 矩形視窗、無重疊SASP §12.8.1 ↗

Non-overlapping rectangular STFT = block DFT. Polyphase matrix is the DFT itself; each bin is a lowpass centred at $k/N$ with sinc-shaped response and terrible side-lobes (-13 dB).

無重疊的矩形 STFT = 分塊 DFT。多相矩陣就是 DFT；每個 bin 是中心在 $k/N$、sinc 形狀、旁波瓣僅 −13 dB 的低通。
