Stage 12-9-1-9 · Dyadic Filter Banks關卡 12-9-1-9 · 二元濾波器組SASP §12.9.1.9 ↗

A dyadic filter bank has octave-band frequency tiling (bandwidths halving per level). Its time-frequency tiles are wide-and-short at high frequencies and narrow-and-tall at low frequencies — matching human hearing.

二元濾波器組按八度切頻（每下一層頻寬減半）。時頻格在高頻寬而短、在低頻窄而高 — 正好貼合人耳聽覺。
