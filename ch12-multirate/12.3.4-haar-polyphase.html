Stage 12-3-4 · Polyphase Decomposition of Haar關卡 12-3-4 · Haar 的多相分解SASP §12.3.4 ↗

For Haar, $E_{00}=E_{01}=E_{10}=1/\sqrt{2}$, $E_{11}=-1/\sqrt{2}$; the polyphase matrix $\mathbf{E}$ is literally $\tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\begin{pmatrix}1&1\\1&-1\end{pmatrix}$ — unitary, so Haar is paraunitary at heart.

Haar 的多相分量：$E_{00}=E_{01}=E_{10}=1/\sqrt{2}$、$E_{11}=-1/\sqrt{2}$；多相矩陣 $\mathbf{E}=\tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\begin{pmatrix}1&1\\1&-1\end{pmatrix}$ — 本身就是么正，所以 Haar 骨子裡是 paraunitary。
