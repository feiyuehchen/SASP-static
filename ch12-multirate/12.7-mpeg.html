Stage 12-7 · MPEG Filter Banks關卡 12-7 · MPEG 濾波器組SASP §12.7 ↗

MPEG-1/2 Layer I/II use a 32-band pseudo-QMF cosine-modulated FB; Layer III (mp3) cascades with an MDCT for finer resolution; AAC uses a 1024/128 MDCT directly. FB choice trades latency, resolution, and alias leakage.

MPEG-1/2 Layer I/II 用 32 頻帶 pseudo-QMF 餘弦調變組；Layer III（mp3）再串接 MDCT 提高解析度；AAC 直接用 1024/128 MDCT。組的選擇是在延遲、解析度、混疊洩漏之間取捨。
