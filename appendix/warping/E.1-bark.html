Stage E-1 · The Bark Frequency Scale關卡 E-1 · Bark 頻率尺度SASP §E.1 ↗

The Bark scale groups frequency into 24 "critical bands" of roughly equal perceptual importance. Bandwidths widen from ~100 Hz at low frequencies to several kHz above 10 kHz — matching the resolution of the basilar membrane.

Bark 尺度把頻率切成 24 個感知重要性相近的「臨界頻帶」。頻寬由低頻約 100 Hz 加寬至 10 kHz 以上幾 kHz — 與耳蝸基底膜的解析度相符。
