Appendix E · Bilinear Frequency Warping for Audio附錄 E · 音訊的雙線性頻率扭曲SASP Appendix E ↗

A single all-pass substitution $z^{-1}\to\tfrac{z^{-1}-\rho}{1-\rho z^{-1}}$ bends the linear-Hz frequency axis onto a perceptual (Bark or ERB) axis. This appendix derives the optimal $\rho^*(f_s)$, gives arctan closed-form approximations, analyses the bandwidth error, and walks an audio-EQ example.
