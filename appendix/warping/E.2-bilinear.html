Stage E-2 · The Bilinear Transform關卡 E-2 · 雙線性變換SASP §E.2 ↗

Bilinear: $s=\tfrac{2}{T}\tfrac{z-1}{z+1}$. Maps continuous-time jω axis to discrete unit circle monotonically, no aliasing. In multirate/warping context, used as an all-pass substitution $z^{-1}\to \tfrac{z^{-1}-\rho}{1-\rho z^{-1}}$ to reshape the frequency axis.

雙線性：$s=\tfrac{2}{T}\tfrac{z-1}{z+1}$，連續時間 $j\omega$ 軸單調地映射到離散單位圓，無混疊。在扭曲中用為全通代換 $z^{-1}\to \tfrac{z^{-1}-\rho}{1-\rho z^{-1}}$，重塑頻率軸。
