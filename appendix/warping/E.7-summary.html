Bottom line: a single all-pass substitution $z^{-1}\to\tfrac{z^{-1}-\rho}{1-\rho z^{-1}}$ with tuned $\rho^*(f_s)$ maps any digital audio filter onto a perceptual (Bark or ERB) frequency axis with under ~10% bandwidth error.

結語：單次全通代換 $z^{-1}\to\tfrac{z^{-1}-\rho}{1-\rho z^{-1}}$ 搭配調整過的 $\rho^*(f_s)$，即可把任意數位音訊濾波器映到感知（Bark 或 ERB）軸，頻寬誤差 < 10%。
