Stage E-5 · Equivalent Rectangular Bandwidth (ERB)關卡 E-5 · 等效矩形頻寬（ERB）SASP §E.5 ↗

ERB: modern refinement of Bark. Glasberg-Moore 1990 fit: $\mathrm{ERB}(f)=24.7(4.37f/1000+1)$ Hz. Smoother than Bark, preferred for auditory modelling and loudness algorithms.

ERB：Bark 的現代精化。Glasberg-Moore 1990 擬合 $\mathrm{ERB}(f)=24.7(4.37f/1000+1)$ Hz。比 Bark 平滑，聽覺建模與響度演算法多用之。
