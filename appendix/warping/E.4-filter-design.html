Stage E-4 · Application to Audio Filter Design關卡 E-4 · 在音訊濾波器設計上的應用SASP §E.4 ↗

Design any digital filter normally, then apply the all-pass bilinear substitution. Result: frequency response warped onto the Bark axis — equaliser bands, perceptual masks, auditory front-ends.

先用一般方法設計數位濾波器，再套入全通雙線性代換。結果：頻率響應被扭曲到 Bark 軸 — 等化器頻帶、感知遮蔽、聽覺前端皆可直接用。
