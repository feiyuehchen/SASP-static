Pre-emphasis (+6 dB/oct) flattens the spectrum so low-amplitude high-frequency partials aren't masked in peak detection. DC removal and high-pass for rumble are standard. Restore spectrum on synthesis.

預加強（+6 dB/oct）把頻譜拉平，避免低振幅高頻分音在峰值偵測時被掩蓋。去 DC 與低頻雜訊的高通是標準步驟。合成時再還原頻譜。
