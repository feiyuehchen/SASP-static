Stage H-1 · Choice of Hop Size關卡 H-1 · 跳距選擇SASP §H.1 ↗

PARSHL analyses input in overlapping frames; hop $R$ controls time resolution. Too small = CPU waste; too large = missed transients. Typical default $R=N/4$ for Hamming window (75% overlap).

PARSHL 以重疊 frame 分析；跳距 $R$ 控制時間解析度。太小浪費 CPU，太大會錯失瞬態。Hamming 視窗常見預設 $R=N/4$（75% 重疊）。
