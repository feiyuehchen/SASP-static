Stage D-13 · Sums of Gaussian RVs關卡 D-13 · 高斯隨機變數之和SASP §D.13 ↗

Linear combinations of joint Gaussians are Gaussian. If $X\sim\mathcal{N}(\mu_X,\sigma_X^2)$ independent of $Y\sim\mathcal{N}(\mu_Y,\sigma_Y^2)$, then $X+Y\sim\mathcal{N}(\mu_X+\mu_Y,\sigma_X^2+\sigma_Y^2)$.

聯合高斯的線性組合仍為高斯。若 $X\sim\mathcal{N}(\mu_X,\sigma_X^2)$ 獨立於 $Y\sim\mathcal{N}(\mu_Y,\sigma_Y^2)$，則 $X+Y\sim\mathcal{N}(\mu_X+\mu_Y,\sigma_X^2+\sigma_Y^2)$。
