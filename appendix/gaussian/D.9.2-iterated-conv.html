Stage D-9-2 · Iterated Convolutions關卡 D-9-2 · 迭代卷積SASP §D.9.2 ↗

Repeatedly convolving any reasonable PDF with itself approaches a Gaussian (after re-standardisation). Spectrally: repeated multiplication of the Fourier transform raises its smooth features and flattens tails.

反覆把任意合理 PDF 自卷積後（再標準化）會收斂到高斯。頻域上：FT 被反覆相乘，平滑特徵凸顯、尾部被壓平。
