Stage D-9-1 · Central Limit Theorem關卡 D-9-1 · 中央極限定理SASP §D.9.1 ↗

For IID RVs with finite variance, the standardised sum $\tfrac{S_n-n\mu}{\sigma\sqrt n}$ converges in distribution to $\mathcal{N}(0,1)$. Explains why measurement noise is almost always Gaussian.

IID 且變異數有限時，標準化和 $\tfrac{S_n-n\mu}{\sigma\sqrt n}$ 分佈收斂到 $\mathcal{N}(0,1)$。解釋為何量測噪音幾乎總是高斯。
