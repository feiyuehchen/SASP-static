Stage D-2-1 · Product of Two Gaussian PDFs關卡 D-2-1 · 兩高斯 PDF 相乘SASP §D.2.1 ↗

Treating two Gaussians as likelihoods and multiplying gives a new Gaussian whose mean is the precision-weighted mean and variance is the harmonic mean. Bayesian-fusion fundamental.

把兩高斯當作似然函數相乘，得到新高斯：均值為精度加權均值、變異數為調和平均。貝氏融合的根本。
