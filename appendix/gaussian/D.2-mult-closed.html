Stage D-2 · Gaussians Closed under Multiplication關卡 D-2 · 高斯對乘法封閉SASP §D.2 ↗

Product of two Gaussians is another (unnormalised) Gaussian. Centres combine via precision-weighted averaging; variances combine by parallel-connection: $1/\sigma^2=1/\sigma_1^2+1/\sigma_2^2$.

兩高斯相乘仍為（未歸一化）高斯。中心以精度加權平均組合；變異數以並聯方式結合：$1/\sigma^2=1/\sigma_1^2+1/\sigma_2^2$。
