$\mathcal{F}\{e^{-\alpha t^2}\}=\sqrt{\pi/\alpha}\,e^{-\omega^2/(4\alpha)}$ for $\Re\alpha>0$. With imaginary $\alpha$ (chirp), maps to a scaled/reversed chirp — basis of chirplet analysis.
