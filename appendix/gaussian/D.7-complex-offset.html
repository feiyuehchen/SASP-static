$\int e^{-(t-c)^2/(2\sigma^2)}\,dt = \sigma\sqrt{2\pi}$ even for complex $c$ (Cauchy's theorem: shift contour back onto real axis). Powers the FT of a shifted/modulated Gaussian.

即使 $c$ 為複數，$\int e^{-(t-c)^2/(2\sigma^2)}\,dt = \sigma\sqrt{2\pi}$ 仍成立（Cauchy 定理：輪廓可回到實軸）。支撐位移/調變高斯的 FT 計算。
