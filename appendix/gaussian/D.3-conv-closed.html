Stage D-3 · Gaussians Closed under Convolution關卡 D-3 · 高斯對卷積封閉SASP §D.3 ↗

Convolving two Gaussians gives a Gaussian whose mean is the sum of means and variance is the sum of variances. Basis of the Central Limit Theorem for IID sums.

兩高斯卷積仍為高斯：均值相加、變異數相加。IID 總和中央極限定理的基礎。
