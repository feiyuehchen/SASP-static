Stage D-12-3 · Higher-Order Moments關卡 D-12-3 · 高階矩SASP §D.12.3 ↗

For zero-mean Gaussian, odd central moments = 0; even $2k$-th $=(2k-1)!!\,\sigma^{2k}$. General multivariate: Isserlis / Wick's theorem — sum over all pairings of $2k$ indices.

零中心高斯：奇階中心矩 = 0；偶階 $2k$ 階 $=(2k-1)!!\,\sigma^{2k}$。多維一般化為 Isserlis / Wick 定理 — 對 $2k$ 個索引的所有配對求和。
