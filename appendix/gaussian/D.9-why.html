Stage D-9 · Why Gaussian?關卡 D-9 · 為何是高斯？SASP §D.9 ↗

Three reasons: (1) CLT — sums of IID RVs converge to Gaussian; (2) max-entropy under fixed mean+variance; (3) self-dual under FT, unique achiever of the uncertainty-principle lower bound.

三個理由：(1) 中央極限定理 — IID 隨機變數之和收斂到高斯；(2) 在指定均值/變異數下最大熵；(3) FT 下自對偶，唯一達到測不準下界。
