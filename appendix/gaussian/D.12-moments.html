Stage D-12 · Gaussian Moments關卡 D-12 · 高斯的矩SASP §D.12 ↗

All higher moments of a Gaussian are polynomial functions of $\mu$ and $\sigma^2$. This over-determination is why Gaussian is uniquely characterised by its first two moments.

高斯的所有高階矩都是 $\mu$ 與 $\sigma^2$ 的多項式函數。這種冗餘讓高斯被前兩階矩唯一決定。
