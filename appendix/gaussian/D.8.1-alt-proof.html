Alternate derivation: $G(\omega)$ satisfies $G'(\omega)=-\tfrac{\omega}{\alpha}\cdot\tfrac{1}{2}\,G(\omega)$ — a first-order ODE with solution $G(\omega)=G(0)e^{-\omega^2/(4\alpha)}$. $G(0)$ from real-axis integral closes the proof.

另一推導：$G(\omega)$ 滿足一階 ODE $G'(\omega)=-\tfrac{\omega}{2\alpha}G(\omega)$，解為 $G(\omega)=G(0)e^{-\omega^2/(4\alpha)}$，用實軸積分值代入 $G(0)$ 即完證。
