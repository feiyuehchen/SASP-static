Stage G-1 · Bernoulli's Modal Decomposition關卡 G-1 · 白努利的模態分解SASP §G.1 ↗

Daniel Bernoulli (1753) observed that a vibrating string's motion is a sum of sinusoidal modes — the first additive-synthesis principle, 70 years before Fourier's mathematics made it rigorous.

Daniel Bernoulli（1753）觀察到振動弦的運動是正弦模態之和 — 第一個加法合成原理，比傅立葉讓它數學嚴謹還早 70 年。
