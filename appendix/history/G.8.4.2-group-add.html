Instead of one envelope per partial, group partials sharing similar evolutions and drive them with a shared amplitude function. Trade-off: fewer controls, much less CPU, slight loss of expressivity.

不為每個分音配一個包絡，而是把變化相似的分音分組，共用一個振幅函數。取捨：控制減少、CPU 大減、表達力稍損。
