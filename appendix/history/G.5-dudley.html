Stage G-5 · Dudley's Channel Vocoder關卡 G-5 · Dudley 的頻道聲碼器SASP §G.5 ↗

Homer Dudley's 1939 Vocoder at Bell Labs decomposed speech into a small number of spectral envelopes + excitation, transmitted them over low-bandwidth telephone lines, and resynthesised on the other end.

Homer Dudley 1939 年在貝爾實驗室的 Vocoder：把語音拆成少量頻譜包絡 + 激勵，透過低頻寬電話線傳輸，再於另一端重新合成。
