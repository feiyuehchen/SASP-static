The sines+noise decomposition (Serra 1989 thesis) became the reference model for sustained tones. Wind-instrument breath noise, string bow noise, brass hiss — all cleanly separated from tonal partials.

正弦 + 雜訊分解（Serra 1989 博論）成為持續音的參考模型。木管的吹氣聲、弦樂弓噪、銅管嘶聲都能與音高分音乾淨分離。
