Adds a transient layer on top of sines+noise: sparse, event-based clicks and onsets modelled separately so time-scale modification doesn't smear them. Essential for percussive TSM.

在正弦 + 雜訊上再加一層瞬態：稀疏、事件式的 click 與起音獨立建模，時間伸縮時不會被抹掉。打擊類 TSM 必備。
