Amplitude and frequency envelopes are very slowly varying — downsample by 10–100× for storage or transmission. Reconstruction upsamples with linear interpolation; quality loss imperceptible.

振幅與頻率包絡變化極慢 — 以 10–100× 降取樣儲存或傳輸。重建時以線性內插升取樣；品質損失不可察。
