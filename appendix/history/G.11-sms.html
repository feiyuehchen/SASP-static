Stage G-11 · Spectral Modeling Synthesis (History)關卡 G-11 · 頻譜建模合成（歷史）SASP §G.11 ↗

Serra's 1987 SMS combined peak-picking sinusoidal modelling with residual noise modelling — the first widely adopted sines+noise framework. Open-source software (SMS Tools) still maintained.

Serra 1987 年的 SMS 將峰值挑選式正弦建模與殘差雜訊建模結合 — 最早被廣泛採納的「正弦+雜訊」框架。開源軟體 SMS Tools 至今仍維護。
