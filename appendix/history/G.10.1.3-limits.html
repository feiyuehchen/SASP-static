Main limit: bin centres are fixed; a partial that slowly drifts across a bin boundary creates a discontinuity in the per-bin instantaneous frequency estimate. Peak-picking (SMS) fixes this.

主要限制：bin 中心固定；跨越 bin 邊界而緩慢漂移的分音會在每個 bin 的瞬時頻率估計中造成不連續。峰值挑選式 SMS 才能解決。
