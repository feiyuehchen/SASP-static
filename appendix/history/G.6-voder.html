Voder: Dudley's keyboard-driven synthesiser demo'd at the 1939 New York World's Fair. A trained operator "played" speech from a keyboard — the first human-playable speech synthesiser.

Voder：Dudley 於 1939 年紐約世博會展示的「以鍵盤演奏語音」合成器。操作員需經訓練才能「彈出」話語 — 第一個可由人即時演奏的語音合成器。
