Stage G-8-3 · Nonparametric Spectral Peak Modeling關卡 G-8-3 · 非參數頻譜峰值建模SASP §G.8.3 ↗

Instead of fitting a parametric model to each peak, store the raw magnitude/phase around each peak and resynthesise by inverse-STFT. Higher fidelity at the cost of larger data.

不對每個峰值擬合參數模型，而是保留峰值周圍原始振幅/相位，以逆 STFT 重建。保真度更高，代價是資料量更大。
