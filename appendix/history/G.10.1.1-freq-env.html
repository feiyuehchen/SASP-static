Connect bin-wise instantaneous frequencies across frames to get smooth per-partial frequency envelopes. Continuity assumption: adjacent frames' frequencies within the same bin are the same partial.

連接跨 frame 的 bin 瞬時頻率，得到平滑的分音頻率包絡。連續性假設：相鄰 frame 同 bin 的頻率屬同一分音。
