Stage G-4 · The Hammond Organ關卡 G-4 · Hammond 管風琴SASP §G.4 ↗

Laurens Hammond's 1935 organ used 91 tonewheels + drawbars to combine 9 harmonics per note — gritty, playable additive synthesis that defined gospel, jazz, and rock organ sounds.

Laurens Hammond 1935 年的管風琴用 91 個音輪與 drawbar，每個音可混合 9 個諧波 — 具「粗糙感」且可即時演奏的加法合成，定義了 gospel、jazz、rock 管風琴音色。
