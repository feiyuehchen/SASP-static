Stage B-17-2 · Time-Limited Signals關卡 B-17-2 · 有限時長訊號SASP §B.17.2 ↗

A signal strictly zero outside $[a,b]$ cannot be band-limited — its spectrum has infinite extent. Conversely, band-limited signals are infinite-duration. The classical impossibility theorem.

嚴格在 $[a,b]$ 外為零的訊號不可能頻帶受限 — 其頻譜必然無限延伸。反之亦然。經典的不可能性定理。
