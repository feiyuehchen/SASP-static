Stage B-3 · Differentiation Theorem Dual關卡 B-3 · 微分定理對偶SASP §B.3 ↗

Dual: multiplication by $-jt$ in time ↔ differentiation in frequency: $-jt\,x(t)\leftrightarrow \tfrac{d}{d\omega}X(\omega)$. Used to compute derivatives of spectra and to generate orthogonal basis functions (e.g., Hermite–Gaussians).

對偶版：時域乘 $-jt$ ↔ 頻域微分：$-jt\,x(t)\leftrightarrow \tfrac{d}{d\omega}X(\omega)$。用來計算頻譜導數、產生正交基底（如 Hermite–Gauss）。
