Stage B-2 · Differentiation Theorem關卡 B-2 · 微分定理SASP §B.2 ↗

If $x(t)\leftrightarrow X(\omega)$, then $\tfrac{d}{dt}x(t)\leftrightarrow j\omega X(\omega)$. Differentiation in time is multiplication by $j\omega$ in frequency — the reason FT turns ODEs into algebra.

若 $x(t)\leftrightarrow X(\omega)$，則 $\tfrac{d}{dt}x(t)\leftrightarrow j\omega X(\omega)$。時域微分 = 頻域乘 $j\omega$ — 這就是 FT 把 ODE 變成代數的原因。
