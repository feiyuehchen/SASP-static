Stage B-1 · Radians versus Cycles關卡 B-1 · 弧度 vs. 週期SASP §B.1 ↗

Radian frequency $\omega$ (rad/s) vs. cyclic frequency $f$ (Hz) related by $\omega=2\pi f$. Radians are natural for calculus (derivatives of $e^{j\omega t}$); cycles are natural for instruments. Mis-choosing hides factors of $2\pi$ in transform definitions — a major source of bugs.

角頻率 $\omega$（rad/s）與頻率 $f$（Hz）透過 $\omega=2\pi f$ 換算。微積分裡弧度自然（$e^{j\omega t}$ 求導乾淨），工程儀器則用赫茲。選錯單位會讓變換定義裡的 $2\pi$ 跑來跑去 — 是 bug 大宗。
