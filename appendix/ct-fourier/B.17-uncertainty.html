Stage B-17 · Uncertainty Principle關卡 B-17 · 測不準原理SASP §B.17 ↗

For any finite-energy signal, $\Delta t\cdot \Delta\omega\ge \tfrac{1}{2}$ where $\Delta t,\Delta\omega$ are rms widths. You cannot localise simultaneously in time and frequency. Gaussian achieves equality; other shapes are strictly worse.

任意有限能量訊號，$\Delta t\cdot \Delta\omega\ge \tfrac{1}{2}$（$\Delta t,\Delta\omega$ 為均方根寬度）。時間與頻率無法同時任意集中；高斯剛好取等號，其他形狀都比它更差。
