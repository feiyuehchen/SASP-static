Stage B-6 · Modulation Theorem (Shift Dual)關卡 B-6 · 調變定理（位移對偶）SASP §B.6 ↗

Dual of shift: $e^{j\omega_c t}\,x(t)\leftrightarrow X(\omega-\omega_c)$. Multiplying by a complex exponential shifts the spectrum by $\omega_c$ — the basis of heterodyning, AM, SSB, mixers.

位移定理的對偶：$e^{j\omega_c t}\,x(t)\leftrightarrow X(\omega-\omega_c)$。乘上複指數使頻譜平移 $\omega_c$ — 超外差、AM、SSB、混頻器的基礎。
