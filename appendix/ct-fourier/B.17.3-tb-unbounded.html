Stage B-17-3 · Time-Bandwidth Products Unbounded Above關卡 B-17-3 · 時頻寬乘積上不受限SASP §B.17.3 ↗

Only the lower bound $\Delta t\Delta\omega\ge 1/2$ is tight — the product can be arbitrarily large. Example: high-variance two-mode distribution sharing little density around the mean.

只有下界 $\Delta t\Delta\omega\ge 1/2$ 緊束；上界可任意大。例：平均值附近密度稀薄的高變異雙峰分佈。
