Stage B-18 · Smoothness ↔ Roll-Off Rate關卡 B-18 · 平滑度 ↔ 滾降速率SASP §B.18 ↗

If $x(t)$ has $k$ continuous derivatives, $|X(\omega)|$ decays faster than $1/|\omega|^k$. Smoothness in one domain ⇔ fast decay in the other. Applies to window design: smoother windows = lower-side-lobe spectra.

若 $x(t)$ 有 $k$ 階連續導數，$|X(\omega)|$ 衰減快於 $1/|\omega|^k$。一域平滑 ⇔ 另一域快速衰減。直接對應視窗設計：更平滑的視窗 = 旁波瓣更低的頻譜。
