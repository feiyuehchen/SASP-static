Stage C-3-3-1 · Sample-Mean Variance關卡 C-3-3-1 · 樣本平均的變異數SASP §C.3.3.1 ↗

For IID samples $\mathrm{Var}(\hat\mu_N)=\sigma^2/N$. For correlated data, replace by $\sigma^2\cdot N_{\text{eff}}^{-1}$ where $N_{\text{eff}}$ is the effective sample count — often much smaller than $N$.

IID 下 $\mathrm{Var}(\hat\mu_N)=\sigma^2/N$。相關資料則改為 $\sigma^2\cdot N_{\text{eff}}^{-1}$，$N_{\text{eff}}$ 為有效樣本數，通常遠小於 $N$。
