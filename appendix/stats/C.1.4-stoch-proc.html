Stage C-1-4 · Stochastic Process關卡 C-1-4 · 隨機過程SASP §C.1.4 ↗

A stochastic process $\{X_n\}$ is a collection of RVs, one per time index. Its statistics are the joint distributions of arbitrary finite subsets. Almost all signals we analyse in this book are modelled this way.

隨機過程 $\{X_n\}$ 是「每個時間索引一個隨機變數」的集合。其統計性質由任意有限子集的聯合分佈決定。本書幾乎所有訊號都用此模型。
