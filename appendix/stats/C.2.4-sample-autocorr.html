Stage C-2-4 · Sample Autocorrelation關卡 C-2-4 · 樣本自相關SASP §C.2.4 ↗

Biased: $\hat r_x[\ell]=\tfrac{1}{N}\sum x[n]x^*[n-\ell]$; unbiased divides by $N-|\ell|$. Biased has positive-semi-definiteness (important for PSD); unbiased has lower bias but higher variance.

偏置估計：$\hat r_x[\ell]=\tfrac{1}{N}\sum x[n]x^*[n-\ell]$；無偏則除以 $N-|\ell|$。偏置估計為半正定（對 PSD 很重要）；無偏估計偏誤較小但變異數大。
