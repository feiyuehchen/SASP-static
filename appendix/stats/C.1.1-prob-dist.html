Stage C-1-1 · Probability Distribution關卡 C-1-1 · 機率分佈SASP §C.1.1 ↗

For real-valued $X$: CDF $F_X(x)=P(X\le x)$, PDF $p_X(x)=F_X'(x)$. Events are measured via $P(X\in A)=\int_A p_X(x)\,dx$. Discrete case uses PMF $p_X[x_k]$.

實數 $X$：CDF $F_X(x)=P(X\le x)$，PDF $p_X(x)=F_X'(x)$。事件機率 $P(X\in A)=\int_A p_X(x)\,dx$。離散則用 PMF $p_X[x_k]$。
