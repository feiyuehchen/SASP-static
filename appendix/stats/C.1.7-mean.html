Mean $\mu_X=E\{X\}$. For WSS processes, $\mu_n=\mu$ (constant). The mean is often removed before spectral analysis so that PSDs describe fluctuation energy only.

平均值 $\mu_X=E\{X\}$。WSS 過程其 $\mu_n=\mu$（常數）。頻譜分析前常扣除平均值，讓 PSD 只描述起伏能量。
