Periodogram: $\hat S_x(\omega_k)=\tfrac{1}{N}|X[k]|^2$. Asymptotically unbiased but variance does NOT decrease with $N$ — the cardinal sin of spectral estimation, fixed by averaging (Welch/Bartlett) or smoothing.

Periodogram：$\hat S_x(\omega_k)=\tfrac{1}{N}|X[k]|^2$。漸近無偏但變異數不隨 $N$ 減小 — 頻譜估計的大坑，靠平均（Welch/Bartlett）或平滑化解決。
