Stage C-1-5 · Stationary Stochastic Process關卡 C-1-5 · 平穩隨機過程SASP §C.1.5 ↗

Strictly stationary = all finite joint distributions are shift-invariant. Wide-sense stationary (WSS) = constant mean + autocorrelation depends only on lag. WSS is what practical spectral analysis requires.

嚴格平穩 = 所有有限聯合分佈皆對平移不變。寬平穩（WSS）= 均值常數且自相關只依賴時延。實務頻譜分析靠 WSS 即可。
