$E\{g(X)\}=\int g(x) p_X(x)\,dx$ (or sum, discrete case). Linearity: $E\{aX+bY\}=aE\{X\}+bE\{Y\}$. Takes random functions and returns deterministic numbers — the bridge from randomness to analysis.
