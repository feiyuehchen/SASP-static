$\sigma^2=E\{(X-\mu)^2\}$. Measures spread; square root is standard deviation $\sigma$. For WSS: variance $=$ autocorrelation at lag 0 $=$ total PSD area divided by $2\pi$.

$\sigma^2=E\{(X-\mu)^2\}$。衡量離散程度；其平方根為標準差 $\sigma$。WSS 時變異數 = 零延遲自相關 = PSD 總面積除 $2\pi$。
