Stage C-1-3 · Random Variable關卡 C-1-3 · 隨機變數SASP §C.1.3 ↗

An RV $X$ is a measurable function from a sample space $\Omega$ to $\mathbb{R}$ (or $\mathbb{C}$). Its distribution follows from pushing $P$ forward through $X$. RVs let us treat measurements as mathematical objects.

隨機變數 $X$ 是從樣本空間 $\Omega$ 到 $\mathbb{R}$（或 $\mathbb{C}$）的可測函數。分佈由 $X$ 把機率 $P$ 推送過去得到。隨機變數把量測當作數學物件處理。
