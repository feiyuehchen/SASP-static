Stage 4-4-1 · Rect-Windowed Oboe 關卡 4-4-1 · 矩形加窗的雙簧管 SASP §4.4.1 ↗

Strong harmonics show up fine. But the spaces between harmonics fill up to about $-13$ dB — that is the side-lobe leakage of the rectangular window.

強諧波看得清楚；但諧波之間的凹谷被填到約 $-13$ dB — 那是矩形視窗的旁瓣洩漏。
