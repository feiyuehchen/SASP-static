Stage 4-4 · Oboe Tone Spectrum Analysis 關卡 4-4 · 雙簧管音的頻譜分析 SASP §4.4 ↗

Real-world example. An oboe tone is highly harmonic — many partials at integer multiples of the fundamental. The spectrum tells us which harmonics are loud, but leakage from the analysis window can obscure the quieter ones.

實戰案例。雙簧管是高諧波訊號 — 許多partial 在基頻整數倍處。頻譜告訴我們哪些諧波強，但分析視窗的洩漏可能蓋住較弱的。

Analysis setup

分析設定

The following three pages compare three window choices on a synthetic oboe-like tone. We generate $8$ harmonics of a $200$ Hz fundamental at $f_s = 8000$ Hz with slowly decreasing amplitudes, window $512$ samples, zero-pad to $4096$, take the FFT, plot dB magnitude.

接下來三頁比較三種視窗在仿雙簧管音上：$f_s = 8000$ Hz 下 $200$ Hz 基頻的 $8$ 個諧波、振幅漸減；取 $512$ 樣本加窗、補零到 $4096$、FFT、畫 dB。

Fundamental: $f_0 = 200$ Hz → first peak at bin $200/(8000/4096) \approx 102$.
Harmonic amplitudes: $[1, 0.6, 0.35, 0.2, 0.1, 0.05, 0.02, 0.01]$ — span of $40$ dB.
Weakest harmonic: at $1600$ Hz, $-40$ dB. The analysis question: can the window's side-lobe floor stay below $-40$ dB so we can see this harmonic cleanly? 基頻：$f_0 = 200$ Hz → 第一個峰在 bin $200/(8000/4096) \approx 102$。
諧波振幅：$[1, 0.6, 0.35, 0.2, 0.1, 0.05, 0.02, 0.01]$ — 跨 $40$ dB。
最弱諧波：在 $1600$ Hz、$-40$ dB。分析問題：視窗旁瓣底能否低於 $-40$ dB，讓這個諧波看得清楚？

Window choice shows up as a practical limit. Rect leaks at $-13$ dB — the 8th harmonic disappears. Hamming reaches $-43$ dB — just barely resolves the 8th. Blackman at $-58$ dB gives $18$ dB of headroom. Pick as much headroom as you can afford.

視窗選擇直接轉成實務上限。矩形 $-13$ dB 洩漏 — 第 8 諧波消失。Hamming $-43$ dB — 勉強看得到。Blackman $-58$ dB 多 $18$ dB 餘裕。在算力允許下選越深越好。

問：最弱諧波在 $-40$ dB。用矩形視窗（第一旁瓣 $-13$ dB），你會：

Lab · All three windows side by side

實驗 · 三種視窗並排

Three dB spectra overlaid. Blue: rect (tallest side-lobe leakage at $-13$ dB). Yellow: Hamming ($-43$ dB). Pink: Blackman ($-58$ dB). The 8 peaks are at $200, 400, \ldots, 1600$ Hz. Count how many you can cleanly distinguish from the leakage noise below.

三組 dB 頻譜疊加：藍=矩形（旁瓣洩漏 $-13$ dB）、黃=Hamming（$-43$ dB）、粉=Blackman（$-58$ dB）。8 個峰在 $200, 400, \ldots, 1600$ Hz。數看看哪種視窗下能乾淨看到幾個。
