Stage 4-4 · Oboe Tone Spectrum Analysis 關卡 4-4 · 雙簧管音的頻譜分析 SASP §4.4 ↗

Real-world example. An oboe tone is highly harmonic — many partials at integer multiples of the fundamental. The spectrum tells us which harmonics are loud, but leakage from the analysis window can obscure the quieter ones.

實戰案例。雙簧管聲音是高度諧波性的 — 基頻的整數倍處有很多 partial。 頻譜告訴我們哪些諧波比較強，但分析視窗的洩漏可能把較弱的諧波蓋住。

The following three pages compare three window choices on a synthetic oboe-like tone. We generate 8 harmonics of a 200 Hz fundamental at $f_s=8000$ Hz with slowly decreasing amplitude, window 512 samples, zero-pad to 4096, take the FFT.

接下來三頁比較三種視窗用在一段仿雙簧管音上：在 $f_s=8000$ Hz 下生成 200 Hz 基頻的 8 個諧波、振幅漸減，取 512 樣本加窗、補零到 4096、做 FFT。
