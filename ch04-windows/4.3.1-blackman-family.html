Stage 4-3-1 · Blackman Window Family 關卡 4-3-1 · Blackman 視窗家族 SASP §4.3.1 ↗

Three-cosine windows parametrized by two numbers. Choose them to minimise the first side-lobe, give a $-58$ dB plateau, or force side lobes to decay fastest asymptotically.

由兩個參數決定的三項餘弦視窗家族。選不同參數可最小化第一旁瓣、形成 $-58$ dB 平台、或讓旁瓣漸進衰減最快。

3-TERM FORM
$$w[n]=a_0-a_1\cos\!\left(\frac{2\pi n}{N-1}\right)+a_2\cos\!\left(\frac{4\pi n}{N-1}\right)$$

The two independent parameters (with $a_0+a_1+a_2=1$ or not, depending on normalisation) place two zeros on the spectrum's side-lobe envelope. Pick where those zeros fall to tune the window for a specific job.

兩個獨立參數（要不要求 $a_0+a_1+a_2=1$ 視歸一化而定）可在頻譜旁瓣包絡放兩個零。選擇零點位置即可針對不同工作調整視窗。

Common members

常見成員

$$\begin{array}{llccr} \text{Name} & a_0 & a_1 & a_2 & \text{1st SL} \\ \hline \text{Classic Blackman} & 0.42 & 0.50 & 0.08 & -58\ \text{dB} \\ \text{Exact Blackman} & 0.42659 & 0.49656 & 0.07685 & -68\ \text{dB} \\ \text{3-term Blackman-Harris} & 0.42323 & 0.49755 & 0.07922 & -71\ \text{dB} \\ \text{Nuttall minimum-SL} & 0.40897 & 0.5 & 0.09103 & -82\ \text{dB} \\ \end{array}$$

Start with Classic Blackman. If you need deeper side-lobes in near-main-lobe interference, jump to 3-term Blackman-Harris. Nuttall wins on paper but for audio the last $10$ dB are rarely audible.

預設選 Classic Blackman。近主瓣干擾要更深旁瓣就改用 3-term Blackman-Harris。Nuttall 理論最佳但音訊上最後 $10$ dB 多半聽不出。

Two numbers, many windows. The Blackman 3-term family demonstrates that systematic optimisation of a small coefficient set can beat "cute-round-number" choices — but only by a few dB.

兩個數字、多種視窗。Blackman 三項家族展示：對小數量係數做系統化最佳化，可勝過「整數好記」的選擇 — 不過只多幾 dB。
