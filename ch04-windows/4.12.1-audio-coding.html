Stage 4-12-1 · Windows for Audio Coding 關卡 4-12-1 · 音訊編碼用視窗 SASP §4.12.1 ↗

A good codec window needs: perfect reconstruction after overlap-add (COLA or sum-of-squares-COLA), a smooth masking-friendly main lobe, and low side lobes to stop quantisation noise in one band spilling into another.

編碼器視窗的需求：重疊相加後可精確還原（COLA 或平方和 COLA）、主瓣形狀與聽覺遮蔽相容、旁瓣夠低不讓量化雜訊洩到別帶。

  • MP3 / AAC: MLT sine window (§4.2.6). Squared-overlap = constant.
  • MP3 / AAC：MLT 正弦視窗（§4.2.6）。平方和重疊為定值。
  • Vorbis / Opus: Vorbis window (a specific optimisation of the MLT sine) and Opus switched windows for transients.
  • Vorbis / Opus：Vorbis 視窗（MLT 正弦的特定最佳化）；Opus 針對瞬態切換視窗。
  • Bluetooth SBC: square-root-Hann variants.
  • Bluetooth SBC：根 Hann 的變形。
