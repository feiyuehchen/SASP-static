Stage 4-7-1 · Matlab for Hann-Poisson 關卡 4-7-1 · Hann-Poisson 的 Matlab SASP §4.7.1 ↗

Matlab's Signal Processing Toolbox ships Hann and Poisson separately but not their product. Build Hann-Poisson by element-wise multiplication.

Matlab Signal Processing Toolbox 內建 Hann 和 Poisson，但不含乘積形式；自己做 — 逐元素相乘即可。

function w = hann_poisson(N, alpha)
  n = (0:N-1)';
  M = (N-1)/2;
  hann_part    = 0.5 * (1 - cos(2*pi*n/(N-1)));
  poisson_part = exp(-alpha * abs(n-M) / M);
  w = hann_part .* poisson_part;
end

% usage
N = 256; alpha = 2;
w = hann_poisson(N, alpha);
plot(w);                                % time-domain shape
W = 20*log10(abs(fftshift(fft(w, 4096))));
plot(linspace(-pi, pi, 4096), W);       % spectrum in dB

Picking α

如何挑 α

Sweep α over a grid (say $0.5 \ldots 3$ in steps of $0.25$) and measure the first side-lobe level and main-lobe width. Plot the tradeoff. Common sweet spots:

對 α 做網格搜索（$0.5 \ldots 3$、步長 $0.25$），測第一旁瓣與主瓣寬度。畫取捨曲線。常見甜蜜點：

α = 0: reduces to Hann. Side-lobe $-32$ dB, main-lobe $8\pi/N$.
α = 1: modest Poisson taper. Side-lobe $\approx -40$ dB.
α = 2: canonical Hann-Poisson. Side-lobe $\approx -55$ dB, main-lobe $\approx 10\pi/N$.
α = 3: strong taper, side-lobe $\approx -65$ dB, main-lobe widens significantly. α = 0：退回 Hann。旁瓣 $-32$ dB、主瓣 $8\pi/N$。
α = 1：輕度 Poisson 收尾。旁瓣約 $-40$ dB。
α = 2：經典 Hann-Poisson。旁瓣約 $-55$ dB、主瓣約 $10\pi/N$。
α = 3：強收尾、旁瓣約 $-65$ dB、主瓣顯著變寬。

α is your single knob. Hann-Poisson turns the standard Hann into a tunable family — cheap to compute, easy to sweep, and COLA-friendly when the Hann factor drives reconstruction.

α 是唯一旋鈕。Hann-Poisson 把標準 Hann 變成可調家族 — 計算便宜、易於掃描，而且當 Hann 主導重建時仍 COLA 友善。
