CHEAT CARD — 2-TERM COSINES
$$\begin{array}{llcc} \text{Name} & \alpha & \text{1st SL} & \text{Roll-off} \\ \text{Rectangular} & 1 & -13.3\ \text{dB} & 6\ \text{dB/oct} \\ \text{Hann} & 0.5 & -31.5\ \text{dB} & 18\ \text{dB/oct} \\ \text{Hamming} & 0.54 & -42.7\ \text{dB} & 6\ \text{dB/oct} \\ \end{array}$$

Main-lobe width in this family is $8\pi/N$ for anything but pure rectangle. The trade is always: first side lobe vs. asymptotic roll-off. If you know where your interfering signals live, pick the window whose worst side lobe is lowest in that band.

除了純矩形外，這家族的主瓣寬皆 $8\pi/N$。取捨永遠是「第一旁瓣 vs 漸進滾降」。 若知道干擾訊號出現在哪一帶，就選在那一帶上旁瓣最低的視窗。
