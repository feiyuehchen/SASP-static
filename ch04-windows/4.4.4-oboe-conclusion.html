Stage 4-4-4 · Conclusions (Oboe) 關卡 4-4-4 · 雙簧管結論 SASP §4.4.4 ↗

Pick your window by what you're looking for. The oboe comparison makes the trade-off concrete — main-lobe width vs. side-lobe depth, directly visible on the spectrogram.

選視窗的依據是：你要看什麼。雙簧管對比讓取捨具體化 — 主瓣寬 vs. 旁瓣深度，直接從頻譜看出來。

Decision tree

決策樹

Close resolution of nearby partials → Hann or Hamming.
    Narrow-ish main lobe ($8\pi/N$), reasonable side-lobe floor ($-32$ to $-43$ dB).

Deep dynamic range, tones well-separated → Blackman or 3-term Blackman-Harris.
    Accept a wider main lobe ($12\pi/N$) for $-58$ to $-71$ dB floor.

Need both → Kaiser (§4.9) or DPSS (§4.8), both with a tunable knob.

Codec / perfect reconstruction → MLT sine (§4.2.6) at 50% overlap. Pythagoras does the heavy lifting.

Minimum time-bandwidth product → Gaussian (§4.11). Sole uncertainty-principle optimum. 相近 partial 要分開 → Hann 或 Hamming。
    主瓣窄一點（$8\pi/N$）、旁瓣 $-32$ ~ $-43$ dB。

要深的動態範圍、頻率分得開 → Blackman 或 3-term Blackman-Harris。
    接受主瓣寬一點（$12\pi/N$），換 $-58$ ~ $-71$ dB 底板。

兩邊都要 → Kaiser（§4.9）或 DPSS（§4.8），兩者都有可調旋鈕。

編碼器 / 完美重建 → 50% 重疊下的 MLT 正弦（§4.2.6），畢氏定理搞定。

最小時間頻寬積 → Gaussian（§4.11），唯一達到測不準下界者。

What the oboe taught us

雙簧管教會我們什麼

1. Side-lobe level is a practical detection floor — pick $\ge 10$ dB below your weakest expected signal.
2. Main-lobe width determines closely-spaced-peak resolution — halve $N$ and watch peaks merge.
3. Zero-padding cleans up the display but does not lower the leakage floor — the window shape does.

1. 旁瓣位階就是實務偵測底線 — 選至少低於你預期最弱訊號 $10$ dB。
2. 主瓣寬決定相鄰峰能否分開 — 把 $N$ 減半，看峰如何糊在一起。
3. 補零讓顯示乾淨但不降洩漏底 — 降底靠視窗形狀。

Analysis is signal-specific. For a harmonic tone the trade is dynamic range vs. resolution. Other signals (noise, transients, chirps) have different priorities — §4.5 onward shows windows tuned to those.

分析是訊號相關的。諧波音的取捨是「動態範圍 vs. 解析度」。其他訊號（雜訊、瞬態、啾頻）有不同優先級 — §4.5 起介紹針對它們的視窗。
