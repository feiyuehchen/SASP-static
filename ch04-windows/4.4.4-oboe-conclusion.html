Pick your window by what you are looking for.

選視窗的依據：你要看什麼。

  • Close resolution of nearby partials → Hann or Hamming. Narrowish main lobe, reasonable side-lobe floor.
  • 相近 partial 要分開 → Hann 或 Hamming。主瓣窄一點、旁瓣還算夠深。
  • Deep dynamic range, tones are well-separated → Blackman or 3-term Blackman-Harris. Accept a wider main lobe for a 60–70 dB floor.
  • 要深的動態範圍、頻率分得開 → Blackman 或 3-term Blackman-Harris。允許主瓣寬一點，換 60–70 dB 的底板。
  • Need both → Kaiser (§4.9) or DPSS (§4.8), which have a tunable knob.
  • 兩邊都要 → Kaiser（§4.9）或 DPSS（§4.8），這兩個有可調旋鈕。
