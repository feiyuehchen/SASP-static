Stage 4-9-4 · Kaiser vs DPSS 關卡 4-9-4 · Kaiser 對照 DPSS SASP §4.9.4 ↗

Kaiser is a simple, closed-form approximation to the DPSS. In practice they agree to within a fraction of a dB across the entire useful parameter range. Kaiser wins on implementation cost; DPSS wins only when you need the exact optimum or the full family of orthogonal tapers.

Kaiser 是 DPSS 的簡單顯式近似。實務上兩者在整個可用範圍內差距不到幾分之一 dB。 Kaiser 實作便宜；只有在要精確最優或整組正交 taper 時，才必須用 DPSS。
