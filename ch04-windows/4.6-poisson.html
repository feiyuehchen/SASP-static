Stage 4-6 · Poisson (Exponential) Window 關卡 4-6 · Poisson（指數）視窗 SASP §4.6 ↗

A two-sided exponential. Its spectrum is a Lorentzian peak — extremely slow side-lobe roll-off, but no zeros: adequate when you want every frequency represented.

雙邊指數衰減。頻譜是 Lorentzian 峰 — 旁瓣滾降極慢，但沒有零點；當你希望所有頻率都有值時好用。

POISSON
$$w[n]=e^{-\alpha|n-M|/M},\quad M=\tfrac{N-1}{2}$$

What α controls

α 控制什麼

Large $\alpha$ means steeper time-domain decay → wider main lobe but quieter side lobes. Small $\alpha$ approaches a rectangular window. Typical practical range: $\alpha \in [1, 4]$.

$\alpha$ 大 → 時域衰減快 → 主瓣寬、旁瓣低。$\alpha$ 小則接近矩形視窗。實務範圍通常 $\alpha \in [1, 4]$。

Alone, Poisson has poor side-lobe depth (first side-lobe stays above $-40$ dB even at $\alpha=4$). Its real use is in compound windows — e.g. the Hann-Poisson (§4.7) multiplies the two for compounded side-lobe suppression.

單用時 Poisson 旁瓣深度不夠（$\alpha=4$ 時第一旁瓣仍 > $-40$ dB）。真正好用是在複合視窗中 — 如 Hann-Poisson（§4.7）把兩者相乘，旁瓣壓制疊加。

Exponentials are smooth but slow-decaying in frequency. Poisson alone isn't competitive with cosine-family windows, but it's the building block for compound designs like Hann-Poisson.

指數在頻域平滑但衰減慢。Poisson 單獨用不如餘弦家族，但是 Hann-Poisson 等複合設計的基石。
