Stage 4-6 · Poisson (Exponential) Window 關卡 4-6 · Poisson（指數）視窗 SASP §4.6 ↗

A two-sided exponential. Its spectrum is a Lorentzian (Cauchy) peak — extremely slow side-lobe roll-off, but no zeros: adequate when you want every frequency represented.

雙邊指數衰減。頻譜是 Lorentzian 峰 — 旁瓣滾降極慢，但沒有零點；當你希望頻譜在所有頻率都有值時好用。

POISSON
$$w[n]=e^{-\alpha|n-(N-1)/2|/((N-1)/2)}$$

Large $\alpha$ means steeper decay → wider main lobe but quieter side lobes. Small $\alpha$ approaches a rectangular window.

$\alpha$ 大 → 衰減快 → 主瓣寬、旁瓣低。$\alpha$ 小則接近矩形視窗。
