Stage 4-13-9 · Monotonicity Constraint 關卡 4-13-9 · 單調約束 SASP §4.13.9 ↗

A "well-behaved" window rises from zero to its peak and falls back — no internal dips. Add $w[n]\le w[n+1]$ on the rising half and $w[n]\ge w[n+1]$ on the falling half as linear inequalities to the LP.

「行為良好」的視窗從零升到峰再落回 — 中間不凹陷。在上升半邊加 $w[n]\le w[n+1]$、下降半邊加 $w[n]\ge w[n+1]$ 作為 LP 線性不等式。

MONOTONIC HALF-WINDOW
$$w[n+1]-w[n]\ge 0,\quad n=0,\dots,\lfloor (N-1)/2\rfloor-1$$

Combined with symmetry, just $\lfloor N/2\rfloor-1$ inequalities cover the whole window. The mirrored decreasing half is automatic.

配合對稱，僅 $\lfloor N/2\rfloor-1$ 條不等式即覆蓋整個視窗。鏡像的下降半邊自動成立。

When you need it

何時需要

  • Time-varying STFT: a non-monotonic taper produces internal energy bumps that look like aliasing artefacts when frames slide.
  • 時變 STFT：非單調收尾在框架滑動時會產生像混疊偽影的內部能量凸起。
  • Pedagogical / display: bell-shaped is what people expect when they see "window".
  • 教學／顯示：人們看到「視窗」時預期的就是鐘形。
  • Energy-envelope analysis: a window that wiggles inside makes envelope estimates noisy.
  • 能量包絡分析：內部擺動使包絡估計變雜訊。
COST
Without monotonicity (Dolph-Chebyshev at $A=80$): negative tails, equiripple sidelobes, narrowest main lobe.
With monotonicity at $A=80$: smooth bell, no negative tails, ~1-3 dB of attenuation lost OR ~5-10% main-lobe widening to keep $A$. 不加單調（Dolph-Chebyshev $A=80$）：負尾、等漣波旁瓣、主瓣最窄。
加單調（$A=80$）：平滑鐘形、無負尾、損失 1-3 dB 衰減 或 主瓣寬約 5-10%。

Hann, Hamming, Blackman, Kaiser ($\beta>0$), Gaussian, Poisson, MLT sine — all naturally monotonic on each half. You only add the constraint when LP-designing custom shapes that might wiggle.

Hann、Hamming、Blackman、Kaiser（$\beta>0$）、Gaussian、Poisson、MLT 正弦 — 各半邊天然單調。只有 LP 設計可能擺動的客製形狀時才加。

Monotonicity is shape insurance. Cheap to add ($\sim N/2$ inequalities), and it costs only a few dB. Worth it whenever a misbehaving taper would create downstream artefacts.

單調是形狀保險。加起來便宜（約 $N/2$ 條不等式）、只損失幾 dB。下游可能因不規則形狀產生偽影時，值得加。

Q. You design an LP window with very high $A$ (≥100 dB) and find it has an internal dip. Add monotonicity — what happens?

問：LP 設計高 $A$（≥100 dB）視窗發現有內部凹陷。加上單調 — 會怎樣？

Lab · Default vs monotonic

實驗 · 預設 vs 單調

100

Yellow: Dolph-Chebyshev (allows wiggles). Pink: monotonised version (cumulative max from edges, then renormalised). Time domain: pink loses negative tails. Spectrum: pink has a few dB higher floor — the cost of shape constraint.

黃：Dolph-Chebyshev（允許擺動）。粉：單調化版（從邊緣累積最大值再歸一）。時域：粉去除負尾。頻譜：粉地板抬高幾 dB — 形狀約束的代價。
