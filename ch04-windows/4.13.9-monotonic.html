Stage 4-13-9 · Monotonicity Constraint 關卡 4-13-9 · 單調約束 SASP §4.13.9 ↗

A well-behaved window rises from zero to its peak and falls back down — no internal dips. Add $w[n]\le w[n+1]$ on the left half and its mirror on the right as linear constraints.

「行為良好」的視窗應從零升到峰再落回 — 中間不凹陷。把 $w[n]\le w[n+1]$（左半）與對應右半約束寫成線性式。

Helpful when the side-lobe-only LP produces jagged shapes that would alias badly in time-varying applications.

若只最佳化旁瓣的 LP 解出鋸齒形狀、時變應用下會混疊時，加這約束比較穩。
