Stage 4-3 · Blackman-Harris Family 關卡 4-3 · Blackman-Harris 家族 SASP §4.3 ↗

Generalize the two-term Hamming family to three or four cosines. More knobs → more zeros we can place on the side lobes of $W_R(\omega)$. Result: ultra-low side lobes, at the cost of wider main lobes.

把兩項 Hamming 家族推廣到三項或四項餘弦。多了旋鈕 → 可以在 $W_R(\omega)$ 旁瓣上放更多零點。結果：旁瓣極低，代價是主瓣更寬。

K-TERM COSINE WINDOW
$$w[n]=\sum_{k=0}^{K-1}(-1)^{k}\,a_k\cos\!\left(\frac{2\pi k n}{N-1}\right)$$

Members at a glance

成員速覽

$$\begin{array}{lcccc} \text{Window} & K & a_0 & a_1 & a_{k\ge 2} \\ \hline \text{Hann} & 2 & 0.5 & 0.5 & - \\ \text{Hamming} & 2 & 0.54 & 0.46 & - \\ \text{Blackman} & 3 & 0.42 & 0.50 & 0.08 \\ \text{3-term BH} & 3 & 0.42323 & 0.49755 & 0.07922 \\ \text{Nuttall 3} & 3 & 0.40897 & 0.5 & 0.09103 \\ \text{4-term BH} & 4 & 0.35875 & 0.48829 & 0.14128,\,0.01168 \\ \end{array}$$

Trade-offs

取捨

Each extra term places one more zero on the side-lobe envelope, pushing peak side-lobe down by $\sim 15$ dB. But the main-lobe width grows by about $4\pi/N$ per term added.
$K=2$: main-lobe $8\pi/N$, first side-lobe $-31$ to $-43$ dB.
$K=3$: main-lobe $12\pi/N$, first side-lobe $-58$ to $-71$ dB.
$K=4$: main-lobe $16\pi/N$, first side-lobe $-74$ to $-92$ dB. 每多一項就在旁瓣包絡多放一個零，第一旁瓣約降 $15$ dB。但主瓣每項加寬約 $4\pi/N$。
$K=2$：主瓣 $8\pi/N$、第一旁瓣 $-31$ ～ $-43$ dB。
$K=3$：主瓣 $12\pi/N$、第一旁瓣 $-58$ ～ $-71$ dB。
$K=4$：主瓣 $16\pi/N$、第一旁瓣 $-74$ ～ $-92$ dB。

More cosine terms = deeper side-lobes but wider main-lobes. Blackman-Harris is the standard when analysing signals with very weak components close to strong ones.

餘弦項越多 → 旁瓣越深、主瓣越寬。Blackman-Harris 是「強弱成分靠近」分析情境的標準選擇。
