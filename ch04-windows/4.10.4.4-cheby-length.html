Given a target main-lobe half-width $\omega_0$ and side-lobe attenuation $A$ (dB), solve for $N$. Invert the width formula: $\omega_0=2\cos^{-1}(1/\beta)$ and $\beta=\cosh\bigl(\tfrac{1}{N-1}\cosh^{-1}10^{A/20}\bigr)$.

給主瓣半寬 $\omega_0$ 與旁瓣衰減 $A$（dB），反推 $N$。反解寬度公式 $\omega_0=2\cos^{-1}(1/\beta)$ 與 $\beta=\cosh\bigl(\tfrac{1}{N-1}\cosh^{-1}10^{A/20}\bigr)$。

LENGTH FORMULA
$$N\;=\;1+\left\lceil\frac{\cosh^{-1}(10^{A/20})}{\cosh^{-1}\!\bigl(1/\cos(\omega_0/2)\bigr)}\right\rceil$$

Back-of-envelope examples

快速估算

$A=60$ dB, $\omega_0=0.1\pi$ → $N\approx 45$.
$A=80$ dB, $\omega_0=0.05\pi$ → $N\approx 120$.
$A=100$ dB, $\omega_0=0.02\pi$ → $N\approx 370$.
$A=120$ dB, $\omega_0=0.01\pi$ → $N\approx 900$. $A=60$ dB，$\omega_0=0.1\pi$ → $N\approx 45$。
$A=80$ dB，$\omega_0=0.05\pi$ → $N\approx 120$。
$A=100$ dB，$\omega_0=0.02\pi$ → $N\approx 370$。
$A=120$ dB，$\omega_0=0.01\pi$ → $N\approx 900$。

Cost scaling: $N$ grows logarithmically with $A$ (the $\cosh^{-1}$ numerator) but roughly inversely with $\omega_0$ (the denominator $\cosh^{-1}(1/\cos(\omega_0/2))\approx \omega_0/\sqrt{2}$ for small $\omega_0$). So halving $\omega_0$ doubles $N$; doubling $A$ adds only a modest increment.

成本縮放：$N$ 與 $A$ 呈對數（分子的 $\cosh^{-1}$）、與 $\omega_0$ 呈反比（小 $\omega_0$ 時分母 $\cosh^{-1}(1/\cos(\omega_0/2))\approx \omega_0/\sqrt{2}$）。$\omega_0$ 減半 → $N$ 加倍；$A$ 加倍 → $N$ 只增加一點。

$\omega_0$ is the expensive axis. The log-cost in $A$ means going from 60 dB to 120 dB roughly doubles $N$; halving $\omega_0$ also doubles $N$. Design specs that push both hard compound multiplicatively.

$\omega_0$ 是昂貴的軸。$A$ 對數成本 → 60→120 dB 使 $N$ 大約加倍；$\omega_0$ 減半也使 $N$ 加倍。兩者同推，成本乘法成長。

✦ Mini-Boss ✦

✦ 關底小魔王 ✦

Q. Design at $A=80$ dB, $\omega_0=0.05\pi$ gives $N\approx 120$. You need to tighten $\omega_0$ to $0.025\pi$ (half). Best estimate for new $N$?

問：$A=80$ dB、$\omega_0=0.05\pi$ 時 $N\approx 120$。若把 $\omega_0$ 緊縮到 $0.025\pi$（減半），新 $N$ 約多少？

Lab · Length solver

實驗 · 長度求解器

80 0.157

The computed $N$ gives a Chebyshev window whose first null lands at $\omega_0$ and every side-lobe at $-A$ dB. Drag $\omega_0$ smaller → $N$ grows quickly. Drag $A$ deeper → $N$ grows slowly.

計算出的 $N$ 給出 Chebyshev 視窗，首零落在 $\omega_0$、所有旁瓣在 $-A$ dB。$\omega_0$ 減小 → $N$ 大幅增長。$A$ 加深 → $N$ 只緩慢增長。
