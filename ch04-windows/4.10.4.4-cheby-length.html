Given a target main-lobe half-width $\omega_0$ and side-lobe attenuation $A$ (dB), solve for $N$.

給主瓣半寬 $\omega_0$ 與旁瓣衰減 $A$（dB），反推 $N$。

$$N\;=\;1+\left\lceil\frac{\cosh^{-1}(10^{A/20})}{\cosh^{-1}\!\bigl(1/\cos(\omega_0/2)\bigr)}\right\rceil$$

Typical back-of-envelope: $A=80$ dB, $\omega_0=0.05\pi$ → $N\approx 120$. Smaller main lobes or deeper side lobes both need more samples; the cost grows roughly logarithmically in $A$ and inversely with $\omega_0$.

快速估算：$A=80$ dB、$\omega_0=0.05\pi$ → $N\approx 120$。 要主瓣更窄或旁瓣更深都要更多樣本；成本對 $A$ 大致對數、對 $\omega_0$ 反比。
