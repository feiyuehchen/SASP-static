Stage 4-4-3 · Blackman-Windowed Oboe 關卡 4-4-3 · Blackman 加窗的雙簧管 SASP §4.4.3 ↗

Noise floor now sits around $-70$ dB. We can see every harmonic the synthetic oboe has, with roughly $30$ dB of headroom below the weakest one. Main lobes are visibly wider — but for well-separated harmonics that is a worthwhile trade.

雜訊地板降到約 $-70$ dB，所有諧波都看得見，最弱諧波以下還有約 $30$ dB 的餘裕。主瓣明顯變寬 — 但對諧波分得開的訊號很值得。

What made the final $15$ dB

最後 $15$ dB 從何而來

Blackman's third cosine term places two additional zeros on $W_R(\omega)$'s side-lobe envelope (§4.3.5), dropping the first side lobe from Hamming's $-43$ dB to Blackman's $-58$ dB. The main lobe widens from $8\pi/N$ (Hamming) to $12\pi/N$ (Blackman) — a $50\%$ penalty on frequency resolution.

Blackman 多出的第三項餘弦在 $W_R(\omega)$ 旁瓣包絡再放兩個零（§4.3.5），把第一旁瓣從 Hamming 的 $-43$ dB 拉到 Blackman 的 $-58$ dB。主瓣由 $8\pi/N$（Hamming）變成 $12\pi/N$（Blackman），頻率解析度付出 $50\%$ 代價。

If your signal has closely-spaced peaks (< $12\pi/N$ apart) Blackman will merge them. For that case Hamming (or Hann) keeps peaks separable.

若訊號有相距 $< 12\pi/N$ 的峰，Blackman 會把它們糊在一起。此時 Hamming 或 Hann 才能分開。

Deeper side-lobes ≠ always better. You're trading main-lobe width for side-lobe depth. Pick the window that matches your signal's peak-spacing vs. dynamic-range profile.

旁瓣越深不一定越好。你是在用主瓣寬度換旁瓣深度。挑視窗要配合訊號的「峰間距 vs. 動態範圍」輪廓。
