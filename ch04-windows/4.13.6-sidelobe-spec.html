Stage 4-13-6 · Sidelobe Specification 關卡 4-13-6 · 旁瓣規格 SASP §4.13.6 ↗

Instead of a flat floor across all $\omega$, let the allowed side-lobe envelope depend on $\omega$: deeper where interferers live, relaxed where you don't care. Same LP, different right-hand side.

旁瓣不必全頻段同一高度 — 讓允許包絡隨 $\omega$ 變化：干擾所在處更深、不重要處放寬。同一 LP、只是右端不同。

WEIGHTED BOUND
$$|W(\omega_i)|\le\delta\cdot g(\omega_i)\quad \forall\omega_i\in\text{stopband}$$

$g(\omega)$ is a user-supplied weight function. Small $g$ → tight bound at that $\omega$ (hard requirement). Large $g$ → loose bound (relaxed). The LP minimises the single scalar $\delta$; the weighted bound translates that into a $\omega$-dependent envelope.

$g(\omega)$ 為使用者給定的權重函數。$g$ 小 → 該 $\omega$ 上嚴格界（硬要求）；$g$ 大 → 寬鬆界。LP 最小化純量 $\delta$；加權界轉為 $\omega$ 相依包絡。

Typical shapes

常見形狀

Flat $g=1$: classical Dolph-Chebyshev — equiripple.
Notch at $\omega_0$: $g(\omega)=0.01$ near $\omega_0$, $g=1$ elsewhere → deep notch in spectrum at target interferer.
Roll-off $g=1/\omega$: mimic Hamming's far-field decay but keep the LP's flexibility.
Step $g=1$ then $g=10$: tight below some cutoff, loose above — DC-to-mid bands clean, HF relaxed. 平坦 $g=1$：經典 Dolph-Chebyshev — 等漣波。
$\omega_0$ 陷波：$\omega_0$ 附近 $g=0.01$、其他 $g=1$ → 目標干擾處陷波。
滾降 $g=1/\omega$：模仿 Hamming 遠場衰減但保留 LP 彈性。
階梯 $g=1$ 然後 $g=10$：某截止以下緊、以上鬆 — 低中頻乾淨、高頻放寬。

Radar matched filter: sidelobes near the main lobe are fine (close returns are actual targets), but sidelobes far from the main lobe could mask a weak distant target. Set $g$ small in the far region.

雷達匹配濾波：主瓣附近旁瓣無妨（近距回波就是目標），但遠處旁瓣會掩蓋弱遠目標。遠處 $g$ 取小。

$g(\omega)$ is your design weapon. Any spectral shape you can describe as "ceiling at this $\omega$" becomes a single LP. Named windows are just different flat (or uniformly weighted) $g$ choices.

$g(\omega)$ 是設計武器。任何「此 $\omega$ 上限」的頻譜形狀都能化為單一 LP。具名視窗只是不同的平坦（或均勻加權）$g$ 選擇。
