Stage 4-3-6 · Power-of-Cosine Family 關卡 4-3-6 · 餘弦冪次家族 SASP §4.3.6 ↗

$w[n]=\sin^p(\pi n/(N-1))$, or equivalently $\bigl[\tfrac{1}{2}(1-\cos(2\pi n/(N-1)))\bigr]^{p/2}$. $p=1$ is the MLT sine window; $p=2$ is the Hann; higher $p$ gives even softer taper.

$w[n]=\sin^p(\pi n/(N-1))$，或等於 $\bigl[\tfrac{1}{2}(1-\cos(2\pi n/(N-1)))\bigr]^{p/2}$。$p=1$ 是 MLT 正弦視窗；$p=2$ 是 Hann；$p$ 更大代表收尾更軟。

Advantages: no extra cosine coefficients to tune. Disadvantages: loses the flexibility of Blackman-Harris. Useful when you want a smooth one-parameter family (for example sweeping window softness in real time).

優點：沒有額外的餘弦係數要調。缺點：沒有 Blackman-Harris 的彈性。適用於想用單一參數平滑調整視窗柔軟度的情境（例如即時動態調整）。
