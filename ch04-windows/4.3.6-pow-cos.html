Stage 4-3-6 · Power-of-Cosine Family 關卡 4-3-6 · 餘弦冪次家族 SASP §4.3.6 ↗

$w_p[n]=\sin^p(\pi n/(N-1))$ — a single real $p$ controls how soft the taper is. $p=1$ is the MLT sine window; $p=2$ is the Hann; larger $p$ keeps going softer.

$w_p[n]=\sin^p(\pi n/(N-1))$ — 單一實數 $p$ 控制收尾柔軟度。$p=1$ 是 MLT 正弦視窗；$p=2$ 是 Hann；$p$ 更大代表收尾更軟。

POWER-OF-COSINE WINDOW
$$w_p[n]=\sin^p\!\left(\frac{\pi n}{N-1}\right)\;=\;\left[\tfrac{1}{2}\!\left(1-\cos\tfrac{2\pi n}{N-1}\right)\right]^{p/2}$$

Identification by $p$

依 $p$ 辨識

$$\begin{array}{cll} p & \text{name} & \text{1st SL} \\ \hline 1 & \text{MLT sine} & -23\ \text{dB} \\ 2 & \text{Hann} & -31\ \text{dB} \\ 3 & \text{Rife-Vincent class I} & -39\ \text{dB} \\ 4 & \text{Rife-Vincent class II} & -47\ \text{dB} \\ \end{array}$$

Each integer step of $p$ buys about $8$ dB of first side-lobe depth. Main-lobe width grows roughly linearly with $\sqrt{p}$ — a cheap, predictable family.

$p$ 每加 1 大約多 $8$ dB 第一旁瓣深度。主瓣寬約隨 $\sqrt{p}$ 線性增長 — 便宜、可預測的家族。

When you want a continuous one-knob family — e.g. sweeping window softness in real time, or automating a design search. For fixed needs, Blackman / Hamming are specific members that are easier to recall.

想要連續單旋鈕家族時用 — 例如即時調整視窗柔軟度，或自動化設計搜索。固定需求時，Blackman / Hamming 更好記。

One real number parameterises a whole family. Simpler than Blackman-Harris (which has three), at the cost of less depth per main-lobe unit.

一個實數參數化整個家族。比 Blackman-Harris（三個參數）簡單，代價是「每單位主瓣寬度的旁瓣深度」稍差。
