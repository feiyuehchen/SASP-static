Think of $w[n]$ as an $N$-vector of unknowns. The spectrum is a linear function of that vector. Constraints on the spectrum become linear inequalities — which is exactly what a linear program (LP) solver eats.

把 $w[n]$ 當成一個 $N$ 維未知向量。頻譜是它的線性函數，對頻譜的要求就變成線性不等式 — 正是 LP 求解器吃的輸入。
