Stage 4-13-11 · L1 Derivative Objective 關卡 4-13-11 · L1 導數目標 SASP §4.13.11 ↗

$\min \sum_n |w[n+1]-w[n]|$. Encourages piecewise-constant shapes; most of the ripple is concentrated on a few large steps while the rest is flat.

$\min\sum_n|w[n+1]-w[n]|$。偏好分段常數形狀；大部分振盪集中在少數幾個大台階，其餘平坦。

Less common than L∞ for audio windows; useful when your downstream processing is insensitive to sparse discontinuities but cares about total variation.

音訊視窗上比 L∞ 少見；適合下游處理對稀疏不連續不敏感、但在意總變差時。
