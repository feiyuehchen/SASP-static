Stage 4-11-2 · Gaussian & Its Transform 關卡 4-11-2 · Gaussian 與其轉換 SASP §4.11.2 ↗

A Gaussian in time is a Gaussian in frequency. The widths trade off as $\sigma_t \sigma_\Omega = 1$ at the minimum — the continuous-time statement of the uncertainty principle.

時域高斯 ↔ 頻域高斯。寬度以 $\sigma_t \sigma_\Omega = 1$ 互換（取最小值）— 測不準原理的連續時間版本。

CONTINUOUS-TIME PAIR
$$w(t)=e^{-t^{2}/(2\sigma^{2})}\;\longleftrightarrow\;W(\Omega)=\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}\,e^{-\Omega^{2}\sigma^{2}/2}$$

Reading the pair: time-domain $\sigma_t = \sigma$; frequency-domain $\sigma_\Omega = 1/\sigma$. Their product is the Heisenberg bound — no non-Gaussian window achieves it.

讀這對：時域 $\sigma_t = \sigma$、頻域 $\sigma_\Omega = 1/\sigma$。兩者乘積即 Heisenberg 下界 — 非高斯視窗都達不到。

Discrete & truncated version

離散與截斷版本

On the unit circle (DTFT) and after truncation to length $N$, the sampled Gaussian is only approximately Gaussian near the main lobe. Two artefacts appear:

單位圓上（DTFT）且截斷到長度 $N$ 後，取樣高斯只在主瓣附近近似為高斯。出現兩個瑕疵：

(1) Finite-length endpoints introduce weak side-lobes (the "truncation error").
(2) Aliasing: a sampled Gaussian's DTFT is a Gaussian plus its periodic copies — overlap is minimal when $\sigma$ is small enough relative to $N$. (1) 有限長度端點產生弱旁瓣（截斷誤差）。
(2) 混疊：取樣高斯的 DTFT = 高斯 + 其週期副本；當 $\sigma$ 相對於 $N$ 夠小，重疊可忽略。

Continuous Gaussian is exactly self-dual; discrete / truncated Gaussian is only approximately so. For theoretical elegance use the continuous statement; for practical FFTs, remember the two caveats and use §4.11.3's exact-discrete version if you need perfect self-duality.

連續高斯嚴格自身對偶；離散/截斷版只近似。理論優雅取連續敘述；實際 FFT 時記住兩個注意事項，需完美自身對偶時請用 §4.11.3 的精確離散版。
