Stage 4-3-4 · Three-Term Blackman-Harris 關卡 4-3-4 · 三項 Blackman-Harris SASP §4.3.4 ↗

Same three-cosine shape as classic Blackman, but the weights are re-tuned by Blackman and Harris (1978) to push the first side-lobe all the way down to $-71$ dB.

三項餘弦形狀同經典 Blackman，但 Blackman 與 Harris（1978）重新調整權重，把第一旁瓣壓到 $-71$ dB。

3-TERM BLACKMAN-HARRIS COEFFICIENTS
$$a_0=0.42323,\quad a_1=0.49755,\quad a_2=0.07922$$

Numerically very close to classic Blackman (0.42, 0.50, 0.08) but with side-lobe depth gaining about $13$ dB. The optimisation problem: minimise $\max_\omega |W(\omega)|$ over the side-lobe region — a Chebyshev-type minimax problem.

數值上極接近經典 Blackman（0.42, 0.50, 0.08）但旁瓣深度多約 $13$ dB。最佳化問題：把旁瓣區域的 $\max_\omega |W(\omega)|$ 壓到最小 — 一個 Chebyshev 型minimax 問題。

Equioscillation

等振盪性質

The optimal 3-term BH window's spectrum has all side-lobes of equal height (the $-71$ dB plateau). Any coefficient perturbation raises some side-lobe above that floor while lowering others — the classical sign that we've hit the Chebyshev optimum.

最佳 3-term BH 頻譜的所有旁瓣等高（$-71$ dB 平台）。任何係數擾動都會讓某些旁瓣高出、某些降低 — Chebyshev 最佳解的經典特徵。

When the signal of interest has weak components close (in frequency) to strong ones, and first-side-lobe level is what limits your analysis. Otherwise Hann or classic Blackman are faster to type and effectively identical.

當關切訊號有弱成分靠近強成分、且第一旁瓣位階限制分析時使用。其他情形 Hann 或經典 Blackman 寫起來快又幾乎一樣好。

Minimax optimisation ≈ "flat" side-lobes. Beyond a certain design effort, classical round-number windows are essentially as good — but when you're measurement-limited, 3-term BH is the first stop.

Minimax 最佳化 ≈「平坦」旁瓣。超過某程度設計努力，經典整數版視窗差不多好用 — 但若你受量測限制，3-term BH 是第一站。
