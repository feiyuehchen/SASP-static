Stage 4-3-4 · Three-Term Blackman-Harris 關卡 4-3-4 · 三項 Blackman-Harris SASP §4.3.4 ↗

Same three-cosine shape as Blackman, but the weights are re-tuned by Blackman and Harris (1978) to push the first side-lobe all the way down to $-71$ dB.

三項餘弦形狀同 Blackman，但 Blackman 與 Harris（1978）重新調整權重，把第一旁瓣壓到 $-71$ dB。

3-TERM BLACKMAN-HARRIS
$$a_0=0.42323,\; a_1=0.49755,\; a_2=0.07922$$

Very close numerically to classic Blackman but gains about $13$ dB of first-side-lobe depth. Useful when near-main-lobe interference matters and main-lobe width is acceptable.

數值上很接近經典 Blackman，但第一旁瓣深度多約 $13$ dB。 當近主瓣干擾重要、主瓣寬度可接受時很適合。
