When someone says just "Blackman window," they mean this one: $(0.42, 0.5, 0.08)$.

當人家只說「Blackman 視窗」時，預設就是這一組係數：$(0.42, 0.5, 0.08)$。

CLASSIC BLACKMAN
$$w[n]=0.42-0.5\cos\!\left(\frac{2\pi n}{N-1}\right)+0.08\cos\!\left(\frac{4\pi n}{N-1}\right)$$
  • Main-lobe width: $12\pi/N$ (three times rectangular).
  • 主瓣寬：$12\pi/N$（矩形的 3 倍）。
  • First side lobe: $\approx -58$ dB.
  • 第一旁瓣約 $-58$ dB。
  • Side-lobe roll-off: $18$ dB/octave.
  • 旁瓣滾降：$18$ dB/八度。

Use it when you need about 60 dB of dynamic range in the spectrum and do not mind a wider main lobe. A good default for audio forensic inspection or tone-in-noise detection.

適用：頻譜需要約 60 dB 的動態範圍、主瓣寬一點可以接受。例如音訊鑑識或弱音偵測。
