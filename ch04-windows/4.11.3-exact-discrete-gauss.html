Stage 4-11-3 · Exact Discrete Gaussian 關卡 4-11-3 · 精確離散高斯 SASP §4.11.3 ↗

On the unit circle (DTFT), a sampled Gaussian is NOT self-dual. The exact discrete Gaussian is the sequence whose DFT is genuinely proportional to itself.

單位圓上（DTFT）取樣高斯並非嚴格自身對偶。精確離散高斯則是其 DFT 嚴格與自身成比例的序列。

Construction

構造

VIA JACOBI THETA
$$w[n]\;=\;\vartheta_3\!\left(\pi n/N,\,q\right),\qquad q=e^{-\pi/N}$$

where $\vartheta_3$ is the Jacobi theta function of the third kind. The key property: its DFT satisfies $W[k] = c\,w[k]$ for some constant $c$ — so the window and its spectrum are literally the same sequence up to scaling.

其中 $\vartheta_3$ 是 Jacobi theta 第三類函數。關鍵性質：DFT 滿足 $W[k] = c\,w[k]$（$c$ 為常數）— 視窗與其頻譜字面上就是同一個序列（差一個倍數）。

When this matters

何時派上用場

Theoretical frameworks needing perfect time-frequency self-duality (e.g. certain chirp-Gabor decompositions).
Precision measurement where any residual aliasing of the sampled Gaussian's spectrum would contaminate results.
Education: showing students that the continuous "Gaussian ↔ Gaussian" duality has a discrete analogue, not just an approximation. 理論框架需要完美時頻自身對偶（例如某些 chirp-Gabor 分解）。
精密量測中任何取樣高斯頻譜的殘餘混疊都會污染結果。
教學：向學生展示連續「高斯 ↔ 高斯」對偶有離散對應、不是近似而已。

For standard audio STFT work, the sampled Gaussian from §4.11 is accurate enough — the truncation side-lobes are below $-100$ dB at $\sigma \le 0.3$. Reach for the exact discrete version only when the theory demands it.

一般音訊 STFT，§4.11 的取樣高斯已經夠準 — $\sigma \le 0.3$ 時截斷旁瓣低於 $-100$ dB。只有理論要求時才用精確離散版。

The discrete world has its own Gaussian. It just takes Jacobi theta functions to spell it out. Most of the time the sampled continuous version is indistinguishable.

離散世界有自己的高斯。只是要用 Jacobi theta 函數才能寫下來。多數情況下和取樣連續版無法區分。
