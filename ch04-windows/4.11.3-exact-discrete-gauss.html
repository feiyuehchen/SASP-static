Stage 4-11-3 · Exact Discrete Gaussian 關卡 4-11-3 · 精確離散高斯 SASP §4.11.3 ↗

On the unit circle (DTFT), a sampled Gaussian is NOT self-dual. The exact discrete Gaussian is the sequence whose DFT is also proportional to itself — a genuine discrete analogue built from Jacobi theta functions.

在單位圓上，取樣高斯並不是真的自身對偶。精確離散高斯是其 DFT 與自己成比例的序列 — 用 Jacobi theta 函數建構的離散對應版。

For most audio practice the sampled Gaussian from §4.11 is close enough; the exact version matters when you build theoretical frameworks that need perfect self-duality (e.g. certain chirp-Gabor decompositions).

音訊實務中 §4.11 的取樣高斯已經夠用；只有在需要完美自身對偶的理論框架中才要用精確版，例如某些 chirp-Gabor 分解。
