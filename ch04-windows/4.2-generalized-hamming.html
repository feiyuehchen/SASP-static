Stage 4-2 · Generalized Hamming Family 關卡 4-2 · 廣義 Hamming 家族 SASP §4.2 ↗

A two-term cosine combination. One parameter $\alpha$ picks whether you are closer to a rectangle ($\alpha=1$), a Hann ($\alpha=0.5$), or a Hamming ($\alpha\approx 0.54$).

一個兩項餘弦組合。用一個參數 $\alpha$ 就能在矩形（$\alpha=1$）、Hann（$\alpha=0.5$）、Hamming（$\alpha\approx 0.54$）之間切換。

GENERALIZED HAMMING
$$w[n]=\alpha-(1-\alpha)\cos\!\left(\frac{2\pi n}{N-1}\right),\quad 0\le n

The spectrum is a linear combination of the rectangular's $W_R(\omega)$ and two shifted copies. Choosing $\alpha$ cancels the first side-lobe of $W_R$ to differing degrees, exchanging main-lobe width for side-lobe depth.

頻譜是矩形 $W_R(\omega)$ 與兩份平移副本的線性組合。選 $\alpha$ 不同，對 $W_R$ 的第一旁瓣抵消程度不同， 用主瓣寬度換旁瓣深度。
