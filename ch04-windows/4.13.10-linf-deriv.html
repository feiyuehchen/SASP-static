Stage 4-13-10 · L∞ Derivative Objective 關卡 4-13-10 · L∞ 導數目標 SASP §4.13.10 ↗

Replace the side-lobe-depth objective with the largest first-difference of the window: $\min \max_n|w[n+1]-w[n]|$. This is an L∞ derivative objective: produces the smoothest possible shape (minimum max slope) given a main-lobe target.

把旁瓣深度目標換成「最大相鄰差」最小化：$\min \max_n|w[n+1]-w[n]|$。這是L∞ 導數目標：在給定主瓣目標下產生最平滑形狀（最小最大斜率）。

L∞ DERIVATIVE LP
$$\begin{aligned} &\min_{w,\eta}\;\eta\\ &\text{s.t.}\; w[n+1]-w[n]\le\eta,\quad-(w[n+1]-w[n])\le\eta\\ &\phantom{\text{s.t.}\;}|W(\omega_i)|\le\delta\;\text{(side-lobe ceiling, fixed)}\\ &\phantom{\text{s.t.}\;}\sum_n w[n]=1\;\text{(DC anchor)} \end{aligned}$$

The new variable $\eta$ bounds every first-difference. Side-lobe depth becomes a fixed ceiling rather than the objective; smoothness becomes the thing you minimise. Two objectives swap roles.

新變數 $\eta$ 限制所有相鄰差。旁瓣深度變為固定上限而非目標；平滑度成為被最小化的量。兩目標角色互換。

Where it shines

適用場景

Time-domain plotting / pedagogy: smooth shape avoids visual artefacts.
Differentiation-based feature extraction: low max slope = low ringing in derivative.
Asymptotic side-lobe roll-off: smoothness controls $1/\omega^k$ decay rate. L∞-smooth ≈ Hann-like 18 dB/oct.
Time-stretch overlap-add: smooth windows reduce phase artefacts. 時域繪圖/教學：平滑形狀避免視覺偽影。
基於微分的特徵抽取：最大斜率小 → 導數振鈴小。
漸進旁瓣滾降：平滑度控制 $1/\omega^k$ 衰減率。L∞ 平滑 ≈ Hann 類 18 dB/oct。
時間伸縮 OLA：平滑視窗減少相位偽影。

Trading sidelobe depth for smoothness. A smoothness-optimised window typically has a higher first sidelobe (~5-8 dB) than the equivalent Chebyshev, but its asymptotic roll-off is much steeper. Choose based on whether your interferer is near or far.

用旁瓣深度換平滑。平滑最佳化視窗的第一旁瓣比 Chebyshev 高 5-8 dB，但漸進滾降陡得多。視干擾遠近選擇。

Same LP framework, different objective. Smoothness, sidelobe depth, total variation — any can be the thing you minimise; the others become inequality constraints. LP is the universal optimisation glue.

同 LP 框架、不同目標。平滑、旁瓣深度、總變差 — 任一可作目標，其餘成不等式約束。LP 是通用最佳化膠水。
