Stage 4-13-10 · L∞ Derivative Objective 關卡 4-13-10 · L∞ 導數目標 SASP §4.13.10 ↗

Swap the side-lobe-depth objective for the largest first-difference of the window: $\min \max_n|w[n+1]-w[n]|$. Produces the smoothest possible shape for a given main-lobe target.

把旁瓣深度目標換成「最大相鄰差」的最小化：$\min\max_n|w[n+1]-w[n]|$。在給定主瓣目標下產生最平滑的形狀。

The resulting window has roll-off that beats Hann/Hamming/Blackman at the smoothness axis, at the cost of a somewhat higher first side lobe.

所得視窗在平滑度軸上勝過 Hann/Hamming/Blackman，代價是第一旁瓣稍高。
