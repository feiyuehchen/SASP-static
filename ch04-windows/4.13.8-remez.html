Stage 4-13-8 · Remez Exchange 關卡 4-13-8 · Remez 交換演算法 SASP §4.13.8 ↗

A smarter way to solve the minimax side-lobe problem. Instead of thousands of frequency-grid inequalities, exchange a small set of extremal frequencies iteratively. Converges in a handful of steps.

解 minimax 旁瓣問題的快速方法。不用密格點上的幾千條不等式，而是迭代地交換一小組極值頻率。幾步內收斂。

Historically used for FIR filter design (Parks-McClellan). For window design the structure is the same: the extremal set has at most $\lceil N/2\rceil+1$ points, and the solution equalises the error magnitude at all of them.

歷史上用於 FIR 濾波器設計（Parks-McClellan）。視窗設計結構相同： 極值集合最多 $\lceil N/2\rceil+1$ 個點，解會讓所有這些點上的誤差振幅一致。
