Three settings of $A$ side by side at fixed $N$. Every side-lobe in each curve sits exactly at $-A$ dB — the equiripple signature.

固定 $N$ 下三種 $A$ 並排。各曲線每個旁瓣都剛好位於 $-A$ dB — 這就是等漣波特徵。

Reading the plot

圖的讀法

Blue $A=40$ dB: widest side-lobe floor, narrowest main lobe. Yellow $A=60$ dB: audio standard, main lobe $\sim 30\%$ wider. Pink $A=100$ dB: floor buried, main lobe about $2\times$ the $A=40$ version.

藍 $A=40$ dB：旁瓣地板最高、主瓣最窄。黃 $A=60$ dB：音訊標準，主瓣寬 $\sim 30\%$。粉 $A=100$ dB：地板埋入底部，主瓣寬度約為 $A=40$ 的 $2\times$。

$A$ buys attenuation by spending main-lobe width. But Dolph-Chebyshev does this trade optimally — any other window pays more width for the same attenuation.

$A$ 用主瓣寬換衰減。Dolph-Chebyshev 做這個交易是最優的 — 其他視窗同衰減下主瓣更寬。

Q. Looking at the plot, what makes Dolph-Chebyshev unmistakable?

問：從圖上看，Dolph-Chebyshev 的辨識特徵是什麼？

Lab · Three Chebyshev spectra

實驗 · 三個 Chebyshev 頻譜

64

Blue $A=40$, yellow $A=60$, pink $A=100$. As $N$ grows the main lobes narrow proportionally while side-lobe floors stay locked at their target. This is the beauty of the minimax design.

藍 $A=40$、黃 $A=60$、粉 $A=100$。$N$ 變大主瓣同比例變窄，旁瓣地板鎖在目標值不動。這就是 minimax 設計的美。
