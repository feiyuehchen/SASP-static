Stage 4-3-5 · Frequency-Domain Blackman-Harris 關卡 4-3-5 · 頻域 Blackman-Harris 實作 SASP §4.3.5 ↗

Applying a cosine-sum window in the time domain is equivalent to convolving the spectrum with a few delta functions — or, more usefully, summing a few frequency-shifted copies of the rectangular-window DFT.

時域上「乘一個餘弦和視窗」等價於頻域上「對頻譜做幾個 delta 卷積」— 或更實用地說，把矩形視窗 DFT 做幾份頻率位移副本相加。

SPECTRAL IDENTITY
$$W(\omega)=\sum_{k=0}^{K-1}(-1)^{k}\,a_k\,W_R\!\left(\omega-\frac{2\pi k}{N-1}\right)$$

Each cosine term $a_k\cos(2\pi k n/(N-1))$ is a frequency shift of a constant; after multiplying it by $x[n]$ we get two copies of $X(\omega)$ at $\pm\omega_k$. Stacking $K$ of these with the right $a_k$ is exactly what the time-domain window does.

每個餘弦項 $a_k\cos(2\pi k n/(N-1))$ 是常數的頻率位移；乘上 $x[n]$ 後得到 $X(\omega)$ 在 $\pm\omega_k$ 的兩份副本。以適當 $a_k$ 把 $K$ 份疊起來，恰好就是時域加窗的結果。

Why implement it this way?

為何這樣做？

(1) For windows longer than the transform (Portnoff-style, §10.7) you can't just multiply in time; the frequency-domain realisation is cleaner.
(2) Hardware DSP often runs the DFT first then windows in frequency — saves a pass through time-domain memory.
(3) Pedagogically, it makes the convolution-theorem link (§3.7) concrete: BH spectrum = 3 or 4 shifted Dirichlet kernels added. (1) 視窗長於 transform 時（Portnoff，§10.7）不能單純時域乘；頻域實作更乾淨。
(2) 硬體 DSP 經常 DFT 先做、再於頻域加窗 — 省一次時域記憶體存取。
(3) 教學上把卷積定理（§3.7）具體化：BH 頻譜 = 3 或 4 個位移 Dirichlet 核相加。

Blackman spectrum = three Dirichlet sincs, nicely aligned. The $a_k$ place zeros on the side-lobe envelope; choose them well and the result is the Blackman-Harris family.

Blackman 頻譜 = 三個排好的 Dirichlet sinc。$a_k$ 決定旁瓣包絡的零點位置；選得好就是 Blackman-Harris 家族。
