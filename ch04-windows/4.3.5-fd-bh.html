Stage 4-3-5 · Frequency-Domain Blackman-Harris 關卡 4-3-5 · 頻域 Blackman-Harris 實作 SASP §4.3.5 ↗

A cosine-sum window applied to time-domain samples is equivalent to adding a few shifted copies of the rectangular-window DFT in the frequency domain.

時域上「乘一個餘弦和視窗」等價於頻域上「把矩形視窗的 DFT 做幾個位移副本相加」。

Because each cosine term is a frequency shift, applying the window in the time domain is like convolving the spectrum with a few deltas whose weights are the $a_k$. In real codecs this is sometimes done directly in the frequency domain — especially for windows much longer than the transform size.

每個餘弦項就是一個頻率位移，時域乘視窗等同於頻域上對頻譜做若干 delta 卷積（權重為 $a_k$）。 實際編碼器有時直接在頻域做這件事 — 尤其當視窗比 transform 長許多時。

SPECTRAL IDENTITY
$$W(\omega)=\sum_{k=0}^{K-1}(-1)^{k}\,a_k\,W_R\!\left(\omega-\frac{2\pi k}{N-1}\right)$$

For $K=3$ this is three deltas, so the Blackman window's spectrum is the sum of three shifted Dirichlet kernels — a straight read from the convolution theorem of §3.7.

$K=3$ 時共三個 delta，所以 Blackman 頻譜就是三個位移過的 Dirichlet 核相加 — §3.7 的卷積定理直接套用。
