Stage 4-10 · Dolph-Chebyshev Window 關卡 4-10 · Dolph-Chebyshev 視窗 SASP §4.10 ↗

All side lobes sit at the exact same height $A$ dB. For any chosen $A$, the Dolph-Chebyshev window has the narrowest possible main lobe — provably optimal for that criterion.

所有旁瓣都是同一高度 $A$ dB。對任何 $A$，Dolph-Chebyshev 視窗擁有最窄主瓣 — 在此準則下可證明最優。

SPECTRUM (CLOSED FORM)
$$W(\omega)=\frac{\cos\!\bigl[N\cos^{-1}\!\bigl(\beta\cos(\omega/2)\bigr)\bigr]}{\cosh\!\bigl[N\cosh^{-1}(\beta)\bigr]}$$

Inside the main lobe $|\beta\cos(\omega/2)| > 1$ so the numerator is a $\cosh$ (growing); outside it's a real $\cos$ oscillating between $\pm 1$, hence the perfectly flat side-lobe ripple at level $1/\cosh(N\cosh^{-1}\beta)$.

主瓣內 $|\beta\cos(\omega/2)| > 1$，分子為 $\cosh$（上升）；主瓣外為實數 $\cos$ 在 $\pm 1$ 間振盪，因此旁瓣完全平坦、高度 $1/\cosh(N\cosh^{-1}\beta)$。

β FROM TARGET A (dB)
$$\beta\;=\;\cosh\!\left[\tfrac{1}{N}\cosh^{-1}\!\bigl(10^{A/20}\bigr)\right]$$

Time-domain $w[n]$ is obtained by inverse DFT of the magnitude-only spectrum above; the result is real and symmetric. All the design information flows from the spectrum backward — the opposite direction of Kaiser and DPSS.

時域 $w[n]$ 由上面振幅頻譜反 DFT 得到，結果為實數且對稱。所有設計資訊都從頻譜反推時域 — 和 Kaiser、DPSS 方向相反。

Because all side lobes are equally tall, total side-lobe energy is high even though each is small. For steady-state sinusoid separation this is ideal; for transient or impulsive signals, the equal-ripple pattern looks like ringing.

因所有旁瓣等高，單一旁瓣雖低但總能量不小。 對穩態正弦分離理想；但對瞬態訊號，等漣波圖樣看起來就像振鈴。

Dolph-Chebyshev is minimax in the frequency domain. Given $A$, main-lobe width is provably minimum. The price: equal side-lobe energy everywhere instead of the usual roll-off.

Dolph-Chebyshev 是頻域 minimax。給定 $A$，主瓣寬度可證最小。代價：旁瓣等高全域不衰減，而非平常的滾降。

Q. Why might you prefer Kaiser over Dolph-Chebyshev in audio DSP?

問：音訊 DSP 中，為何偏好 Kaiser 而非 Dolph-Chebyshev？

Watch every side-lobe sit on the same horizontal dashed line at $-A$ dB. Larger $A$ pushes the ripple level down and pulls the time-domain window shape toward a narrow peaked bell with slight negative tails at the ends.

觀察每個旁瓣都躺在 $-A$ dB 的水平虛線上。$A$ 大旁瓣壓低，時域形狀變成窄峰鐘狀，兩端略有負值小翹。
