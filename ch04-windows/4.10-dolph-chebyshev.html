Stage 4-10 · Dolph-Chebyshev Window 關卡 4-10 · Dolph-Chebyshev 視窗 SASP §4.10 ↗

All its side lobes have the exact same height. For any chosen side-lobe level $A$ dB, this window has the narrowest possible main lobe — proven optimal for that criterion.

所有旁瓣都是同一高度。對任何指定旁瓣高度 $A$ dB，這個視窗擁有最窄的主瓣 — 在此準則下可證明最優。

SPECTRUM (CLOSED FORM)
$$W(\omega)=\frac{\cos\!\bigl[N\cos^{-1}\!\bigl(\beta\cos(\omega/2)\bigr)\bigr]}{\cosh\!\bigl[N\cosh^{-1}(\beta)\bigr]}$$

Time-domain $w[n]$ is obtained by inverse FFT of this magnitude spectrum. $\beta$ is related to the side-lobe level $A$ by $\beta=\cosh(\tfrac{1}{N}\cosh^{-1}(10^{A/20}))$.

時域 $w[n]$ 由上面振幅頻譜反 FFT 得到。 $\beta$ 與旁瓣高度 $A$ 的關係：$\beta=\cosh\!\bigl(\tfrac{1}{N}\cosh^{-1}(10^{A/20})\bigr)$。

Because all side lobes are equally tall, total side-lobe energy is high even though each is small. For steady-state sinusoid separation this is ideal; for transient or impulsive signals, the equal-ripple pattern can add distracting structure.

因為所有旁瓣都等高，單一旁瓣雖低，但總旁瓣能量不小。 對穩態正弦分離理想；但對瞬態訊號，這個等漣波圖樣可能看起來很礙眼。
