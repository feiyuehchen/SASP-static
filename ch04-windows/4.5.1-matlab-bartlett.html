% Matlab: bartlett(N) gives endpoint zeros; triang(N) does not
w = bartlett(N);

% Manual one-liner
n = (0:N-1)';
M = (N-1)/2;
w = 1 - abs((n - M) / M);

% JS
const w = DSP.WINDOWS.bartlett(N);

bartlett vs triang: Matlab's bartlett includes the two endpoint zeros; triang does not (endpoints at $1/N$). For most analyses it does not matter.

Matlab 的 bartlett 含兩端零；triang 不含（端點為 $1/N$）。大多分析用哪個都沒差。
