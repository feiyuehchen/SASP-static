Stage 4-13-5 · DC Constraint 關卡 4-13-5 · DC 約束 SASP §4.13.5 ↗

Without a DC constraint the LP would solve $w=0$. Fixing the main-lobe peak $W(0)=\sum w[n]=1$ turns the problem into "how low can side lobes go if DC is clamped to 1".

沒有 DC 約束，LP 會直接解出 $w=0$。把主瓣峰 $W(0)=\sum w[n]=1$ 釘住，問題就變成「DC 固定為 1 時旁瓣能壓到多低」。

$$\sum_{n=0}^{N-1} w[n]\;=\;1$$

This is a single equality constraint — cheap to add, essential for the LP to be well-posed.

只是一條等式約束 — 加起來便宜、是 LP 定義良好不可少的。
