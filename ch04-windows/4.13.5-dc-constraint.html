Stage 4-13-5 · DC Constraint 關卡 4-13-5 · DC 約束 SASP §4.13.5 ↗

Without a DC constraint, the LP's trivial optimum is $w=0$ — every side-lobe is zero, but so is the main lobe, so the "window" does nothing. Fixing the main-lobe peak $W(0)=\sum_n w[n]=1$ turns the problem into the meaningful question: how low can side-lobes go while main-lobe peak stays at 1?

沒有 DC 約束，LP 的平凡最優就是 $w=0$ — 旁瓣皆零但主瓣也為零，這個「視窗」什麼都沒做。固定主瓣峰 $W(0)=\sum_n w[n]=1$ 把問題化為有意義的：主瓣峰維持 1 時旁瓣能壓到多低？

THE CONSTRAINT
$$W(0)\;=\;\sum_{n=0}^{N-1} w[n]\;=\;1$$

This is a single equality constraint — one row added to $A_{eq}$ with all-ones coefficients, $b_{eq}=1$. Essential; cheap; non-negotiable.

單一等式約束 — 在 $A_{eq}$ 加一列全 1、$b_{eq}=1$。必要、便宜、不可省。

Variants

變體

DC = 1 (standard): Dolph-Chebyshev-like, side-lobe floor in dB is the LP's reported $\delta$.
DC fixed, $\max w=1$: normalise the peak sample instead — different scale, same shape.
$\sum w^2 = 1$: "unit-energy" window — not LP (quadratic); handled by QP or SDP.
$w[\lfloor N/2\rfloor]=1$: normalise centre sample — simplest to state, shape unchanged. DC = 1（標準）：類 Dolph-Chebyshev，旁瓣地板 dB = LP 回報的 $\delta$。
DC 固定、$\max w=1$：改歸一峰值樣本 — 尺度不同、形狀同。
$\sum w^2 = 1$：「單位能量」視窗 — 非 LP（二次）；需 QP 或 SDP。
$w[\lfloor N/2\rfloor]=1$：歸一中央樣本 — 陳述最簡、形狀不變。

Whatever normalisation you use during solving, you typically re-normalise $\max(w)=1$ or $\sum w=1$ afterwards for downstream use. The LP result is geometrically the same shape either way.

不論求解時用哪種歸一，後續通常再以 $\max(w)=1$ 或 $\sum w=1$ 再歸一以便下游使用。幾何形狀不變。

The DC constraint is the anchor. It's what makes the side-lobe-minimisation problem non-trivial. Without it, LP collapses; with it, LP becomes the Dolph-Chebyshev solver (plus any extra constraints you stack).

DC 約束是錨點。旁瓣最小化因它而有意義。沒它 LP 崩潰；有它 LP 就是 Dolph-Chebyshev 求解器（加你的附加約束）。

✦ 關底小魔王 ✦

Q. What happens to an LP window-design problem if you remove the DC constraint (and don't replace it with something equivalent)?

問：LP 視窗設計若移除 DC 約束（也不換等效約束），會怎樣？

Lab · With / without DC

實驗 · 有無 DC

1.00

Drag DC to 0 — the window shrinks toward zero (the trivial solution). Every finite DC in between gives a scaled Dolph-Chebyshev; the spectrum shape is identical, only the dB scale shifts. That's why DC=1 is the natural normalisation.

把 DC 拉到 0 — 視窗萎縮至零（平凡解）。中間任意有限 DC 都給出縮放的 Dolph-Chebyshev；頻譜形狀相同，只是 dB 尺度平移。這就是 DC=1 為何為自然歸一。
