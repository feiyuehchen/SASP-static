Stage 4-11 · Gaussian Window 關卡 4-11 · 高斯視窗 SASP §4.11 ↗

A Gaussian in time is a Gaussian in frequency. This is the only function that achieves the minimum time-bandwidth product, which is why it keeps showing up in STFT theory.

時域高斯 ↔ 頻域高斯。這是唯一能達到最小時間頻寬積的函數，也因此 STFT 理論裡到處看得到它。

GAUSSIAN
$$w[n]=\exp\!\left(-\tfrac12\Bigl[\tfrac{n-(N-1)/2}{\sigma\,(N-1)/2}\Bigr]^{2}\right)$$
  • $\sigma$ controls how tight the taper is. $\sigma=0.4$ is a common default.
  • $\sigma$ 決定收尾嚴格度；$\sigma=0.4$ 是常見預設。
  • No zeros in the spectrum — side lobes smoothly decay.
  • 頻譜沒有零點 — 旁瓣平滑衰減。
  • Gaussian-windowed chirplets are the centrepiece of World 11 §11.6.
  • Gaussian 加窗的 chirplet 是第 11 世界 §11.6 的核心。

Lab · σ controls the time / freq trade

實驗 · σ 控制時頻取捨

0.40

Small σ = tight time bump, wide spectrum Gaussian. Large σ = broad time Gaussian, narrow spectrum. The product of their widths is constant — the uncertainty principle made visible.

σ 小 = 時域窄、頻域寬；σ 大 = 時域寬、頻域窄。兩者寬度乘積為常數 — 把測不準原理看個清楚。
