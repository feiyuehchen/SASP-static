Stage 4-8 · Slepian / DPSS (Prolate Spheroidal) 關卡 4-8 · Slepian / DPSS（扁球體波函數） SASP §4.8 ↗

The DPSS (Discrete Prolate Spheroidal Sequences, or Slepian sequences) is the window that maximises the fraction of its spectrum energy inside a chosen bandwidth $|\omega|

DPSS（離散扁球體波函數，或 Slepian 序列）是「使頻譜能量集中在選定頻寬 $|\omega|最大化的視窗。這個目標下可證最佳。

OPTIMIZATION
$$w^*\;=\;\underset{\|w\|=1}{\arg\max}\;\frac{\int_{-W}^{W}|W(\omega)|^2 d\omega}{\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}|W(\omega)|^2 d\omega}$$

The solution is the dominant eigenvector of an $N\times N$ Toeplitz matrix $\mathbf{B}$ with entries $B_{k\ell} = \frac{\sin(2W(k-\ell))}{\pi(k-\ell)}$. The time-bandwidth product $NW$ parametrises the family — larger $NW$ pushes side-lobes deeper but widens the main lobe.

解為 $N\times N$ Toeplitz 矩陣 $\mathbf{B}$（$B_{k\ell} = \frac{\sin(2W(k-\ell))}{\pi(k-\ell)}$）的主特徵向量。時間頻寬乘積 $NW$ 為家族參數 — $NW$ 越大旁瓣越深，主瓣也越寬。

Multiple tapers

多個 taper

The matrix has several eigenvectors concentrated in the same band. The first $2NW$ of them are "good"; using them as a set of orthogonal tapers is the heart of the Thomson multi-taper spectrum estimator (Ch 7).

同一矩陣有多個在相同頻帶內集中的特徵向量。前 $2NW$ 個為「佳」tapers；用這組正交 tapers 是 Thomson 多 taper 頻譜估計器（Ch 7）的核心。

Requires an $N\times N$ eigendecomposition. Matlab's dpss caches precomputed values. For one-off audio analysis, the Kaiser window (§4.9) is a closed-form approximation indistinguishable for most practical purposes.

需 $N\times N$ 特徵分解。Matlab 的 dpss 有快取。一次性音訊分析中，Kaiser（§4.9）的封閉解在多數實務上難分軒輊。

DPSS is the "gold standard" concentration window. Expensive to compute but mathematically optimal. Everything else in this chapter can be compared against it.

DPSS 是能量集中的「金標準」視窗。計算貴但數學最優。本章所有其他視窗都可拿來跟它比。

Lab · Kaiser as a DPSS surrogate

實驗 · Kaiser 作為 DPSS 代理

6.0 64

DPSS requires an eigendecomposition (not practical in-browser for live demo). The Kaiser window gives an excellent approximation — $\beta \approx \pi NW$ matches the main-lobe / side-lobe trade. Use this lab as a DPSS stand-in; the shape and spectrum agree to within a dB for typical audio sizes.

DPSS 需特徵分解，瀏覽器即時示範不實際。Kaiser 是極佳近似 — $\beta \approx \pi NW$ 可匹配主瓣/旁瓣取捨。把本 lab 當成 DPSS 替身；一般音訊尺寸下形狀與頻譜差異不到 1 dB。
