Stage 4-8 · Slepian / DPSS (Prolate Spheroidal) 關卡 4-8 · Slepian / DPSS（扁球體波函數） SASP §4.8 ↗

The DPSS (Discrete Prolate Spheroidal Sequences) is the window that maximises the fraction of its spectrum energy inside a chosen bandwidth $|\omega|

DPSS（離散扁球體波函數）是讓「頻譜能量集中在選定頻寬 $|\omega|

The shape is the dominant eigenvector of a certain Toeplitz matrix; in practice Matlab's dpss(N, NW) gives it back, with NW being the time-bandwidth product. NW controls the trade-off: larger NW means lower sidelobes and wider main lobe.

形狀是某個 Toeplitz 矩陣的主特徵向量；實務上 Matlab 的 dpss(N, NW) 直接給你， NW 為時間頻寬乘積。NW 越大 → 旁瓣越低、主瓣越寬。

  • Theoretically optimal for sidelobe suppression at a given main-lobe width.
  • 在主瓣寬給定時，旁瓣壓制理論最優。
  • Multiple orthogonal DPSS sequences exist — used in the multi-taper spectral estimator.
  • 同一 NW 下有多個互正交的 DPSS 序列 — 多視窗頻譜估計（multi-taper）就是用它們。
  • Computation is nontrivial; Kaiser (next) is a close approximation.
  • 計算較複雜；Kaiser（下一關）是它的近似。
