Stage 4-13-2 · LP Formulation of Chebyshev Design 關卡 4-13-2 · LP 描述 Chebyshev 設計 SASP §4.13.2 ↗

MIN-δ LP
$$\begin{aligned}&\min_{w,\delta}\;\delta\\ &\text{s.t.}\; W(\omega_i)\le\delta, \; \omega_i\in\text{stopband}\\ &\phantom{\text{s.t.}\;} -W(\omega_i)\le\delta\\ &\phantom{\text{s.t.}\;} W(0)=1\end{aligned}$$

Discretise $\omega$ on a dense grid in the side-lobe region, write the spectrum $W(\omega_i)=\sum_n w[n]\cos(\omega_i n)$ (for symmetric real windows) — that is linear in $w$. Plug into the LP. The solver returns Dolph-Chebyshev for free, and lets you tweak any constraint.

在旁瓣區以密格點取 $\omega$，寫 $W(\omega_i)=\sum_n w[n]\cos(\omega_i n)$（對稱實窗）— 對 $w$ 線性。 放進 LP，解器會自動吐出 Dolph-Chebyshev；還能隨時加其他約束。
