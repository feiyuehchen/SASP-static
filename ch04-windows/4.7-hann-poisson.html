Stage 4-7 · Hann-Poisson Window 關卡 4-7 · Hann-Poisson 視窗 SASP §4.7 ↗

Product of a Hann and a Poisson window. Inherits the Hann's gentle zeros and the Poisson's exponential decay, killing side lobes faster than either alone.

Hann 與 Poisson 視窗的乘積。同時具備 Hann 的兩端平滑為零、以及 Poisson 的指數衰減 — 旁瓣壓得比兩者單獨都低。

HANN-POISSON
$$w[n]=\tfrac{1}{2}\Bigl(1-\cos\tfrac{2\pi n}{N-1}\Bigr)\cdot e^{-\alpha|n-M|/M},\quad M=\tfrac{N-1}{2}$$

Why multiply instead of add?

為什麼是相乘而非相加？

Time-domain product ↔ frequency-domain convolution. Convolving a Hann's spectrum (which is already three Dirichlet bumps) with a Poisson's spectrum (a Lorentzian) produces a composite with deeper side-lobes than either factor. Adding time-domain shapes would not compound the side-lobe suppression — only multiplication gives the convolution-based reinforcement.

時域相乘 ↔ 頻域卷積。Hann 頻譜（三個 Dirichlet 凸起）與 Poisson 頻譜（Lorentzian）卷積，旁瓣會比兩者單獨更深。時域相加不會讓旁瓣壓制疊加 — 只有相乘才能靠卷積互相強化。

Use when: you want Hann's 50%-overlap COLA property plus deeper side-lobes than Hann alone. Good for analysis before aggressive per-band modification (SMS, pitch shift).

用於：需要 Hann 的 50% 重疊 COLA、加上比 Hann 更深的旁瓣。分析後要激進逐帶修改（SMS、變調）時特別適合。
