Stage 4-7 · Hann-Poisson Window 關卡 4-7 · Hann-Poisson 視窗 SASP §4.7 ↗

Product of a Hann and a Poisson window. Inherits the Hann's gentle zeros and the Poisson's exponential decay, killing side lobes faster than either.

Hann 與 Poisson 的乘積。同時擁有 Hann 的兩端平滑為零、以及 Poisson 的指數衰減，旁瓣壓得比兩者都低。

$$w[n]=\tfrac{1}{2}\Bigl(1-\cos\tfrac{2\pi n}{N-1}\Bigr)\cdot e^{-\alpha|n-(N-1)/2|/((N-1)/2)}$$

Useful when you want Hann's nice COLA behaviour plus an extra tunable taper.

當你想要 Hann 的 COLA 特性，又想多一個可調收尾時適用。
