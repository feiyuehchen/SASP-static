Stage 4-2-6 · MLT Sine Window 關卡 4-2-6 · MLT 正弦視窗 SASP §4.2.6 ↗

A half-cycle of sine. Designed so the sum of squares of overlapping windows is constant — the MLT / MDCT reconstruction condition used in MP3, AAC, Vorbis, Opus.

半週期正弦。設計上讓相鄰視窗平方和為常數 — MLT / MDCT 完美重建條件，MP3、AAC、Vorbis、Opus 都用。

MLT SINE WINDOW
$$w[n]=\sin\!\left(\frac{\pi\,(n+\tfrac12)}{N}\right),\quad 0\le n<2N$$
POWER-COMPLEMENTARY PROPERTY
$$w^2[n]+w^2[n+N]=1\quad\text{for }0\le n

Why codecs love it

編碼器為何喜愛

At 50% overlap both the analysis and synthesis windows are this MLT sine. The sum-of-squares property means that even after quantising the intermediate frames in frequency, the time-domain reconstruction is exact (up to the quantisation noise). That's the secret that lets MP3 encode at 128 kbps without audible artefacts on typical material.

50% 重疊下分析與合成視窗都是 MLT 正弦。平方和性質讓你即使在中間對頻譜做量化，時域仍可精確重建（只差量化噪音）。這就是 MP3 在 128 kbps 普通素材上聽不出瑕疵的祕密。
