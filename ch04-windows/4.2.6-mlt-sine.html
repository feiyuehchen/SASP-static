Stage 4-2-6 · MLT Sine Window 關卡 4-2-6 · MLT 正弦視窗 SASP §4.2.6 ↗

A half-cycle of sine. Designed so the sum of squares of overlapping windows is constant — the MLT / MDCT reconstruction condition used in MP3, AAC, Vorbis, Opus.

半週期的正弦。設計上讓相鄰視窗的平方和為常數 — MLT / MDCT 完美重建的條件；MP3、AAC、Vorbis、Opus 都用。

MLT SINE WINDOW
$$w[n]=\sin\!\left(\frac{\pi\,(n+\tfrac12)}{N}\right),\quad 0\le n PROPERTY
$$w^2[n]+w^2[n+N]=1$$

Why codecs like it: with $50\%$ overlap the analysis window and the synthesis window are both the MLT sine, and the sum-of-squares condition lets you reconstruct exactly even after frequency-domain quantisation of intermediate frames.

編碼器愛用它的原因：50% 重疊下，分析與合成視窗都是 MLT 正弦， 平方和守恆讓你即使在中間對頻譜做量化，仍可精確重建。
