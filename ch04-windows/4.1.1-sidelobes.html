Stage 4-1-1 · Side Lobes 關卡 4-1-1 · 旁瓣 SASP §4.1.1 ↗

Side lobes are the spectral "bleed-through" from one frequency into its neighbours. They decide how easily a weak tone can be hidden next to a strong one.

旁瓣是一個頻率「漏」到鄰近頻率的部分。決定了弱音被強音遮蔽的難易度。

Anatomy of the rectangular spectrum

矩形頻譜的解剖圖

$|W_R(\omega)|$ has a tall main lobe centred at $\omega=0$ surrounded by a series of smaller lobes. Each lobe corresponds to one period of $\sin(N\omega/2)$. The $k$-th side-lobe peak sits near $\omega \approx (2k+1)\pi/N$ and has height roughly $1/[(k+\tfrac12)\pi]$ relative to the main lobe.

$|W_R(\omega)|$ 主瓣在 $\omega=0$ 中心，兩側一個個小旁瓣。每個旁瓣對應 $\sin(N\omega/2)$ 的一個週期。 第 $k$ 個旁瓣峰大約在 $\omega\approx(2k+1)\pi/N$，相對主瓣高度約 $1/[(k+\tfrac12)\pi]$。

  • First side lobe: about $-13.26$ dB.
  • 第一旁瓣：約 $-13.26$ dB。
  • Second side lobe: about $-17.8$ dB.
  • 第二旁瓣：約 $-17.8$ dB。
  • Roll-off: $6$ dB per octave of frequency (each doubling of $\omega$ halves the envelope).
  • 滾降：每頻率八度 $6$ dB（$\omega$ 翻倍，包絡折半）。

A 440 Hz tone at 0 dB will put energy at about $-13$ dB into neighbours. If you want to hear a 100× quieter ($-40$ dB) tone nearby, the rectangular window cannot show it — you need a window with deeper side lobes (Hann, Blackman, Kaiser, …).

0 dB 的 440 Hz 音會在鄰近頻率留下約 $-13$ dB 的能量。如果要看到比它小 100 倍（$-40$ dB）的弱音， 矩形視窗看不到 — 得換旁瓣更深的視窗（Hann、Blackman、Kaiser…）。
