We declare the spectrum to be a scaled $T_{N-1}$ and back out $w[n]$ by inverse DFT. No direct time-domain formula.

先宣告頻譜形狀為縮放的 $T_{N-1}$，再用反 DFT 推出 $w[n]$。沒有直接的時域公式。

SPECTRUM
$$W(\omega)=\frac{T_{N-1}\!\bigl(\beta\cos(\omega/2)\bigr)}{T_{N-1}(\beta)},\qquad \beta=\cosh\!\left(\tfrac{1}{N-1}\cosh^{-1} 10^{A/20}\right)$$

Evaluate $W$ on $N$ uniformly spaced points around the unit circle, then inverse FFT → the Dolph-Chebyshev coefficients $w[n]$.

在單位圓上均勻取 $N$ 點算 $W$、再反 FFT，得到 Dolph-Chebyshev 係數 $w[n]$。
