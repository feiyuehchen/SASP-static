Stage 4-10-4-2 · Window Definition 關卡 4-10-4-2 · 視窗定義 SASP §4.10.4.2 ↗

Unlike every other window in this chapter, Dolph-Chebyshev is defined by its spectrum. We declare $W(\omega)$ to be a scaled Chebyshev polynomial, then back out the time-domain $w[n]$ by inverse DFT. There is no closed-form time-domain formula.

與本章其他視窗不同，Dolph-Chebyshev 是由頻譜定義。先宣告 $W(\omega)$ 為縮放的 Chebyshev 多項式，再以反 DFT 推出時域 $w[n]$。沒有時域封閉式。

SPECTRUM
$$W(\omega)=\frac{T_{N-1}\!\bigl(\beta\cos(\omega/2)\bigr)}{T_{N-1}(\beta)},\qquad \beta=\cosh\!\left(\tfrac{1}{N-1}\cosh^{-1} 10^{A/20}\right)$$

The denominator $T_{N-1}(\beta)$ normalises the peak at $\omega=0$ to 1. For $|\beta\cos(\omega/2)| \leq 1$ the numerator oscillates between $\pm 1$ — those are the side lobes at level $1/T_{N-1}(\beta) = 10^{-A/20}$. For $|\beta\cos(\omega/2)| > 1$ it grows as $\cosh$ — that's the main lobe.

分母 $T_{N-1}(\beta)$ 將 $\omega=0$ 峰值歸一至 1。$|\beta\cos(\omega/2)| \leq 1$ 時分子在 $\pm 1$ 振盪 — 即旁瓣，高度 $1/T_{N-1}(\beta) = 10^{-A/20}$。$|\beta\cos(\omega/2)| > 1$ 時分子以 $\cosh$ 增長 — 即主瓣。

Construction recipe

建構食譜

1. Pick $N$ and $A$ (dB).
2. Compute $\beta=\cosh\bigl(\tfrac{1}{N-1}\cosh^{-1} 10^{A/20}\bigr)$.
3. Evaluate $W(\omega_k)=T_{N-1}(\beta\cos(\omega_k/2))/T_{N-1}(\beta)$ at $N$ points $\omega_k=2\pi k/N$, $k=0,\dots,N-1$.
4. Apply inverse DFT to the sampled spectrum → $w[n]$ (real, symmetric). 1. 決定 $N$ 和 $A$（dB）。
2. 算 $\beta=\cosh\bigl(\tfrac{1}{N-1}\cosh^{-1} 10^{A/20}\bigr)$。
3. 在 $\omega_k=2\pi k/N$（$k=0,\dots,N-1$）$N$ 點上計算 $W(\omega_k)=T_{N-1}(\beta\cos(\omega_k/2))/T_{N-1}(\beta)$。
4. 對頻譜樣本反 DFT → $w[n]$（實對稱）。

SASP §4.10 writes the spectrum as $W(\omega) = \cos\!\bigl[N\cos^{-1}(\beta\cos(\omega/2))\bigr] / \cosh\!\bigl[N\cosh^{-1}\beta\bigr]$. This is the same formula — just $T_N = \cos(N\cos^{-1} \cdot)$ unwound. Use whichever form you find clearer.

SASP §4.10 寫成 $W(\omega) = \cos\!\bigl[N\cos^{-1}(\beta\cos(\omega/2))\bigr] / \cosh\!\bigl[N\cosh^{-1}\beta\bigr]$。同一公式 — 只是展開 $T_N = \cos(N\cos^{-1} \cdot)$。擇一採用即可。

Spectrum-first, time-domain-second. Choose the spectrum to be Chebyshev-optimal, then let inverse DFT give you the time coefficients. No other window in this chapter is defined this way.

頻譜先、時域後。選 Chebyshev 最優頻譜，反 DFT 得時域係數。本章其他視窗都不是這樣定義。
