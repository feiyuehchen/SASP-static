Stage 4-5 · Bartlett (Triangular) Window 關卡 4-5 · Bartlett（三角形）視窗 SASP §4.5 ↗

$w_B[n]$ is the convolution of two rectangular windows, so its spectrum is the square of a Dirichlet kernel — always non-negative. Unique among windows that make the PSD estimation math especially clean.

$w_B[n]$ 是兩個矩形視窗的卷積；其頻譜是 Dirichlet 核的平方 — 處處非負。視窗家族中罕見的性質，讓功率譜估計的數學乾淨。

BARTLETT / TRIANGULAR
$$w_B[n]=1-\left|\frac{n-(N-1)/2}{(N-1)/2}\right|$$
SPECTRUM = DIRICHLET²
$$|W_B(\omega)|=\left|\frac{\sin(N\omega/4)}{(N/2)\sin(\omega/2)}\right|^2 \cdot (N/2)$$

Key numbers

關鍵數字

Main-lobe width: $8\pi/N$ (same as Hann / Hamming).
First side-lobe: $\approx -26.5$ dB.
Asymptotic roll-off: $12$ dB/octave (squared Dirichlet = twice the roll-off of a rect).
Uniqueness: non-negative real spectrum — the reason Bartlett's method of averaging periodograms is named after him. 主瓣寬：$8\pi/N$（與 Hann / Hamming 相同）。
第一旁瓣：約 $-26.5$ dB。
漸進滾降：$12$ dB/oct（平方 Dirichlet = rect 的兩倍快）。
獨特之處：頻譜為非負實數 — 這就是 Bartlett periodogram 平均法以他命名的原因。

Bartlett is Hann's cousin. Same main-lobe width, slightly worse first side-lobe but $12$ dB/oct roll-off (vs. Hann's $18$). Use it when you want a simple-to-compute window with guaranteed non-negative spectrum.

Bartlett 是 Hann 的堂兄弟。主瓣同寬、第一旁瓣稍差但滾降 $12$ dB/oct（Hann 是 $18$）。想要簡單實作、保證非負頻譜時用。
