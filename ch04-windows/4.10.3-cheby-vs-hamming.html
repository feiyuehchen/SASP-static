Stage 4-10-3 · Chebyshev & Hamming Compared 關卡 4-10-3 · Chebyshev 與 Hamming 比較 SASP §4.10.3 ↗

Pick Chebyshev with $A=42$ dB so its flat floor equals Hamming's first side-lobe height. Near the main lobe both look similar; far from it, Hamming's floor rolls off 6 dB/octave and eventually sinks below Chebyshev's equiripple plateau.

把 Chebyshev 的 $A$ 設成 42 dB 使其平地板等於 Hamming 的第一旁瓣高度。主瓣附近兩者相似；遠處 Hamming 以 6 dB/octave 滾降，最終沉到 Chebyshev 的等漣波平台之下。

Which wins depends on your interferer

誰勝取決於干擾位置

  • Near interferers (within a few bins of the target) → Chebyshev's guaranteed floor is better.
  • 近干擾（目標幾個 bin 內）→ Chebyshev 的保證地板較佳。
  • Far interferers (across the band) → Hamming's rolled-off floor sinks lower.
  • 遠干擾（跨頻帶）→ Hamming 滾降地板沉得更低。
  • Audio transients → Hamming looks cleaner (less ringing artefact).
  • 音訊瞬態 → Hamming 看來更乾淨（振鈴偽影較少）。
HAMMING ASYMPTOTIC FLOOR
$$|W_\text{Hamming}(\omega)|\;\sim\;\frac{C}{\omega^2}\quad\Longrightarrow\quad -12\,\text{dB/oct}\;\text{in}\;|W|^2$$

(Hamming's side-lobe peaks sit on a curve that decays 6 dB per octave in $|W(\omega)|$ — i.e. $1/\omega$ on a linear scale. The quadratic $1/\omega^2$ form above applies to the power spectrum after non-perfect tuning.)

（Hamming 旁瓣峰沿著 $|W(\omega)|$ 每 octave 降 6 dB 的曲線 — 線性尺度就是 $1/\omega$。能量頻譜在非完美微調下為二次衰減 $1/\omega^2$。）

Equiripple buys a floor; rolled-off buys far-field depth. You can't have both with a single classical window — pick based on where your interferers live.

等漣波買到地板；滾降買到遠場深度。單一經典視窗不可兼得 — 依干擾位置擇一。
