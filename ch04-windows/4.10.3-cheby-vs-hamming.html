Stage 4-10-3 · Chebyshev vs Hamming 關卡 4-10-3 · Chebyshev 對照 Hamming SASP §4.10.3 ↗

Tune Chebyshev to $-42$ dB (same first-sidelobe as Hamming). Chebyshev then holds the floor perfectly flat; Hamming's floor decays 6 dB/octave and eventually goes lower. Which wins depends on where your interferers live.

把 Chebyshev 調成 $-42$ dB（與 Hamming 第一旁瓣同高）。Chebyshev 之後維持平的地板；Hamming 地板則以 6 dB/octave 衰減，遠處反而更低。誰贏，看干擾訊號在哪一帶。

  • Near the main lobe → Chebyshev gives you the guaranteed floor.
  • 靠近主瓣 → Chebyshev 提供保證地板。
  • Far from the main lobe → Hamming falls below Chebyshev's equi-ripple floor.
  • 遠離主瓣 → Hamming 會降到比 Chebyshev 的等漣波地板更低。
