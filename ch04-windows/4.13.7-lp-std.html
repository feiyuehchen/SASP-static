The window-design LP in one tableau:

視窗設計 LP 的總表：

$$\begin{aligned} &\text{variables: } w[0],\ldots,w[(N-1)/2],\;\delta\\ &\min\;\delta\\ &\text{s.t.}\;\sum_n w[n]=1\quad\text{(DC)}\\ &\phantom{\text{s.t.}\;}\left|\sum_n w[n]\cos(\omega_i n)\right|\le\delta\,g(\omega_i)\;\forall\omega_i\in\text{stopband}\\ &\phantom{\text{s.t.}\;}w[n]\ge 0\;(\text{optional non-negativity}) \end{aligned}$$

The absolute value turns into two linear inequalities per $\omega_i$. Feed to linprog and you have your custom window.

絕對值拆成每個 $\omega_i$ 兩條線性不等式。丟進 linprog 就得到你要的視窗。
