Stage 4-13-8-1 · Remez Convergence 關卡 4-13-8-1 · Remez 收斂 SASP §4.13.8.1 ↗

Remez converges in finitely many iterations and always reaches the global optimum of the minimax side-lobe problem, thanks to the Chebyshev alternation theorem.

Remez 有限步必收斂，且總能達到 minimax 旁瓣問題的全局最優 — 由 Chebyshev 交替定理保證。

Each iteration does one grid search (for the next extremal frequency) and one small linear solve. Iteration counts of 5–15 are typical for windows of length up to a few thousand.

每次迭代：一次網格搜尋（找下一個極值頻率）加一次小型線性求解。 長度至數千的視窗，通常 5–15 次迭代即可。
