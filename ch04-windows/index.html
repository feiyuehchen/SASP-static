World 4 — Spectrum-Analysis Windows 第 4 世界 — 頻譜分析視窗 SASP Ch. 4 ↗

Every spectrum we compute is the spectrum of a windowed segment. The window's shape controls main-lobe width, side-lobe level, and how much spectral leakage we suffer. This world catalogues every window you will see in practice.
