Stage 4-12-2 · General Rule 關卡 4-12-2 · 通則 SASP §4.12.2 ↗

Pick the window that makes your worst-case interferer smallest in the band you care about, subject to the main-lobe and reconstruction constraints you can afford.

在可接受的主瓣寬與重建約束下，選能讓你最關心的那一帶上最糟干擾變最小的視窗。

That is the entire philosophy of window design. Every named window in this world is one point on a Pareto frontier. The next section shows how to compute other points using linear programming.

這就是視窗設計的全部哲學。本世界所有的具名視窗，都是某條柏拉圖前緣上的一點。 下一節會示範如何用線性規劃算出其他點。
