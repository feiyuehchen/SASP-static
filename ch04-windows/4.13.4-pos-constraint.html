Stage 4-13-4 · Positive-Sample Constraint 關卡 4-13-4 · 正樣本約束 SASP §4.13.4 ↗

Negative samples add oscillation to the shape. Forcing $w[n]\ge 0$ guarantees a non-oscillating taper — often desirable for pedagogical reasons or when subsequent processing assumes a positive envelope.

負樣本會讓形狀振盪。強制 $w[n]\ge 0$ 保證是不振盪的收尾 — 常在教學或要求正包絡的後處理中需要。

Cost: larger main-lobe or higher side-lobe floor. Only add this constraint if you truly need non-negativity.

代價：主瓣變寬或旁瓣抬高。真的需要非負時才加。
