Stage 4-13-1 · Linear Programming 關卡 4-13-1 · 線性規劃 SASP §4.13.1 ↗

Minimise a linear cost subject to linear inequalities. Matlab's linprog, scipy's linprog, GLPK, CVX, CPLEX — every numerical environment has one.

在線性不等式約束下最小化一個線性成本。Matlab 有 linprog、scipy 有 linprog、GLPK、CVX、CPLEX — 每個數值環境都提供。

LP STANDARD FORM
$$\min_{x}\ c^{T}x \quad\text{s.t.}\quad Ax\le b,\;\; x\ge 0$$

The trick for window design is to encode "main-lobe peak = 1, side lobes ≤ δ, δ minimal" as linear statements. When δ becomes another variable, minimising δ is the LP.

視窗設計的訣竅是把「主瓣峰 = 1、旁瓣 ≤ δ、δ 最小」寫成線性式。把 δ 也視為變數，極小化它就是一個 LP。
