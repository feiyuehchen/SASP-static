Stage 4-2-1 · Hann Window 關卡 4-2-1 · Hann 視窗 SASP §4.2.1 ↗

The default audio window for a reason: tapers smoothly to zero at both ends, kills the rectangular's first side lobe, and leaves you with a clean $-31$ dB floor.

音訊預設視窗的原因：兩端平滑收斂到 0、抵消矩形的第一旁瓣，留下乾淨的 $-31$ dB 旁瓣地板。

HANN
$$w[n]=\frac{1}{2}\Bigl[1-\cos\!\left(\frac{2\pi n}{N-1}\right)\Bigr]$$
  • $\alpha=0.5$ in the generalized Hamming family.
  • 廣義 Hamming 家族裡的 $\alpha=0.5$。
  • Main-lobe width $8\pi/N$ (twice the rectangular).
  • 主瓣寬 $8\pi/N$（矩形的 2 倍）。
  • First side lobe $\approx -31$ dB.
  • 第一旁瓣約 $-31$ dB。
  • Side-lobe roll-off $18$ dB/octave (much faster than rectangular).
  • 旁瓣滾降 $18$ dB/八度（比矩形快很多）。
  • A Hann window squared is also a Hann window (squared) — useful for COLA reconstruction.
  • Hann 平方仍是 Hann 類型 — COLA 重建常用。
