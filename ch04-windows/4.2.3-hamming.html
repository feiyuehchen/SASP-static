Stage 4-2-3 · Hamming Window 關卡 4-2-3 · Hamming 視窗 SASP §4.2.3 ↗

Same shape as Hann but with α tuned to flatten the first side lobe all the way to $-42$ dB. Cost: farther side lobes drop more slowly.

形狀同 Hann，但把 α 調成讓第一旁瓣壓到 $-42$ dB。代價是遠處旁瓣滾降較慢。

HAMMING
$$w[n]=0.54-0.46\cos\!\left(\frac{2\pi n}{N-1}\right)$$
  • First side lobe $\approx -42$ dB (almost 12 dB better than Hann!)
  • 第一旁瓣約 $-42$ dB（比 Hann 還低近 12 dB！）
  • But side-lobe roll-off is only $6$ dB/octave — far side lobes eventually exceed Hann's.
  • 但旁瓣滾降僅 $6$ dB/八度 — 遠離主瓣後反而比 Hann 高。
  • Ends don't reach zero; the window "sits on a pedestal" of about $0.08$.
  • 兩端不會降到 0；視窗坐在約 $0.08$ 的底座上。
