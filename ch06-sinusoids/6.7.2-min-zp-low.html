Stage 6-7-2 · Min Zero-Padding for Low-Freq Peaks關卡 6-7-2 · 低頻峰最小補零SASP §6.7.2 ↗

Low-frequency peaks are distorted by the mirror copy at the negative frequency. Push ZP factor higher — typically $L\ge 8$ — to push the mirror far enough not to bias the peak.

低頻峰會被負頻率鏡像干擾，補零倍率要更大（通常 $L\ge 8$），把鏡像推得夠遠以免偏差。
