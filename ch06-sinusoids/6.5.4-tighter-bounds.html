If you know phases match (worst-case constructive), you can resolve at about $0.6$× the null-to-null width with Hann. With random relative phase the required separation averages out to a fraction of a main-lobe width.

若知道相位同相（最糟建設性），Hann 能在零點到零點寬的約 $0.6$ 倍分辨。隨機相位下平均只需主瓣寬的一部分。

Practical advice: use the null-to-null width as a conservative guide and only push lower when you have extra phase information from the application.

實務建議：把零點到零點寬當保守基準；只有當應用提供額外相位資訊時才再壓低。
