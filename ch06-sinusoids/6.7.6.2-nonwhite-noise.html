Stage 6-7-6-2 · Non-White Noise關卡 6-7-6-2 · 非白雜訊SASP §6.7.6.2 ↗

Coloured noise with known PSD $P_n(\omega)$: apply the whitening filter $1/\sqrt{P_n(\omega)}$ before MLE, turning the problem back into AWGN.

已知有色雜訊功率譜 $P_n(\omega)$：先通過 whitening 濾波器 $1/\sqrt{P_n(\omega)}$，再做 MLE，問題回到 AWGN。

Equivalent to weighting the frequency-domain error by $1/P_n(\omega)$ — frequencies with lots of noise contribute less to the fit.

等同於在頻域以 $1/P_n(\omega)$ 加權誤差 — 雜訊大的頻率對擬合貢獻小。
