Stage 6-7-1 · Min Zero-Padding for High-Freq Peaks關卡 6-7-1 · 高頻峰最小補零SASP §6.7.1 ↗

For peaks near $\omega=\pi$, a zero-padding factor of about $L\ge 5$ is enough for parabolic interpolation to reach within 0.1% of a bin. More is wasted effort.

接近 $\omega=\pi$ 的峰，補零倍率 $L\ge 5$ 就足以讓拋物線內插精度達 bin 的 0.1% 以內；再多只是浪費。
