$$\hat{a}=\tfrac{2}{N}\sum x[n]\cos(\omega_0 n),\;\hat{b}=\tfrac{2}{N}\sum x[n]\sin(\omega_0 n),\;\hat A=\sqrt{\hat a^2+\hat b^2},\;\hat\phi=\mathrm{atan2}(-\hat b,\hat a)$$

Fit both a cosine and a sine at $\omega_0$, then combine. This is the complex DFT bin at $\omega_0$ in disguise.

在 $\omega_0$ 上同時擬合餘弦與正弦，再合成。等同於 $\omega_0$ 處的複 DFT bin。
