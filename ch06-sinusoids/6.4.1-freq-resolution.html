Stage 6-4-1 · Frequency Resolution關卡 6-4-1 · 頻率解析度SASP §6.4.1 ↗

Two sinusoids can be "resolved" if their main lobes do not overlap — i.e. $|\omega_1-\omega_2|>\text{main-lobe width}$. That width is approximately $2\pi K/N$ where $K$ depends on the window.

兩個正弦波能「分辨開」的條件：主瓣不重疊，也就是 $|\omega_1-\omega_2|>$ 主瓣寬。主瓣寬約 $2\pi K/N$，$K$ 看視窗。

$$\begin{array}{lc}\text{Window} & K \\\text{rect} & 2 \\\text{Hann} & 4 \\\text{Hamming} & 4 \\\text{Blackman} & 6 \\\text{Kaiser }\beta=6 & \approx 5 \end{array}$$
