The recipe — peak-pick with a windowed DFT, refine with least squares under Gaussian noise — is the maximum-likelihood answer for a huge family of sinusoid estimation problems. Add transients, harmonics, or overlapping partials and the same machinery extends.

「視窗 DFT 找峰 → 高斯雜訊下最小平方細調」在非常大一類正弦估計問題中都是 MLE 解。加入瞬態、諧波或重疊 partial 後，同一套工具仍可延伸。
