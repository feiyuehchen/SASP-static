Stage 6-2 · Sampled Complex Sinusoid關卡 6-2 · 取樣複正弦SASP §6.2 ↗

$$x[n]=A\,e^{j(\omega_0 n+\phi)}\;\longleftrightarrow\;2\pi A\,e^{j\phi}\sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty}\delta(\omega-\omega_0-2\pi k)$$

Sampling turns the pair of deltas at $\pm\omega_0$ into a pair of delta trains — an image of the ideal spectrum repeats every $2\pi$ in the normalized frequency axis. For a real cosine, the DTFT is the two delta trains summed.

取樣把兩個 $\pm\omega_0$ 的 delta 變成兩個 delta 列 — 理想頻譜在 $2\pi$ 週期複製一份。對實餘弦，DTFT 是兩個 delta 列相加。
