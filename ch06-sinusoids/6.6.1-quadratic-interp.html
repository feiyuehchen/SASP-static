Stage 6-6-1 · Quadratic Peak Interpolation關卡 6-6-1 · 拋物線峰內插SASP §6.6.1 ↗

Let $\alpha,\beta,\gamma$ be the log-magnitudes at the peak bin $k^*$ and its two neighbours. Fit a parabola through them; the vertex offset gives a sub-bin peak location.

設 $\alpha,\beta,\gamma$ 為峰 bin $k^*$ 與兩鄰居的對數振幅。在這三點擬合拋物線，頂點偏移給出 sub-bin 的峰位置。

$$p\;=\;\tfrac{1}{2}\,\frac{\alpha-\gamma}{\alpha-2\beta+\gamma},\quad k_{\text{peak}}\approx k^*+p$$

Best used on log magnitudes (dB) with a smooth window like Gaussian or Hann — the parabola model matches the log-magnitude shape near the peak.

對對數振幅（dB）做效果最好；配 Gaussian 或 Hann 等平滑視窗時，拋物線剛好近似峰附近的對數形狀。
