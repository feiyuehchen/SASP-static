$$\hat A=\frac{2}{N}\sum_{n=0}^{N-1}x[n]\cos(\omega_0 n+\phi_0)$$

Known frequency and phase → project the signal onto the matching cosine and scale by $2/N$. This is the DFT bin at $\omega_0$ (properly scaled).

已知頻率與相位 → 把訊號投影到匹配餘弦、再乘 $2/N$。其實就是 $\omega_0$ 處的 DFT bin（加對比例）。
