function [p, y] = qint(ym1, y0, yp1)
  p = (ym1 - yp1) / (2*(ym1 - 2*y0 + yp1));
  y = y0 - 0.25 * (ym1 - yp1) * p;
end

Returns offset $p\in[-0.5,0.5]$ and the interpolated peak value. Feed it log-magnitudes for best accuracy.

回傳偏移 $p\in[-0.5,0.5]$ 與內插後的峰值。傳對數振幅效果最佳。
