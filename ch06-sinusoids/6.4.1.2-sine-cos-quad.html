Stage 6-4-1-2 · Sine and Cosine (Phase Quadrature)關卡 6-4-1-2 · 正弦與餘弦（正交相位）SASP §6.4.1.2 ↗

Two tones 90° apart in phase add incoherently: their main lobes overlap but partly cancel, producing a wider, lower plateau. Visually the situation is even less resolvable than the in-phase case.

相位差 90° 的兩個音會非相干相加：主瓣重疊但部分抵消，形成更寬但更矮的平台。視覺上比同相還難分辨。
