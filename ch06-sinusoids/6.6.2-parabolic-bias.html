Stage 6-6-2 · Bias of Parabolic Interpolation關卡 6-6-2 · 拋物線內插的偏差SASP §6.6.2 ↗

The log magnitude of a windowed sinusoid's main lobe is not exactly a parabola. So QINT has a small systematic offset. For Gaussian windows the parabola fits perfectly and bias vanishes — a reason Gaussian is popular for peak-picking.

加窗正弦主瓣的對數振幅並非真正拋物線，所以 QINT 有小系統性偏差。Gaussian 視窗下擬合完美、無偏差 — Gaussian 常被用於 peak-picking 的原因。

Quantify: bias is typically <1% of a bin for Hann at moderate zero-padding. For most music/pitch work that is below the ear's JND.

量化：Hann 加中度補零時偏差約 <1% bin；音樂/基頻應用下通常在聽感 JND 之下。
