Stage 6-3 · Spectrum of a Windowed Sinusoid關卡 6-3 · 加窗正弦的頻譜SASP §6.3 ↗

Multiply a sinusoid by a window in time = convolve window's spectrum with the sinusoid's delta train in frequency. Result: the window shape centred on each sinusoid frequency.

時域乘視窗 = 頻域卷積視窗頻譜與正弦的 delta 列。結果：視窗形狀在每個正弦頻率中心上。

$$x_w[n]=w[n]\cos(\omega_0 n)\;\Rightarrow\;X_w(\omega)=\tfrac12 W(\omega-\omega_0)+\tfrac12 W(\omega+\omega_0)$$

For a real cosine, two mirror copies of $W$ appear at $\pm\omega_0$. If the window's main lobe width is much smaller than $2\omega_0$ the two copies do not interact; if not, they overlap and interfere.

實餘弦下 $\pm\omega_0$ 各有一份 $W$。視窗主瓣寬遠小於 $2\omega_0$ 時兩份不互相干擾；否則會重疊干涉。
