Stage 6-1 · Spectrum of a Sinusoid關卡 6-1 · 正弦的頻譜SASP §6.1 ↗

A real cosine is two complex exponentials, so its continuous spectrum is a pair of impulses.

實餘弦 = 兩個複指數之和，因此連續頻譜是一對脈衝。

$$A\cos(\omega_0 t+\phi)\;\leftrightarrow\;\tfrac{A}{2}\,e^{j\phi}\,\delta(\Omega-\omega_0)+\tfrac{A}{2}\,e^{-j\phi}\,\delta(\Omega+\omega_0)$$

That is the ideal picture. Real measurements smear those impulses by the analysis window's spectrum — which is what the rest of this chapter is about.

這是理想版本。實際量測把這兩個脈衝依分析視窗的頻譜塗抹開來 — 本章接下來就在講這件事。
