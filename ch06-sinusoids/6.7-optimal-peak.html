Given noise statistics, what accuracy can peak-finding achieve? The Cramér-Rao bound says $\sigma_f \gtrsim \sqrt{6/(SNR\cdot N^3)}$ — accuracy grows with signal length like $N^{-3/2}$.

給定雜訊統計，找峰精度能達多少？Cramér-Rao 界告訴我們 $\sigma_f\gtrsim\sqrt{6/(SNR\cdot N^3)}$ — 精度隨樣本長度以 $N^{-3/2}$ 成長。
