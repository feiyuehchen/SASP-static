Once you have found the bin of maximum magnitude, a simple interpolation of that bin and its two neighbours can locate the peak to a small fraction of a bin — enough for musical pitch accuracy.

找到振幅最大 bin 後，拿它與兩側鄰居做簡單內插，就能把峰的位置鎖到遠小於 bin 的精度 — 做音樂基頻綽綽有餘。
