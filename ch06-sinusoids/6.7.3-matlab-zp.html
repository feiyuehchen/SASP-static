function L = zpfmin(omega0, tol)
  % Minimum zero-padding factor to find peak at omega0 to tol bins
  L = ceil(1 / (tol * sqrt(1 - (omega0/pi)^2)));
end

Returns the smallest integer $L$ so that parabolic peak-finding at $\omega_0$ is accurate to a fraction tol of a bin.

回傳最小整數 $L$，使 $\omega_0$ 處拋物線找峰的精度達 bin 的 tol 倍之內。
