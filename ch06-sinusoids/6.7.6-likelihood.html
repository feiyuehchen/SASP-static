Stage 6-7-6 · Likelihood Function關卡 6-7-6 · 似然函數SASP §6.7.6 ↗

$$L(\theta)\;=\;\prod_{n=0}^{N-1}\tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\sigma^{2}}}\exp\!\left(-\tfrac{(x[n]-s_\theta[n])^{2}}{2\sigma^{2}}\right)$$

Log-likelihood is proportional to $-\|x-s_\theta\|^2$ — so MLE ≡ minimum-residual fit. If noise is non-white, replace the sum with a weighted one.

對數似然與 $-\|x-s_\theta\|^2$ 成比例 — MLE = 最小殘差擬合。若雜訊非白，求和換成加權求和。
