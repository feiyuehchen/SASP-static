Stage 6-4 · Effect of Windowing關卡 6-4 · 加窗的影響SASP §6.4 ↗

Windowing exchanges the ideal two-delta spectrum for two smeared bumps. Two costs follow: frequency resolution limits (how close can two tones be before their bumps merge?) and dynamic range limits (side lobes bury weaker tones).

加窗把理想的兩個 delta 變成兩團拖尾。兩個代價：頻率解析度（兩個音多近才會融合？）與動態範圍（旁瓣會蓋掉弱音）。
