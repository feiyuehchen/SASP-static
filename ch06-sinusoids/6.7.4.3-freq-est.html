Stage 6-7-4-3 · Frequency Estimation關卡 6-7-4-3 · 頻率估計SASP §6.7.4.3 ↗

Frequency is non-linear in the amplitude-phase equations — closed-form solution is not possible. Standard recipe: locate the peak bin, interpolate with QINT (§6.6.1), refine with Newton or gradient descent on the full MSE cost.

頻率與振幅相位方程式是非線性的 — 沒有封閉解。標準做法：先找峰 bin，用 QINT（§6.6.1）內插，再對整體 MSE 用 Newton 或梯度法細調。
