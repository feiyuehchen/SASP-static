Set the analysis window length to an integer number of periods. The sinusoid's two deltas land exactly on DFT bins — no leakage, regardless of window choice.

若視窗長度剛好是週期的整數倍，正弦的兩個 delta 會剛好落在 DFT bin 上 — 不管什麼視窗都沒有洩漏。

This is the only scenario where a rectangular window wins: perfect tone separation at minimum length. Outside music analysis, where signals are rarely harmonically perfect, you cannot rely on it.

這是矩形視窗能贏的唯一情境：最短長度下完美分離。但音樂外，訊號很少剛好完美諧波，不能依賴這招。
