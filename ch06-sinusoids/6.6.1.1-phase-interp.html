Stage 6-6-1-1 · Phase at the Interpolated Peak關卡 6-6-1-1 · 內插峰處的相位SASP §6.6.1.1 ↗

The phase at the sub-bin peak location is found by linear interpolation of unwrapped phases from the same three bins.

sub-bin 峰位置處的相位：對同三個 bin 的展開相位做線性內插即可。

Phase is linear in frequency near a single-tone peak (the Shift Theorem), so a linear fit is sufficient. Remember to unwrap first: $-\pi$ and $+\pi$ should become adjacent, not opposite.

單音峰附近相位對頻率近似線性（位移定理），線性內插夠用。先做展開：$-\pi$ 與 $+\pi$ 要變相鄰，而不是相反。
