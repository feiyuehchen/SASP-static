Stage 11-4-2-3 · Tracking Peaks Across FFTs關卡 11-4-2-3 · 跨 FFT 追蹤波峰SASP §11.4.2.3 ↗

Assignment problem: match frame-$m$ peaks to frame-$(m+1)$ peaks minimising total (frequency + amplitude) distance. Hungarian algorithm solves it in $O(n^3)$; greedy nearest-neighbour usually suffices.

指派問題：把 frame $m$ 的峰對到 frame $m+1$，最小化總（頻率 + 振幅）距離。Hungarian 演算法 $O(n^3)$ 解；貪婪最近鄰通常夠用。
