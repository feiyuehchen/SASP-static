Stage 11-7-5 · Improving the Channel Filters關卡 11-7-5 · 改善通道濾波器SASP §11.7.5 ↗

Rectangular weighting gives Dirichlet side-lobes. Use cosine / Hann / Kaiser weights to smooth the band edges and suppress leakage between bands.

矩形加權給出 Dirichlet 旁瓣。改用餘弦 / Hann / Kaiser 加權可平滑頻帶邊緣、降低頻帶間洩漏。
