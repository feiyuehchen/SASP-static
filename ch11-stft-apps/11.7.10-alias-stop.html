Stage 11-7-10 · Restricting Aliasing to Stop-Bands關卡 11-7-10 · 將混疊限於停帶SASP §11.7.10 ↗

Design the band filters and the synthesis weights together so aliased copies from one band appear only inside the stop-bands of its neighbours — where they are killed when bands are summed. This is the key to perfect-reconstruction filter banks.

一起設計頻帶濾波器與合成權重，讓某頻帶的混疊副本只出現在相鄰頻帶的停帶 — 合成時剛好互相抵消。這是完美重建濾波器組的關鍵。
