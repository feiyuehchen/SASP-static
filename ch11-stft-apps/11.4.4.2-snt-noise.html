Stage 11-4-4-2 · S+N+T Noise Model關卡 11-4-4-2 · S+N+T 雜訊模型SASP §11.4.4.2 ↗

After removing S (sinusoidal tracks) and T (transient spikes), the leftover N is the "rough" part of the sound — stochastic, broadband, reasonably stationary on short windows. Modelled as filtered white noise with a frame-rate envelope.

扣除 S（正弦軌跡）與 T（瞬態峰）後，剩下 N 是聲音中的「粗糙」成份 — 隨機、寬頻、短窗內近穩態。以 frame-rate 包絡濾波白雜訊建模。
