SOLA-FS (Synchronous Overlap-Add, Fixed Synthesis hop) finds the cross-correlation peak between analysis and synthesis windows to snap waveforms together. Very low CPU; great on speech, less so on polyphonic music.

SOLA-FS（同步重疊相加、固定合成跳距）以分析/合成視窗間的互相關峰對齊波形。CPU 極低；對語音很好，複音音樂較弱。
