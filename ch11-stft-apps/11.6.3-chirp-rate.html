Stage 11-6-3 · Identifying the Chirp Rate關卡 11-6-3 · 辨識 chirp 率SASP §11.6.3 ↗

Correlate the signal with chirplets at a grid of $(\omega_0,\beta)$ values and pick the maximum-response atom. That peak tells you both the frequency and the frequency-rate of the event.

以 $(\omega_0,\beta)$ 格點上的 chirplet 做相關，取最大響應原子。該峰同時告訴你事件的頻率與頻率變化率。
