Stage 11-7-12 · Optimal Band Filters關卡 11-7-12 · 最優頻帶濾波器SASP §11.7.12 ↗

For each band, solve the minimax (Parks-McClellan / LP) problem with target = 1 in the passband, 0 outside. Resulting filters sum to the flattest possible total response subject to the band specs.

對每頻帶解 minimax（Parks-McClellan / LP）：通帶目標 1、其外 0。所得濾波器總和在頻帶規格下給出可能最平的總響應。
