Solve $R\boldsymbol{a}=-\boldsymbol{r}$ where $R$ is the autocorrelation matrix and $\boldsymbol{r}$ its first column. Levinson-Durbin solves this in $O(p^2)$ using the matrix's Toeplitz structure.

解 $R\boldsymbol{a}=-\boldsymbol{r}$，$R$ 為自相關矩陣、$\boldsymbol{r}$ 是其首列。Levinson-Durbin 用 Toeplitz 結構以 $O(p^2)$ 求解。
