Drum hits, key clicks, bow scrapes — these "transients" are neither tonal nor stochastic in the usual sense. A dedicated transient layer uses short-window STFTs (multi-resolution) to preserve their attack.

鼓擊、按鍵咔聲、運弓刮擦 — 這些「瞬態」既非音色又非通常意義雜訊。專門的 T 層用短視窗 STFT（多解析度）保留攻擊段。
