Stage 11-1 · F0 Estimation from Spectral Peaks關卡 11-1 · 由頻譜波峰估計基頻SASP §11.1 ↗

A periodic tone at $f_0$ shows up in the spectrum as peaks at $f_0,2f_0,3f_0,\ldots$. Finding $f_0$ reduces to: locate peaks, fit an integer-multiple comb, and report the smallest common divisor.

基頻 $f_0$ 的週期音在頻譜中以 $f_0,2f_0,3f_0,\ldots$ 的波峰呈現。估基頻就是：找峰、擬合整數倍梳、回報最小公因數。
