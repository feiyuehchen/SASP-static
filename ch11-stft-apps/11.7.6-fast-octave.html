Stage 11-7-6 · Fast Octave Filter Banks關卡 11-7-6 · 快速八度濾波器組SASP §11.7.6 ↗

Octave $k$ uses twice as many FFT bins as octave $k-1$. A dyadic tree of FFTs with doubled lengths gives a computationally efficient octave filter bank whose per-octave resolution stays roughly constant in Q.

第 $k$ 個八度使用的 FFT bin 數是第 $k-1$ 的兩倍。以 FFT 長度倍增的二進樹可得到運算效率高、每八度近似等 Q 解析度的濾波器組。
