Stage 11-4-4-1 · S+N+T Time-Frequency Maps關卡 11-4-4-1 · S+N+T 時頻圖SASP §11.4.4.1 ↗

Use a long window for S (frequency resolution) and a short window for T (time resolution). Each frame has both representations; a classifier decides which energy belongs where.

S 用長視窗（頻率解析度），T 用短視窗（時間解析度）。每 frame 兩種表示都有；用分類器判定能量歸屬。
