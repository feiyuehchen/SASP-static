Stage 11-7-9 · Aliasing on Downsampling關卡 11-7-9 · 降取樣的混疊SASP §11.7.9 ↗

Decimate each band to its band-specific sample rate to save computation downstream. Aliasing appears from out-of-band energy; restrict it to stopbands so neighbour bands cancel it.

把每頻帶降到其自有取樣率以節省下游算力。帶外能量會產生混疊；限制其進入停帶，讓鄰近頻帶抵消之。
