Stage 11-8 · FFT Filter-Bank Summary & OLA Duality關卡 11-8 · FFT 濾波器組小結與 OLA 對偶SASP §11.8 ↗

FFT filter banks and OLA STFT processing are two sides of the same coin: one slices by frequency, the other by time. Any band-specific modification in the FB view is equivalent to a per-frame spectral modification in the OLA view.

FFT 濾波器組與 OLA STFT 處理是一體兩面：一者按頻率切、一者按時間切。FB 觀點下各頻帶的修改，等同於 OLA 觀點下每 frame 的頻譜修改。
