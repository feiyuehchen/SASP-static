Rule of thumb for speech: $p=2+f_s/1000$ (e.g. $p=12$ at 10 kHz, $p=18$ at 16 kHz). Too low misses formants; too high starts fitting pitch harmonics.

語音口訣：$p=2+f_s/1000$（10 kHz 用 $p=12$、16 kHz 用 $p=18$）。太低丟 formant；太高開始擬合基頻諧波。
