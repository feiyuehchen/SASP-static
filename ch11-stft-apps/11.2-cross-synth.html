Stage 11-2 · Cross-Synthesis關卡 11-2 · 交叉合成SASP §11.2 ↗

Take the spectral envelope of sound A and multiply it by the fine excitation spectrum of sound B, frame by frame. Classic "talking instruments" and channel vocoder are both this recipe.

把 A 的頻譜包絡乘上 B 的精細激發頻譜，逐 frame 合成。經典「會說話的樂器」與通道 vocoder 皆是此法。
