Take an STFT; group bins into bands using a weighting profile per band; each band's time-domain output is the inverse of its grouped bins. Great for wide-band processing at STFT resolution.

取 STFT；以每頻帶的加權配置把 bin 合併；每頻帶的時域輸出即其合併 bin 的反轉換。適合在 STFT 解析度下做寬頻處理。
