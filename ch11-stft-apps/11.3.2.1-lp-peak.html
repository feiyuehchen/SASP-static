Stage 11-3-2-1 · LP Is Peak Sensitive關卡 11-3-2-1 · LP 對峰敏感SASP §11.3.2.1 ↗

LP minimises the energy of the prediction error. Because peaks dominate energy, LP fits peaks (formants) accurately at the expense of valleys — exactly what you want for vocal-tract modelling.

LP 最小化預測誤差的能量。能量由波峰主導，故 LP 把波峰（formant）擬合得準，谷處較粗 — 正是聲道建模要的。
