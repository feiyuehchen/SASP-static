Stage 11-6-3-1 · Chirplet Frequency-Rate Estimation關卡 11-6-3-1 · Chirplet 頻率率估計SASP §11.6.3.1 ↗

Under Gaussian noise, the max-likelihood chirp-rate estimator is the chirplet with highest inner-product magnitude. In practice, coarse search then refine with quadratic interpolation.

高斯雜訊下，最大似然 chirp 率估計器即為內積振幅最大的 chirplet。實務上先粗搜再以拋物線內插細修。
