Stage 11-4 · Spectral Modeling Synthesis關卡 11-4 · 頻譜建模合成SASP §11.4 ↗

Model a sound as tracked sinusoidal partials + a residual noise + sharp transients. Each layer is analysable, editable, resynthesisable. The most powerful and flexible spectral sound model.

把聲音建模為：被追蹤的正弦 partials + 殘餘雜訊 + 瞬態。每層皆可分析、編輯、再合成。是頻譜音色建模中最強最靈活的模型。
