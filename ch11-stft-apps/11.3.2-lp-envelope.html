Stage 11-3-2 · Linear Prediction Envelope關卡 11-3-2 · 線性預測包絡SASP §11.3.2 ↗

Model each sample as a linear combination of the previous $p$ samples plus residual noise. The resulting all-pole filter $1/A(z)$ has a frequency response whose magnitude is the spectral envelope.

把每個樣本模擬為前 $p$ 個樣本的線性組合加上殘餘噪聲。所得全極點濾波器 $1/A(z)$ 的頻率響應振幅即為頻譜包絡。
