Stage 11-1-2 · Getting Closer to Maximum Likelihood關卡 11-1-2 · 更靠近最大似然SASP §11.1.2 ↗

A maximum-likelihood F0 estimator weights each peak by its amplitude and penalises mismatch. Simpler peak-picking approximates this — the gap is usually small for steady tones and large for noisy ones.

ML 基頻估計器依峰值振幅加權並懲罰不匹配。簡單找峰是其近似；穩定音差不多，雜訊大時差距明顯。
