Build a test signal from a known all-pole filter (three formant pairs) driven by a 100 Hz impulse train. Both envelope methods should recover the three resonances; how closely they agree with the ground truth is the benchmark.

用已知全極點濾波器（三個 formant 對）被 100 Hz 脈衝列驅動，合成測試訊號。兩種包絡方法都應還原三共振峰；與真值吻合度就是基準。
