Stage 11-5 · Time-Scale Modification關卡 11-5 · 時間尺度變化SASP §11.5 ↗

Stretch or compress the duration of a sound without changing its frequency content. Done by re-timing STFT frames rather than resampling the waveform.

在不改變頻率內容的前提下伸長或壓縮聲音。做法是重新安排 STFT frame 的時序，而非重新取樣波形。
