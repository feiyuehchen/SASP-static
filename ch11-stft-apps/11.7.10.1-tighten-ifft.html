Stage 11-7-10-1 · Tightening the IFFTs關卡 11-7-10-1 · 緊化 IFFTSASP §11.7.10.1 ↗

Each band only needs an IFFT as long as its own bandwidth; shorter IFFTs both save CPU and sharpen the band's time-domain impulse response.

每頻帶只需要與自身頻寬等長的 IFFT；較短的 IFFT 同時省 CPU 並使頻帶時域脈衝響應更銳利。
