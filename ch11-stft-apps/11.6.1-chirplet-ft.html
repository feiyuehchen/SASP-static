Stage 11-6-1 · Chirplet Fourier Transform關卡 11-6-1 · Chirplet 的傅立葉轉換SASP §11.6.1 ↗

The FT of a Gaussian-windowed chirp is another Gaussian, rotated in the time-frequency plane by an angle set by $\beta$. That rotation is what lets chirplets tile oblique regions of time-frequency that straight STFT bins cannot.

Gaussian 加窗 chirp 的 FT 仍是高斯，但在時頻平面上依 $\beta$ 旋轉。這個旋轉使 chirplet 能覆蓋 STFT 平直 bin 覆蓋不到的斜向區。
