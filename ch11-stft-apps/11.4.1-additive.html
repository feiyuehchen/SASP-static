Stage 11-4-1 · Additive Synthesis (Early Sinusoidal Modeling)關卡 11-4-1 · 加法合成（早期正弦建模）SASP §11.4.1 ↗

Represent the sound as a sum of sinusoids: $x[n]=\sum_k A_k[n]\cos(\phi_k[n])$. Time-varying amplitudes and phases let one partial each follow one harmonic across time.

把聲音寫成正弦和：$x[n]=\sum_k A_k[n]\cos(\phi_k[n])$。時變振幅與相位讓每個 partial 隨時間追一條諧波。
